Gleason's Gym

Located in Brooklyn, New York, Gleason's Gym is perhaps the most famous boxing gym in the world. Founded in 1937, the gym has been home to some of the greatest boxers of all time, including Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson, and Sugar Ray Robinson. Today, Gleason's Gym is still going strong, and it offers a range of classes and training programs for boxers of all skill levels.

Wild Card Boxing Club

Located in Hollywood, California, Wild Card Boxing Club is another famous gym that has produced some of the greatest boxers of all time, including Manny Pacquiao and Oscar De La Hoya.
