Reliance Industries Limited

Reliance Industries Limited is one of the biggest fabric manufacturers in India. It was founded in 1960 and has since grown to become one of the largest conglomerates in the country. Reliance Industries Limited caters to both domestic and international markets, and its fabrics are exported to over 100 countries. The company has a strong focus on sustainability and has implemented various initiatives to reduce its environmental impact. Welspun India Limited is another leading fabric manufacturer in India. The company was founded in 1985 and has since grown to become a major player in the global textile industry. Welspun India Limited produces a wide range of fabrics, including bed linen, bath linen, rugs, and carpets.

Aditya Birla Group

The Aditya Birla Group is a multinational conglomerate that operates in various industries, including textiles. Arvind Limited

Arvind Limited is one of the oldest textile companies in India. The company was founded in 1931 and has since grown to become a major player in the global textile industry. Arvind Limited produces a wide range of fabrics, including denim, shirting, and knits.
