Mens Wedding Suits Ideas India A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Weddings are always a big deal in India, and finding the perfect suit for the groom is no exception. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one that fits both the occasion and the groom's style. In this blog post, we'll provide you with some mens wedding suits ideas India to help you make the right choice and look your best on your big day.

Every groom wants to look his best on his wedding day, but it can be challenging to find the right suit that fits well and looks stylish. With so many options available, it can be a confusing and frustrating experience. However, with the right guidance, you can find the perfect suit that complements your personality and style.

When it comes to mens wedding suits ideas India, the target is to find the right balance between traditional and modern styles. Indian weddings are known for their grandeur, and a wedding suit should reflect that. The suit should be comfortable, stylish, and classy. It should also complement the bride's outfit and the overall theme of the wedding.

In this post, we'll discuss some of the best mens wedding suits ideas India that you can choose from. We'll cover everything from the fabric, color, and style to the accessories and footwear.

Mens Wedding Suits Ideas India: A Personal Experience

Before we dive into the details of mens wedding suits ideas India, let's talk about my personal experience. When I was getting married, I wanted to wear a suit that was both traditional and modern. I didn't want to go for the typical black or navy blue suit that most grooms wear. Instead, I opted for a light grey suit that was made from linen fabric. The suit was comfortable and breathable, which was perfect for the summer wedding. I paired the suit with a white shirt and a maroon tie that complemented my bride's outfit. For footwear, I went with a pair of brown leather shoes that completed the look.

Types of Wedding Suits for Men in India

When it comes to mens wedding suits ideas India, there are many different types of suits that you can choose from. Here are some of the most popular ones:

Sherwani Suit

The sherwani suit is a traditional Indian suit that is commonly worn by grooms on their wedding day. It is a long coat-like garment that is usually made from silk, brocade, or velvet. The sherwani suit is typically paired with a churidar or dhoti, which is a traditional Indian pant. This suit is perfect for grooms who want to embrace their culture and tradition.

Jodhpuri Suit

The Jodhpuri suit is a modern take on the traditional sherwani suit. It is a two-piece suit that consists of a coat and trousers. The coat is usually made from silk or velvet and has a Nehru collar. The Jodhpuri suit is perfect for grooms who want to keep it traditional but also want to add a modern twist.

Tuxedo Suit

The tuxedo suit is a classic suit that is perfect for grooms who want to look sophisticated and stylish. It is a two or three-piece suit that is usually black or navy blue. The tuxedo suit is typically paired with a white shirt, black bowtie, and black leather shoes.

How to Choose the Right Suit for Your Body Type

Choosing the right suit for your body type is crucial to ensure that you look your best on your wedding day. Here are some tips to help you choose the right suit:

1. If you have a slim body type, opt for a slim-fit suit that hugs your body and accentuates your physique.

2. If you have a muscular body type, opt for a regular-fit suit that has a broader shoulder and chest area.

3. If you have a heavy body type, opt for a loose-fit suit that doesn't cling to your body and provides you with ample room to move around.

Accessories and Footwear for Mens Wedding Suits

Accessories and footwear are essential elements of any wedding suit. Here are some accessories and footwear ideas to complete your look:

1. Turban or Pagdi: For traditional Indian weddings, grooms can wear a turban or a pagdi to complete their look.

2. Pocket Square: A pocket square adds a touch of sophistication to your suit and can elevate your overall look.

3. Watch: A watch is a classic accessory that every groom should wear on their wedding day. It adds an element of style and class to your look.

4. Shoes: Shoes are an essential part of any wedding suit. For Indian weddings, grooms can opt for jutti or mojari, which are traditional Indian shoes. Alternatively, they can also wear brown or black leather shoes.

Question and Answer

Q. What color suit should a groom wear for an Indian wedding?

A. Grooms can wear a variety of colors for their wedding suit, including beige, grey, navy blue, and black.

Q. Can grooms wear a western-style suit for an Indian wedding?

A. Yes, grooms can wear a western-style suit for an Indian wedding. However, they should try to incorporate elements of Indian tradition, such as a turban or a pagdi.

Q. What type of fabric is suitable for an Indian wedding suit?

A. Silk, brocade, velvet, and linen are all suitable fabrics for an Indian wedding suit.

Q. What accessories should a groom wear with his wedding suit?

A. Grooms can wear a turban or a pagdi, a pocket square, a watch, and traditional Indian shoes like jutti or mojari.

Conclusion of Mens Wedding Suits Ideas India

Choosing the right wedding suit is crucial for every groom. By following these mens wedding suits ideas India and tips, you can find the perfect suit that complements your style and personality. Whether you opt for a traditional sherwani suit or a modern tuxedo suit, make sure to add your personal touch to it to make it your own. Don't forget to accessorize and choose the right footwear to complete your look. We hope this guide has helped you find the perfect wedding suit for your big day!
