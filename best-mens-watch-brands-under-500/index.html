Best Men s Watch Brands Under 500

Written by Bardi Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Watches are a fashion statement and an essential accessory for men. A good watch not only tells you the time but also adds to your personality. However, finding a good watch can be a daunting task, especially when you are on a budget. In this post, we will discuss the best men's watch brands under $500.

There are several pain points when it comes to buying a watch under $500. Some of the common problems include finding a watch that is durable, stylish, and has good features. Moreover, most watches in this price range are not Swiss-made, which can be a deal-breaker for some people.

The target of this post is to help you find a good quality watch that fits your budget. We have researched and tested several watches and have come up with the best men's watch brands under $500. These watches not only look good but also come with some great features.

To summarize, we will be discussing the best men's watch brands under $500 and their features. We will also cover some of the common pain points and how to overcome them. Let's get started!

Bulova

Bulova is a well-known American watch brand that has been around since 1875. They are known for their high-quality watches that are both stylish and affordable. One of their best watches under $500 is the Bulova Men's Precisionist Watch. This watch features a quartz movement that is accurate to within 10 seconds per year. It also comes with a stainless steel case and bracelet, a mineral crystal, and a date function. The Precisionist Watch is perfect for those who want a watch that is both accurate and stylish.

Seiko

Seiko is a Japanese watch brand that has been around since 1881. They are known for their high-quality watches that are both durable and stylish. One of their best watches under $500 is the Seiko Men's Prospex Automatic Watch. This watch features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a black dial, and a date function. It is also water-resistant up to 200 meters and comes with a 41-hour power reserve. The Prospex Automatic Watch is perfect for those who want a watch that is both stylish and durable.

Timex

Timex is an American watch brand that has been around since 1854. They are known for their affordable watches that are both stylish and functional. One of their best watches under $500 is the Timex Men's Marlin Automatic Watch. This watch features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a black dial, and a date function. It is also water-resistant up to 30 meters and comes with a 40-hour power reserve. The Marlin Automatic Watch is perfect for those who want a watch that is both affordable and stylish.

Citizen

Citizen is a Japanese watch brand that has been around since 1918. They are known for their high-quality watches that are both stylish and durable. One of their best watches under $500 is the Citizen Men's Eco-Drive Watch. This watch features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a black dial, and a date function. It is also water-resistant up to 100 meters and comes with an Eco-Drive movement that is powered by light. The Eco-Drive Watch is perfect for those who want a watch that is both stylish and environmentally friendly.

Q: Can I find a watch under $500 with a date function?

Q: Are there any waterproof watches under $500?

Conclusion of Best Men's Watch Brands Under $500

Overall, there are several good quality watches under $500 that are both stylish and durable. We have discussed some of the best men's watch brands under $500, including Bulova, Seiko, Timex, and Citizen. When buying a watch under $500, it is important to look for watches with a stainless steel case and bracelet, a mineral or sapphire crystal, and a clear and easy-to-read display.
