Tanishq

Tanishq is a well-known jewellery brand in India that offers a wide range of artificial jewellery designs. They use high-quality materials such as gold, silver, and diamonds, which ensure the durability of the product. Tanishq's artificial jewellery collection includes traditional designs such as jhumkas, necklaces, and bangles, as well as modern designs such as statement earrings and bracelets. Their prices are reasonable, and they offer discounts and promotions throughout the year.

Swarovski

Swarovski is a popular international brand that offers a wide range of artificial jewellery designs. Their jewellery is made with high-quality materials such as crystal, which gives it a unique and elegant look. Swarovski's artificial jewellery collection includes necklaces, earrings, bracelets, and rings, which are perfect for any occasion. Their prices are on the higher side, but the quality of their products justifies the cost.

Fashion Jewellery

Fashion Jewellery is a budget-friendly brand that offers a wide range of artificial jewellery designs. Their jewellery is made with non-precious metals such as brass and copper, which makes it affordable yet stylish. Fashion Jewellery's collection includes traditional and modern designs that cater to every taste and occasion. Their prices are reasonable, and they offer discounts and promotions throughout the year.
