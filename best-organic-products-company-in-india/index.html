FAQs

Q. Are organic products more expensive than non-organic products?

A. Yes, organic products are generally more expensive than non-organic products. This is because organic farming practices require more labor and resources than conventional farming practices.

Q. Are all organic products the same?

A. No, not all organic products are the same. Different companies have different farming and manufacturing practices, which can affect the quality of the product.

Q. Are organic products better for the environment?

A. Yes, organic products are better for the environment. Organic farming practices promote sustainability and reduce the environmental impact of agriculture.

Q. Can I trust the certification on organic products?

A. Yes, you can trust the certification on organic products if it is from a reputable organization. Look for certifications from organizations such as USDA, Ecocert, and Soil Association.
