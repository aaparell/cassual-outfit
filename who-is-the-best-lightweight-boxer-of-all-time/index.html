After taking into account all the factors, the answer to who is the best lightweight boxer of all time is none other than Roberto Duran.

Duran was born in Panama in 1951 and began boxing at the age of 8. He turned professional at the age of 16 and went on to have an incredible career spanning five decades. Duran held world titles in four different weight classes and had a record of 103 wins, with 70 of those wins coming by knockout. Roberto Duran's legacy as the best lightweight boxer of all time cannot be denied.
