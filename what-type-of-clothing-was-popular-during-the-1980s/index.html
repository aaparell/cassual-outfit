In the 1980s, fashion was all about standing out and making a statement. It was a time where people expressed themselves through their clothes, and nothing was too bold or too bright. From shoulder pads to neon colors, the 80s had a style all its own.

For some, the idea of dressing like it's the 80s may seem daunting or uncomfortable. However, understanding the popular clothing trends of the decade can help you bring a touch of the 80s to your wardrobe without feeling like you're wearing a costume.

What type of clothing was popular during the 1980s?

The 1980s was a decade of excess, and nowhere was that more evident than in the fashion. Some of the most popular clothing items during the 80s included:

1. Shoulder Pads

Shoulder pads were a quintessential part of 80s fashion. They were worn by both men and women and were used to create a bold, powerful silhouette. From blazers to dresses, shoulder pads were everywhere.

Pro Tip:

If you're looking to add some 80s flair to your wardrobe, try wearing a blazer with shoulder pads or a dress with puffy sleeves.

2. Neon Colors

Neon colors were all the rage in the 80s. From bright pinks to electric greens, neon hues were used to make a bold statement. These colors were often paired with black to create a striking contrast.

Pro Tip:

If you're hesitant to wear neon, try incorporating it into your wardrobe with accessories like shoes or jewelry.

3. Acid Wash Jeans

Acid wash jeans were a staple of 80s fashion. These jeans had a worn, faded look that was achieved through a process of using bleach and pumice stones. They were often paired with oversized tops and chunky jewelry.

Pro Tip:

If you want to add some acid wash jeans to your wardrobe without going full 80s, try wearing a pair with a simple white t-shirt and sneakers.

4. Leg Warmers

Leg warmers were a popular accessory in the 80s. They were worn with leggings or tights and were often layered over each other for a bold, colorful look.

Pro Tip:

If you're looking to add some leg warmers to your wardrobe, try wearing them with ankle boots and a skirt for a modern take on an 80s classic.

What was the overall style of the 80s?

The overall style of the 80s was bold, colorful, and eclectic. The fashion of the decade was heavily influenced by pop culture, music, and movies. It was a time when people were encouraged to express themselves through their clothes and to have fun with fashion.

What are some 80s fashion trends that are popular today?

Some 80s fashion trends that have made a comeback in recent years include:

1. High Waisted Jeans

High waisted jeans were a popular style in the 80s and have made a comeback in recent years. They are a flattering style that can be dressed up or down.

2. Oversized Tops

Oversized tops were a staple of 80s fashion, and they have become popular once again. They can be worn with leggings or skinny jeans for a comfortable, casual look.

3. Scrunchies

Scrunchies were a popular hair accessory in the 80s, and they have made a comeback in recent years. They are a fun way to add some color and texture to your hair.

4. Fanny Packs

Fanny packs were a popular accessory in the 80s, and they have become popular once again. They are a convenient way to carry your essentials while keeping your hands free.

Conclusion of what type of clothing was popular during the 1980s

The 1980s was a decade of excess, and nowhere was that more evident than in the fashion. From shoulder pads to neon colors, the 80s had a style all its own. If you're looking to add some 80s flair to your wardrobe, try incorporating some of these trends into your outfits. Remember, the most important thing is to have fun with fashion and to express yourself through your clothes.
