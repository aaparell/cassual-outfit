Dhoti

Dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth that is worn as a lower garment. It is usually made of cotton, silk, or linen and is draped around the waist and legs. Dhoti is a common attire for men in rural areas and is also worn for religious ceremonies.

How to Wear a Dhoti

To wear a dhoti, you need to tuck one end of the cloth into your waistband, take the other end and wrap it around your legs, and then tuck it into your waistband again. It may take some practice to get it right, but once you do, it's a comfortable and elegant attire to wear.
