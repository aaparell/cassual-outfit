What To Wear To An Outdoor Spring Wedding

Written by Christine Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you attending an outdoor spring wedding but have no idea what to wear? Don't worry, you're not alone! Dressing for an outdoor spring wedding can be tricky, especially if you're not sure what the dress code is or if the weather is unpredictable. But fear not, we're here to help you navigate this fashion conundrum and make sure you look your best on the big day.

When it comes to what to wear to an outdoor spring wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to dress appropriately for the occasion. This means taking into account the dress code, the location, and the time of day. You also want to make sure that you're comfortable and that your outfit is weather-appropriate, as spring weather can be unpredictable.

To ensure you're perfectly dressed, here are some tips on what to wear to an outdoor spring wedding:

1. Consider the Dress Code

Before you start planning your outfit, make sure you know the dress code. If the invitation specifies black tie or formal attire, you'll need to dress accordingly. If it's a more casual affair, you can dress down a bit. However, keep in mind that even if the dress code is casual, you still want to look put together and stylish.

2. Choose Light Fabrics

Spring weather can be warm and sunny, so it's a good idea to choose light fabrics that will keep you cool and comfortable. Opt for breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, or silk. Avoid heavy materials like wool or velvet, as they can be too warm for outdoor weddings.

3. Embrace Spring Colors

Spring is all about fresh, vibrant colors, so embrace the season and choose a colorful dress or outfit. Pastels are always a great choice for spring weddings, but you can also opt for bright, bold hues that will make a statement. Just make sure to avoid wearing white or anything that could be mistaken for a wedding dress.

4. Accessorize Wisely

When it comes to accessories, less is often more. Choose a few key pieces that will complement your outfit and add some flair. For example, a statement necklace or earrings can elevate a simple dress, while a clutch or small handbag can be both functional and fashionable. Just make sure your accessories don't overpower your outfit.

5. Wear Comfortable Shoes

An outdoor wedding usually means walking on grass or uneven terrain, so make sure you choose comfortable shoes that you can walk in. Flats or low heels are a great option, but if you prefer heels, choose a chunky or block heel that will provide more stability.

6. Dress in Layers

Spring weather can be unpredictable, so it's always a good idea to dress in layers. Choose a dress or outfit that you can layer with a cardigan, jacket, or shawl, in case the temperature drops or the wind picks up.

Conclusion of What to Wear to an Outdoor Spring Wedding

Attending an outdoor spring wedding can be both exciting and challenging when it comes to choosing the right outfit. But with these tips, you can make sure you're perfectly dressed for the occasion. Remember to consider the dress code, choose light fabrics, embrace spring colors, accessorize wisely, wear comfortable shoes, and dress in layers. With these guidelines in mind, you're sure to look stylish and feel comfortable at any outdoor spring wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a floral dress to an outdoor spring wedding?

A: Absolutely! Floral dresses are perfect for outdoor spring weddings. Just make sure the dress is appropriate for the dress code and doesn't clash with the wedding party's attire.

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to an outdoor spring wedding?

A: Yes, you can! Jumpsuits can be a stylish and comfortable option for outdoor spring weddings. Just make sure the jumpsuit is appropriate for the dress code and that you accessorize it appropriately.

Q: Can I wear white to an outdoor spring wedding?

A: It's generally best to avoid wearing white to a wedding, as this color is typically reserved for the bride. Opt for pastels or other spring colors instead.

Q: Can I wear sandals to an outdoor spring wedding?

A: Sandals can be a great option for outdoor spring weddings, as long as they're dressy enough for the occasion. Avoid flip-flops or anything too casual, and choose sandals with a low heel or wedge for added comfort and stability.
