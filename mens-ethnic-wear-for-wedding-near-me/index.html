Types of Men's Ethnic Wear for Wedding Near Me

There are several types of men's ethnic wear for a wedding near you. These include:

Sherwani

A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar. It is typically made from silk or brocade and is often embroidered with intricate designs. Sherwanis are a popular choice for grooms and are usually worn with a turban and mojari shoes.

Kurta

A kurta is a long tunic that is worn over pajama pants or churidar. It can be made from a variety of fabrics, including cotton, silk, and linen. Kurta pajama sets are a popular choice for weddings and can range from simple and elegant to ornate and intricately embroidered.

Dhoti

A dhoti is a traditional Indian garment that is worn by men. It is a rectangular piece of cloth that is wrapped around the waist and legs. Dhotis are typically made from cotton or silk and are often worn with a kurta.
