Discovering Australian Skate Brands

Written by Robby Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Skateboarding is not just a sport or a hobby, but a way of life for many people. If you are a skateboarder, you know the importance of having the right gear and equipment. One of the most crucial components of skateboarding is the skateboard itself. Skateboards come in various shapes and sizes, and choosing the right one can make all the difference in your performance. However, finding a reliable and suitable skateboard brand can be a daunting task, especially if you are looking for an Australian brand.

Skateboarders have specific needs when it comes to their gear. They need equipment that can withstand the harsh conditions of skateboarding, such as rough surfaces and hard landings. Additionally, skateboarders often look for brands that offer unique designs and styles that can help them express their individuality. However, finding a brand that meets all these requirements and is also Australian can be difficult.

If you are looking for a reliable and high-quality skateboard brand that is Australian, you are in luck. Australia has several exceptional skate brands that cater to skateboarders of all ages and skill levels. These brands offer everything from complete skateboards to individual components such as decks, wheels, and trucks.

The Best Australian Skate Brands

As an Australian skateboarder, I have had the pleasure of using several local skate brands. Here are some of the top Australian skate brands that you should check out:

1. PASS~PORT

PASS~PORT is a skateboarding company based in Sydney, Australia. The brand was established in 2009 and has since gained a reputation for its unique designs and high-quality products. PASS~PORT offers a wide range of skateboards, apparel, and accessories that cater to skateboarders of all levels.

2. Butter Goods

Butter Goods is an Australian skate brand that was founded in Perth in 2008. The brand offers a wide range of skateboards, clothing, and accessories that are designed to meet the needs of skateboarders. Butter Goods is known for its unique designs and high-quality products, which have made it popular among skateboarders worldwide.

3. Globe

Globe is a global skateboarding brand that was founded in Melbourne, Australia, in 1994. The brand offers a wide range of skateboards, shoes, and apparel that cater to skateboarders of all levels. Globe is known for its innovative designs and high-quality products, which have made it a favorite among skateboarders worldwide.

4. DSCO

DSCO is a skateboarding company based in Melbourne, Australia. The brand offers a wide range of skateboards, apparel, and accessories that are designed to meet the needs of skateboarders. DSCO is known for its unique designs and high-quality products, which have made it popular among skateboarders worldwide.

Why Choose Australian Skate Brands

Australian skate brands are known for their quality, durability, and unique designs. These brands understand the specific needs of skateboarders and create products that can meet those needs. Additionally, choosing an Australian skate brand means supporting local businesses and contributing to the growth of the skateboard community in Australia.

How to Choose the Right Australian Skate Brand

Choosing the right Australian skate brand depends on your personal preferences and needs. Consider factors such as the type of skateboarding you do, your skill level, and the design and style of the brand. Additionally, read reviews and ask other skateboarders for recommendations to find a brand that suits you.

Conclusion of what skate brands are Australian

If you are a skateboarder looking for a reliable and high-quality skateboard brand that is Australian, you have several great options. PASS~PORT, Butter Goods, Globe, and DSCO are just a few of the many exceptional Australian skate brands that offer unique designs and high-quality products. By choosing an Australian skate brand, you are not only getting a great product but also supporting local businesses and contributing to the growth of the skateboarding community in Australia.
