Where Can I Get Branded Shoes For Cheap In Bangalore

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you on the hunt for quality, branded shoes in Bangalore but don't want to break the bank? Look no further! In this article, we'll explore where you can find affordable branded shoes in Bangalore without sacrificing quality or style.

When it comes to shopping for shoes, there are a few pain points that come to mind. For one, many people assume that quality shoes come with a high price tag. Additionally, it can be time-consuming to navigate through the crowded markets and shopping districts in Bangalore to find the perfect pair at an affordable price.

So, where can you find affordable, branded shoes in Bangalore? The answer lies in a few hidden gems throughout the city.

Firstly, if you're looking for high-end brands at discounted prices, head to the outlets at Premium Malls such as Brand Factory, Orion Mall, and Phoenix Market City. Here, you'll find a wide selection of branded shoes at discounted prices, sometimes up to 70% off the original price.

Personal Experience

As someone who loves shoes but doesn't want to spend a fortune on them, I've found that Brand Factory is a great option. I recently purchased a pair of Nike shoes that were originally priced at Rs. 6,000 for only Rs. 2,500. The shoes were in perfect condition and looked as though they had never been worn before.

Another great option for affordable branded shoes in Bangalore is Commercial Street. Here, you'll find a vast array of stores selling shoes at a reasonable price. While you won't find high-end brands here, you'll find many local brands and styles that are both fashionable and affordable.

Online Shopping

If you prefer to do your shoe shopping from the comfort of your own home, there are plenty of online options to choose from. One popular website is Myntra, which offers a wide selection of branded shoes at discounted prices. Another great option is Amazon, which offers a variety of shoes at affordable prices, including many branded options.

Street Markets

If you're looking for a more authentic shopping experience, head to one of Bangalore's many street markets. Here, you'll find a vast array of shoes at affordable prices, including many branded options. Some popular street markets in Bangalore include Chickpet Market and Brigade Road Market.

Bargaining Tips

When shopping at markets or smaller stores, it's important to remember that bargaining is often expected. Be sure to negotiate the price with the seller to get the best deal possible. It's also a good idea to bring cash, as many smaller stores may not accept credit or debit cards.

Personal Experience

I recently visited Brigade Road Market and was able to find a pair of Adidas shoes that were originally priced at Rs. 4,500 for only Rs. 2,000 after bargaining with the seller. The shoes were in excellent condition and were the perfect addition to my shoe collection.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find high-end brands at affordable prices in Bangalore?
A: Yes, you can find high-end brands at discounted prices at Premium Malls such as Brand Factory, Orion Mall, and Phoenix Market City.

Q: Are there any online options for affordable branded shoes?
A: Yes, websites such as Myntra and Amazon offer a wide selection of branded shoes at discounted prices.

Q: Is bargaining expected when shopping for shoes in Bangalore?
A: Yes, bargaining is often expected when shopping at markets or smaller stores.

Q: Are there any street markets in Bangalore that sell shoes?
A: Yes, some popular street markets in Bangalore include Chickpet Market and Brigade Road Market.

Conclusion of Where Can I Get Branded Shoes for Cheap in Bangalore

There are plenty of options for finding affordable, branded shoes in Bangalore. From Premium Malls to street markets, there is something for everyone. By exploring these hidden gems, you'll be able to find the perfect pair of shoes at a price that won't break the bank.
