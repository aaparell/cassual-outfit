Top Design Colleges In India Ranking A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for the best design colleges in India? Do you want to pursue a career in the design industry but don't know where to start? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll explore the top design colleges in India ranking and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about your future.

Design is a highly competitive field, and choosing the right college can make all the difference in your career. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which college to attend. Factors such as program offerings, faculty, location, and reputation can all play a role in your decision. That's why it's important to do your research and find a college that aligns with your career goals.

So, what are the top design colleges in India? According to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2021, the top five design colleges in India are:

National Institute of Design (NID)

Founded in 1961, NID is one of the most prestigious design colleges in India. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines such as product design, graphic design, animation film design, and more. NID is known for its innovative curriculum, industry partnerships, and world-class faculty.

Industrial Design Centre, IIT Bombay

Established in 1969, IDC is a leading design school in India. It offers a Master of Design program in areas such as communication design, interaction design, and industrial design. IDC is known for its strong focus on research, collaboration with industry, and state-of-the-art facilities.

National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)

Founded in 1986, NIFT is a premier fashion school in India. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines such as fashion design, textile design, and fashion communication. NIFT is known for its excellent faculty, industry collaborations, and international exposure.

Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology

Established in 1996, Srishti is a multidisciplinary design school located in Bangalore. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in areas such as visual communication, product design, and contemporary art practice. Srishti is known for its innovative approach to design education, focus on sustainability, and international partnerships.

MIT Institute of Design

Founded in 2006, MIT Institute of Design is a leading design school located in Pune. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in areas such as industrial design, communication design, and fashion design. MIT Institute of Design is known for its strong industry connections, practical approach to education, and modern facilities.

Why are these colleges ranked highly?

The colleges listed above are ranked highly for several reasons. Firstly, they have a strong emphasis on practical learning, with a focus on industry partnerships, internships, and projects. Secondly, they have world-class faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. Finally, they provide students with opportunities to showcase their work at national and international events, which helps them gain exposure and recognition in the industry.

How to prepare for the entrance exams?

To get admission to these top design colleges in India, you need to clear the entrance exams. The most common entrance exams for design courses are the National Entrance Examination for Design (NEED) and the Common Entrance Examination for Design (CEED). To prepare for these exams, you should start by familiarizing yourself with the exam pattern and syllabus. You can also join coaching institutes or online courses to get guidance and practice tests.

What are the career opportunities after graduation?

After graduation from these top design colleges in India, you can pursue a career in various fields such as product design, graphic design, fashion design, and more. Some of the most popular job roles in the design industry include:

  • Product Designer
  • Graphic Designer
  • Fashion Designer
  • User Experience (UX) Designer
  • Art Director

Additionally, you can also start your own design studio or work as a freelancer.

Conclusion of Top Design Colleges in India Ranking

Choosing the right design college is crucial for a successful career in the design industry. The top design colleges in India ranking by the NIRF provide an excellent starting point for your research. By considering factors such as program offerings, faculty, location, and reputation, you can find a college that aligns with your career goals. With the right education and skills, you can build a successful career in the design industry.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the eligibility criteria for admission to these colleges?

A: The eligibility criteria vary for each college and program. Generally, candidates need to have completed their 10+2 education or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks. Some colleges also require candidates to clear the entrance exam.

Q: Are these colleges expensive?

A: The fees for these colleges vary depending on the program and college. Generally, the fees range from Rs. 2-3 lakhs per year for undergraduate programs and Rs. 3-4 lakhs per year for postgraduate programs. However, these fees may be higher for international students.

Q: Can I get a scholarship to study in these colleges?

A: Yes, some of these colleges offer scholarships and financial aid to meritorious and deserving students. You can check the college website or contact the admissions office for more information.

Q: What is the duration of these programs?

A: The duration of undergraduate programs is generally 4 years, while the duration of postgraduate programs is 2 years.
