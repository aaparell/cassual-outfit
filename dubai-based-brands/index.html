Dubai Based Brands A Guide To The Best Local Brands

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old brands? Do you want to support local businesses in Dubai? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best Dubai based brands for you to explore.

Living in Dubai, it can be overwhelming to choose from the abundance of international brands available. However, supporting local brands not only helps small businesses thrive, but also promotes sustainability and ethical practices.

Our guide to Dubai based brands will help you discover unique and high-quality products, while also contributing to the local economy.

1. The Camel Soap Factory

Target: Interested in natural and handmade skincare products.

Personal Experience: I stumbled upon The Camel Soap Factory at a local market and was immediately drawn to their natural ingredients and unique designs. I purchased their Camel Milk Soap and was surprised at how smooth and moisturizing it was on my skin.

The Camel Soap Factory creates handmade soaps using natural ingredients such as camel milk, honey, and essential oils. Their products are free from harsh chemicals and come in a variety of designs and scents.

2. Sougha

Target: Interested in traditional Emirati crafts and supporting local artisans.

Personal Experience: I attended a workshop hosted by Sougha where I learned how to make a traditional palm leaf basket. It was a unique experience to connect with Emirati culture and learn from local artisans.

Sougha is a social enterprise that promotes traditional Emirati crafts and supports local artisans. They offer a variety of workshops and sell handmade products such as baskets, jewelry, and textiles.

3. Ripe Market

Target: Interested in organic produce, local food vendors, and handmade crafts.

The Ripe Market is a weekly community market that offers a variety of local produce, food vendors, and handmade crafts. It is a great way to support local businesses and discover unique products.

4. Clean Beauty ME

Target: Interested in clean and sustainable beauty products.

Clean Beauty ME is a Dubai based online store that offers a variety of clean and sustainable beauty products. They prioritize natural ingredients and eco-friendly packaging.

Question and Answer:

Q: Why should I support Dubai based brands?

A: Supporting local brands helps small businesses thrive, promotes sustainability and ethical practices, and contributes to the local economy.

Q: How do I discover new Dubai based brands?

A: You can attend local markets and events, follow Dubai based bloggers and influencers, or search online directories such as Dubai Design District.

Q: What are some benefits of using natural skincare products?

A: Natural skincare products are free from harsh chemicals and can be gentler on the skin, while also promoting sustainability and eco-friendliness.

Q: How can I be more sustainable in my beauty routine?

A: You can choose products with eco-friendly packaging, opt for natural and sustainable ingredients, and reduce waste by using reusable makeup remover pads and cotton swabs.

Conclusion of Dubai Based Brands:

By supporting Dubai based brands, you can discover unique and high-quality products while also contributing to the local economy and promoting sustainability. From natural skincare to traditional crafts, there is something for everyone in the world of Dubai based brands.
