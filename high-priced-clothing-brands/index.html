However, these brands often offer superior quality materials and craftsmanship, as well as unique designs that can't be found in lower priced options.

So, who is the target market for high priced clothing brands? It's usually fashion enthusiasts who are willing to invest in high quality, timeless pieces that will last for years to come. These individuals value style and quality over quantity and are willing to pay a premium for it.

What sets high priced clothing brands apart?

So, what sets high priced clothing brands apart from their lower priced counterparts? One major factor is the quality of materials used. High priced clothing items are often crafted from premium materials such as silk, cashmere, and leather.
