The Ultimate Guide To Old School Skate Clothing Brands

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a fan of skateboarding, you know that the right clothing can make all the difference. Old school skate clothing brands have been around for decades, and they continue to be popular among skaters of all ages. But with so many options out there, it can be tough to know which brands to trust. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at some of the most popular old school skate clothing brands and what makes them stand out.

Skaters have unique needs when it comes to clothing. They need gear that's durable, comfortable, and able to withstand the wear and tear of daily skating. Old school skate clothing brands understand these pain points and have been designing clothing that meets skaters' needs for decades.

The target of old school skate clothing brands is anyone who loves to skate, whether they're a professional or just starting out. These brands offer a wide range of clothing, from t-shirts and hoodies to shoes and accessories, all designed with skaters in mind.

In this article, we'll cover some of the most popular old school skate clothing brands, including Vans, Thrasher, Santa Cruz, and Powell Peralta. We'll also explore what makes these brands unique and why they've remained popular for so many years.

Vans: A Skateboarding Classic

When it comes to old school skate clothing brands, Vans is a true classic. Founded in 1966, Vans quickly became a favorite among skaters thanks to their durable, high-quality shoes. Today, Vans is known for their iconic slip-on sneakers and their comfortable, stylish clothing.

My personal experience with Vans goes back to my teenage years, when I bought my first pair of slip-on sneakers. I was immediately hooked - these shoes were comfortable, durable, and perfect for skating. Over the years, I've continued to wear Vans shoes and clothing, and I've never been disappointed.

Thrasher: The Skateboarding Bible

Thrasher is another old school skate clothing brand that's been around for decades. Founded in 1981, Thrasher started as a skateboarding magazine before branching out into clothing. Today, Thrasher is known for their edgy, punk-inspired clothing and accessories.

I first discovered Thrasher when I was a teenager and was immediately drawn to their rebellious, anti-establishment vibe. Their clothing was unlike anything else I had seen, and I loved the way it made me feel - like I was part of a community of skaters who didn't care about the mainstream.

Santa Cruz: A California Classic

Santa Cruz is a California-based skateboarding company that's been around since the 1970s. They're known for their bold, colorful designs and their commitment to quality. Santa Cruz offers a wide range of clothing, from t-shirts and hoodies to socks and hats.

One of my favorite things about Santa Cruz is their commitment to sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials whenever possible and are always looking for ways to reduce their environmental impact. Plus, their clothing is super comfortable and looks great on and off the skateboard.

Powell Peralta: A Skateboarding Legacy

Powell Peralta is another old school skate clothing brand with a rich history. Founded in the 1970s, Powell Peralta was one of the first companies to make skateboard decks out of durable, high-quality materials. Today, they offer a wide range of clothing and accessories, all designed with skaters in mind.

One of the things I love about Powell Peralta is their commitment to innovation. They're always experimenting with new materials and designs, and their clothing reflects this. Whether you're looking for a classic t-shirt or a cutting-edge hoodie, Powell Peralta has something for everyone.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes old school skate clothing brands different from other clothing brands?

A: Old school skate clothing brands are designed specifically for skaters. They're made with durable, high-quality materials and are designed to withstand the wear and tear of daily skating.

Q: Are old school skate clothing brands only for professional skaters?

A: Not at all! Old school skate clothing brands are for anyone who loves to skate, whether they're a professional or just starting out.

Q: What should I look for when shopping for old school skate clothing?

A: Look for clothing that's made from durable materials like cotton or polyester. You'll also want to make sure the clothing is comfortable and fits well.

Q: Can I wear old school skate clothing even if I don't skate?

A: Absolutely! Old school skate clothing is designed to be comfortable and stylish, so anyone can wear it.

Conclusion of Old School Skate Clothing Brands

Old school skate clothing brands have been around for decades, and they continue to be popular among skaters of all ages. Whether you're looking for durable shoes, comfortable t-shirts, or edgy accessories, there's an old school skate clothing brand out there for you. So why not embrace your inner skater and add some old school skate clothing to your wardrobe today?
