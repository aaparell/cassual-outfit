Average Wedding Suit Cost Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning your wedding and wondering how much you should budget for a wedding suit? As a groom, finding the perfect suit can be challenging, and it can be even more challenging to find a suit that fits your budget. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the average wedding suit cost in the UK and factors that can affect the cost.

When it comes to wedding suits, the cost can vary greatly depending on the style, material, and brand. This can be overwhelming for grooms who are trying to stay within a budget. Additionally, many grooms may not know what to look for in a suit, which can make the process even more challenging.

The average cost of a wedding suit in the UK ranges from £300 to £800. However, this cost can increase significantly if you choose a designer suit or a bespoke suit. The cost of a suit can also vary depending on the material, with wool and silk being more expensive than polyester or cotton. Other factors that can affect the cost include alterations, accessories, and the location of the store.

Personal Experience with Wedding Suit Cost

When I got married last year, I was on a tight budget and wanted to find an affordable suit that looked great. After doing some research, I found a store that had a great selection of suits at reasonable prices. I ended up spending around £500 on my suit, which included alterations and accessories such as a tie and pocket square. The suit looked great, and I received many compliments on it throughout the day.

Factors that Affect Wedding Suit Cost

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect the cost of a wedding suit. Here are some factors to consider:

Style

The style of the suit can greatly affect the cost. Designer suits or bespoke suits can be much more expensive than off-the-rack suits. If you are on a tight budget, consider looking for a suit that is classic and timeless, as these suits tend to be more affordable.

Material

The material of the suit can also affect the cost. Wool and silk are more expensive materials than polyester or cotton. However, wool and silk tend to be more durable and look more luxurious.

Location

The location of the store can also affect the cost. Stores in major cities tend to be more expensive than stores in smaller towns. However, larger stores may also have a wider selection of suits and more experienced staff who can help you find the perfect suit.

How to Save Money on a Wedding Suit

If you are on a tight budget, there are several ways to save money on a wedding suit:

Rent a Suit

If you don't plan on wearing the suit again, consider renting a suit instead of buying one. This can save you a significant amount of money and can still allow you to look great on your wedding day.

Shop Sales

Many stores offer sales throughout the year, so keep an eye out for discounts on suits. You can also consider shopping for a suit during off-peak seasons, as this can lead to further discounts.

Alterations

Instead of buying a bespoke suit, consider buying an off-the-rack suit and having it tailored to fit you perfectly. This can be a more affordable option and can still give you a great-looking suit.

Question and Answer

Q: How much does a designer wedding suit cost in the UK?

A: Designer wedding suits can cost anywhere from £1,500 to £5,000 or more, depending on the brand and style.

Q: How much should I budget for a wedding suit?

A: The average cost of a wedding suit in the UK is between £300 and £800, but this can vary depending on the style, material, and location of the store.

Q: Should I buy or rent a wedding suit?

A: If you plan on wearing the suit again, it may be worth buying a suit instead of renting one. However, if you don't plan on wearing the suit again, renting a suit can be a more affordable option.

Q: How far in advance should I buy my wedding suit?

A: It is recommended to buy your wedding suit at least 3-6 months before your wedding day to allow enough time for alterations and fittings.

Conclusion of Average Wedding Suit Cost UK

When it comes to buying a wedding suit, there are several factors to consider, including style, material, and location. The average cost of a wedding suit in the UK is between £300 and £800, but this can vary depending on several factors. By following the tips in this article, you can find a great-looking suit that fits within your budget.
