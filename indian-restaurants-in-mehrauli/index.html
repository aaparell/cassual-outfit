Discover The Best Indian Restaurants In Mehrauli

Written by Eveline Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to indulge in some delicious Indian cuisine, look no further than Mehrauli. Located in South Delhi, Mehrauli is home to some of the best Indian restaurants in the city. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to eat. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the best Indian restaurants in Mehrauli and help you make an informed decision.

Indian restaurants in Mehrauli cater to various palates and preferences. Whether you're a vegetarian or a non-vegetarian, prefer spicy or mild food, there's something for everyone. Additionally, Indian food is known for its rich flavors and spices, making it a popular choice amongst locals and tourists alike.

Now, let's dive into the best Indian restaurants in Mehrauli. From fine dining to street food, we've got you covered.

1. Andhra Pradesh Bhavan Canteen

If you're in the mood for some authentic South Indian food, head to Andhra Pradesh Bhavan Canteen. With its simple yet delicious food, this restaurant is a favorite amongst locals. Try their signature dish, the Hyderabadi biryani, which is a flavorful rice dish cooked with spices and meat or vegetables.

2. Qutub Minar Complex

Located within the Qutub Minar Complex, this restaurant offers a unique dining experience. With a view of the historic Qutub Minar, you can enjoy your meal in a serene and picturesque setting. The restaurant serves a variety of Indian dishes, including kebabs, curries, and biryanis. The food is cooked in traditional clay ovens, giving it an authentic taste.

3. Kuremal Mohan Lal Kulfi Wale

If you're in the mood for something sweet, head to Kuremal Mohan Lal Kulfi Wale. This iconic dessert shop has been around since 1908 and is known for its delectable kulfis. Made with natural fruits and nuts, their kulfis are a perfect way to cool off on a hot day.

4. Naivedyam

Naivedyam is a vegetarian restaurant that specializes in South Indian cuisine. With its simple yet flavorful food, it's a great option for those looking for a healthy meal. Try their signature dish, the dosa, which is a crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a variety of fillings.

5. The Potbelly Rooftop Cafe

The Potbelly Rooftop Cafe is a must-visit for those looking for a unique dining experience. Located on the rooftop of a heritage building, the restaurant offers a stunning view of the Qutub Minar. The food is a fusion of Bihari and European cuisine, making it a perfect spot for those looking for something different.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it safe to eat street food in Mehrauli?

A: While street food is a popular option in Mehrauli, it's important to exercise caution. Make sure to choose vendors who have a clean and hygienic setup. Additionally, it's recommended to stick to vegetarian options to avoid any health concerns.

Q: What's the best time to visit these Indian restaurants in Mehrauli?

A: The best time to visit these restaurants is during lunch or dinner hours, when they're open. Additionally, it's recommended to make a reservation beforehand to avoid any waiting time.

Q: Are these Indian restaurants in Mehrauli expensive?

A: The cost of dining at these restaurants varies depending on the establishment. While some are affordable, others can be on the pricier side. However, the quality of food and dining experience is worth the price.

Q: Do these Indian restaurants in Mehrauli offer takeout or delivery services?

A: Yes, many of these restaurants offer takeout and delivery services. You can place your order through their website or by calling their phone number.

Conclusion of Indian Restaurants in Mehrauli

Mehrauli is a foodie's paradise, with its diverse range of Indian restaurants. From street food to fine dining, there's something for everyone. So, the next time you're in the area, be sure to check out these Indian restaurants in Mehrauli for a memorable dining experience.
