Best Online Shopping In France A Guide To Finding The Best Deals

Written by Luffy Jun 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the best online shopping deals in France? Perhaps you're new to the country, or you just haven't found the right sites to shop from. Whatever the reason, you're not alone. Many expats and locals alike find it difficult to navigate the world of online shopping in France, but fear not - we're here to help!

One of the biggest pain points of shopping online in France is finding sites that offer a wide range of products at affordable prices. Unlike in other countries, where you might have dozens of options for a particular item, in France the selection can be more limited. Additionally, some of the larger international retailers may not offer shipping to France, or their prices may be too high due to import taxes and other fees.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to finding the best online shopping deals in France, including sites that offer a wide range of products, affordable prices, and fast shipping options.

In summary, the article will cover:

  • The best online shopping sites in France
  • How to find the best deals and discounts
  • Tips for safe and secure online shopping
  • Answers to common FAQs about online shopping in France

Best Online Shopping in France: Target and Personal Experience

When it comes to online shopping in France, one of our top picks is Amazon.fr. While the selection may not be as large as on the US or UK sites, you'll still find a wide range of products at competitive prices. Plus, if you're a Prime member, you'll get free and fast shipping on eligible items. We've personally used Amazon.fr for everything from electronics to home goods to clothing, and have always been happy with our purchases.

Other Top Online Shopping Sites in France

In addition to Amazon.fr, there are several other top online shopping sites in France that are worth checking out:

Cdiscount: With over 20 million products available, Cdiscount is one of the largest online retailers in France. They offer everything from electronics to home goods to fashion, and often have great deals and discounts available.

La Redoute: Known for their stylish and affordable clothing, La Redoute also offers a wide range of home goods and furniture. They frequently have sales and promotions, and offer free shipping on orders over 29€.

Fnac: Specializing in electronics and entertainment, Fnac has a wide selection of products at competitive prices. They also offer free shipping on orders over 25€, and have a loyalty program that rewards frequent shoppers.

How to Find the Best Deals and Discounts

One of the best ways to find deals and discounts when online shopping in France is to sign up for newsletters and alerts from your favorite retailers. Many sites offer exclusive promotions and sales to their subscribers, so you can be the first to know about the latest deals. Additionally, you can use price comparison tools like Google Shopping or PriceRunner to compare prices across multiple sites and find the best deal.

Tips for Safe and Secure Online Shopping

When shopping online in France, it's important to take certain precautions to ensure your personal and financial information is safe. Here are a few tips:

  • Only shop on secure sites that use HTTPS encryption
  • Use strong, unique passwords for each site
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi when making purchases
  • Check your credit card statements regularly for any unauthorized charges

Conclusion of Best Online Shopping in France

Overall, online shopping in France can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tools and resources, you can find great deals and get everything you need delivered right to your doorstep. By using sites like Amazon.fr and Cdiscount, signing up for newsletters and alerts, and taking necessary security precautions, you can shop with confidence and find the best deals in France.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I shop on international sites like Amazon.com or eBay.com from France?

A: Yes, you can shop on these sites from France, but keep in mind that shipping may be more expensive and take longer than on local sites. Additionally, you may be subject to import taxes and other fees.

Q: Are there any French-specific online shopping holidays or sales?

A: Yes, there are several major shopping holidays in France, including Black Friday (the fourth Friday in November) and French Days (a biannual event in the spring and fall). Many retailers also have their own sales and promotions throughout the year, so be sure to check their websites and sign up for newsletters to stay up-to-date.

Q: Do I need to have a French credit card to shop online in France?

A: No, most sites accept international credit cards, but be aware that you may be subject to foreign transaction fees from your bank.

Q: What should I do if I have a problem with an online order in France?

A: If you have an issue with an order, the first step is to contact the retailer's customer service department. If they are unable to resolve the issue, you can file a complaint with the French Directorate General for Competition, Consumer Affairs, and Fraud Control (DGCCRF).
