Mens T Shirt Sizes UK: How To Measure Yourself

The first step in finding the right size mens t-shirt is to measure yourself. You'll need a tape measure and someone to assist you. Here's how to do it:

Chest Measurement

Stand up straight and take the tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, just under your armpits. Make sure the tape measure is snug but not tight, and parallel to the ground. Note down the measurement in inches.

Waist Measurement

Take the tape measure around your natural waistline, which is typically the narrowest part of your waist. Again, make sure the tape measure is snug but not tight, and parallel to the ground. Note down the measurement in inches.

Shoulder Measurement

Ask your assistant to measure the distance between the end of your left shoulder to the end of your right shoulder. Note down the measurement in inches.

Mens T Shirt Sizes UK: Understanding The Different Fits And Styles

Now that you've measured yourself, it's time to understand the different fits and styles of mens t shirts. Here are the most common types:

Regular Fit

A regular fit t-shirt is a classic style that's neither too tight nor too loose. It's a versatile option that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

Slim Fit

A slim fit t-shirt is a more modern and fitted style. It's designed to hug your body and show off your physique, making it a great option for those who work out or have a slimmer build.

Loose Fit

A loose fit t-shirt is a more relaxed and comfortable style. It's designed to be worn loosely and is perfect for those who prefer a more casual look.

V-Neck
