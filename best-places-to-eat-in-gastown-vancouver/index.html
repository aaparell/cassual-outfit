Discovering The Best Places To Eat In Gastown Vancouver

Written by Aprilia Sep 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Gastown Vancouver is a popular tourist destination, known for its rich history and vibrant culture. If you're planning a trip to this area, you might be wondering where to find the best places to eat. After all, a good meal is an important part of any vacation. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the top restaurants in Gastown Vancouver, and help you plan your next culinary adventure.

When it comes to finding the best places to eat in Gastown Vancouver, there are a few important things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you'll want to consider your budget. While there are plenty of high-end restaurants in the area, there are also many more affordable options. Additionally, you'll want to think about the type of cuisine you're interested in. Gastown Vancouver has a diverse food scene, with everything from classic Canadian dishes to international fare.

So, what are the best places to eat in Gastown Vancouver? Here are just a few of our top picks:

1. L'Abattoir

Located in a historic building in the heart of Gastown, L'Abattoir is a popular spot for both locals and tourists. The restaurant offers a contemporary take on French cuisine, with dishes like duck breast and scallops. The atmosphere is cozy and intimate, making it a great spot for a date night or special occasion.

2. Tacofino

If you're in the mood for something a little more casual, Tacofino is a great option. This popular eatery serves up some of the best tacos in Vancouver, with fillings like crispy chicken and Baja fish. The atmosphere is laid-back and fun, with colorful decor and a friendly staff.

3. The Flying Pig

For a hearty meal that won't break the bank, check out The Flying Pig. This gastropub serves up classic comfort food like mac and cheese and pork belly, as well as more refined dishes like seared scallops. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming, with plenty of local beers on tap.

4. Water St. Cafe

If you're looking for a romantic dinner spot with a view, Water St. Cafe is a great choice. This elegant restaurant overlooks the waterfront, and serves up classic Italian dishes like spaghetti carbonara and osso buco. The wine list is extensive, making it a great spot for a special occasion.

5. Bao Bei Chinese Brasserie

If you're in the mood for some Chinese-inspired cuisine, Bao Bei Chinese Brasserie is a must-visit. This trendy restaurant serves up dishes like pork belly bao and mapo tofu, all with a modern twist. The atmosphere is chic and stylish, making it a great spot for a night out with friends.

A Personal Experience

During my recent trip to Gastown Vancouver, I had the chance to dine at L'Abattoir. I was blown away by the cozy atmosphere and delicious food. I opted for the duck breast, which was cooked to perfection and served with a side of seasonal vegetables. The dessert menu was equally impressive, with options like chocolate ganache and almond cake. Overall, I would highly recommend L'Abattoir to anyone looking for a special dining experience in Gastown Vancouver.

Expert Tips

If you're planning a trip to Gastown Vancouver, here are a few expert tips to keep in mind:

1. Make a Reservation

Many of the top restaurants in Gastown Vancouver are quite popular, so it's a good idea to make a reservation in advance. This will ensure that you can snag a table at your preferred time and date.

2. Explore the Neighborhood

Gastown Vancouver is a vibrant neighborhood with plenty of shops, galleries, and attractions to explore. Consider taking a stroll before or after your meal to soak up the local culture.

Question and Answer

Q: What type of cuisine can I expect to find in Gastown Vancouver?

A: Gastown Vancouver has a diverse food scene, with everything from classic Canadian dishes to international fare. Some popular options include French cuisine, tacos, and Chinese-inspired dishes.

Q: Are there any budget-friendly restaurants in Gastown Vancouver?

A: Yes! While there are plenty of high-end restaurants in the area, there are also many more affordable options. The Flying Pig, for example, serves up classic comfort food at reasonable prices.

Q: Do I need to make a reservation at the top restaurants in Gastown Vancouver?

A: Yes, it's a good idea to make a reservation in advance. Many of the top restaurants in the area are quite popular, and tables can book up quickly.

Q: What else is there to do in Gastown Vancouver besides eat?

A: Gastown Vancouver is a vibrant neighborhood with plenty of shops, art galleries, and historic attractions to explore. Consider taking a stroll down Water Street, or checking out the famous steam clock.

Conclusion of Best Places to Eat in Gastown Vancouver

Whether you're a foodie or just looking for a good meal, Gastown Vancouver has plenty to offer. From high-end French cuisine to laid-back tacos, there's something for everyone in this lively neighborhood. Keep in mind our expert tips and personal experiences, and you're sure to have a great dining adventure in Gastown Vancouver.
