Where To Buy Traditional Hawaiian Clothing

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning a trip to Hawaii and want to experience the local culture by wearing traditional Hawaiian clothing? Perhaps you're a fan of the vibrant prints and styles and want to incorporate them into your everyday wardrobe. Whatever your reason may be, finding where to buy traditional Hawaiian clothing can be a challenge.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to finding traditional Hawaiian clothing is knowing where to look. With so many tourist shops and online retailers claiming to sell authentic Hawaiian clothing, it's hard to know who to trust and where to find the real deal.

The good news is that there are plenty of options when it comes to where to buy traditional Hawaiian clothing. Local boutiques and markets are a great place to start, as well as online retailers with a strong reputation for selling authentic Hawaiian clothing.

In summary, when it comes to where to buy traditional Hawaiian clothing, there are a variety of options available. Local boutiques and markets, as well as reputable online retailers, are great places to start your search.

Local Boutiques and Markets

One of the best ways to find authentic traditional Hawaiian clothing is by visiting local boutiques and markets. These shops often carry handmade items and designs that you won't find anywhere else. Plus, you'll be supporting local artisans and businesses.

During my recent trip to Hawaii, I stumbled upon a small boutique in Honolulu that specialized in traditional Hawaiian clothing. The owner was friendly and knowledgeable, and I was able to find a beautiful muumuu dress that fit me perfectly. I also picked up a few accessories like a lei and hair clip that completed the look.

Reputable Online Retailers

If you're unable to visit Hawaii in person or want the convenience of shopping from home, there are plenty of reputable online retailers that sell traditional Hawaiian clothing. However, it's important to do your research and make sure you're buying from a trusted source.

One online retailer that I've had success with is Hilo Hattie. They've been around for over 50 years and are known for their authentic Hawaiian clothing and accessories. They even offer a guarantee of authenticity, so you can shop with confidence.

Additional Tips for Buying Traditional Hawaiian Clothing

When shopping for traditional Hawaiian clothing, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, pay attention to the material. Look for items made from lightweight, breathable fabrics like cotton or rayon. These materials will keep you cool in the tropical climate.

Second, consider the style. Traditional Hawaiian clothing is known for its bright, bold prints and relaxed, flowing silhouettes. Look for designs that embrace these elements.

How to Care for Traditional Hawaiian Clothing

Once you've found the perfect traditional Hawaiian clothing item, it's important to take care of it properly. Always follow the care instructions on the label, and avoid exposing the garment to direct sunlight or harsh chemicals.

When washing, use a gentle detergent and cold water. Avoid using a dryer, as the heat can damage the fabric. Instead, hang the garment to dry in a shaded area.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Traditional Hawaiian Clothing

Overall, finding where to buy traditional Hawaiian clothing doesn't have to be a headache. Whether you prefer to shop in person or online, there are plenty of options available. Just remember to do your research, pay attention to the materials and style, and take care of your garments properly.
