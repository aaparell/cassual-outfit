Question and Answer

Q: Can I find good shoe brands for wide feet in a regular shoe store?

A: While some regular shoe stores may carry shoes for wider feet, it's best to shop at stores that specialize in shoes for wider feet. This will give you a better chance of finding shoes that fit comfortably.

Q: How do I know if a shoe is the right width for my feet?

A: The shoe should fit snugly around the widest part of your foot without feeling tight or constricting. You should be able to wiggle your toes freely.

Q: Are there any other brands that offer shoes for people with wider feet?
