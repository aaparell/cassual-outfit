Virgil Abloh

Virgil Abloh is an American fashion designer, DJ, and entrepreneur. He is the founder of the fashion label Off-White and has been the artistic director of Louis Vuitton's men's wear collection since 2018. Abloh is known for his innovative designs that blend streetwear and high fashion. His unique style has made him one of the top fashion designers in the world for 2020.

Marc Jacobs

Marc Jacobs is an American fashion designer who is known for his unique style and creativity. He is the founder of the Marc Jacobs and Marc by Marc Jacobs fashion labels and has been the creative director of Louis Vuitton's women's wear collection. Jacobs is known for his bold colors, unexpected combinations, and innovative designs. His unique style has made him one of the top fashion designers in the world for 2020.

Miuccia Prada

Miuccia Prada is an Italian fashion designer who is known for her innovative designs and unique style. She is the head designer of the Prada fashion label and has been the artistic director of Miu Miu since 1993. Prada is known for her bold colors, unexpected silhouettes, and innovative use of materials. Her unique style has made her one of the top fashion designers in the world for 2020.

Riccardo Tisci

Riccardo Tisci is an Italian fashion designer who is known for his unique style and creativity. He is the creative director of the Burberry fashion label and has previously worked for Givenchy. Tisci is known for his bold colors, unexpected combinations, and innovative designs. His unique style has made him one of the top fashion designers in the world for 2020.
