The Most Popular Dress In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions, which is reflected in its clothing as well. Every state in India has its own unique clothing style, but some dresses have gained widespread popularity throughout the country. In this post, we will be discussing the most popular dress in India and everything you need to know about it.

When it comes to dressing up for any occasion, Indians prefer something that is comfortable, stylish, and versatile. The most popular dress in India fulfills all these requirements and has become a favorite among women of all ages.

The most popular dress in India is the saree, a six-yard-long piece of cloth that is draped around the body in various styles. It is a traditional Indian attire that has been worn by women for centuries and has now become a fashion statement worldwide.

In this post, we will be discussing the history of sarees, the different types of sarees available, how to drape a saree, and where to buy sarees in India.

The History of Sarees

As mentioned earlier, sarees have been a part of Indian culture for centuries. The earliest references to sarees can be found in the Vedas, which are ancient Hindu texts. Sarees were initially worn as a simple garment to cover the body, but over time, they became more elaborate and stylish.

Sarees were originally made of cotton, but with the advent of trade, silk sarees became popular as well. Today, you can find sarees made of a variety of fabrics, including silk, cotton, georgette, chiffon, and crepe.

Types of Sarees

There are countless types of sarees available in India, each with its own unique design and style. Some of the most popular types of sarees include:

1. Banarasi Sarees: These sarees are made in Varanasi and are known for their intricate designs and gold and silver embroidery.

2. Kanjeevaram Sarees: These sarees are made in Kanchipuram and are known for their vibrant colors and heavy silk fabric.

3. Chiffon Sarees: These sarees are made of chiffon fabric and are lightweight and easy to drape. They are perfect for parties and other formal occasions.

4. Cotton Sarees: These sarees are made of cotton fabric and are perfect for everyday wear. They are comfortable and easy to manage.

How to Drape a Saree

Draping a saree can seem daunting at first, but with a little practice, anyone can do it. The most popular way to drape a saree is the Nivi style, which involves pleating the fabric at the waist and draping it over the shoulder.

Where to Buy Sarees in India

You can buy sarees in India from a variety of places, including local markets, malls, and online stores. Some of the most popular places to buy sarees in India include:

1. Chandni Chowk: Located in Delhi, Chandni Chowk is a popular market for sarees and other traditional Indian clothing.

2. Commercial Street: Located in Bangalore, Commercial Street is a popular shopping destination for sarees and other clothing items.

3. M.G. Road: Located in Mumbai, M.G. Road is a popular shopping destination for designer sarees and other high-end clothing items.

Conclusion of Most Popular Dress in India

The saree is undoubtedly the most popular dress in India and has become an integral part of Indian culture. It is a versatile attire that can be worn for any occasion and is loved by women of all ages. So, if you're planning to visit India, make sure to buy a saree and try draping it in different styles!
