Classic Sunglasses Brands A Timeless Fashion Statement

Written by Eveline May 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is here and it's time to protect our eyes from the sun's harmful rays. One of the most stylish ways to do this is with a pair of classic sunglasses. Not only do they provide protection, but they also add a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

However, finding the perfect pair of classic sunglasses can be a challenge. With so many brands and styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming. Plus, not all sunglasses are equal in terms of quality and durability.

If you're in the market for a new pair of classic sunglasses, keep reading to learn about some of the most popular brands and what to look for when shopping.

Classic sunglasses brands are those that have stood the test of time and have remained popular for decades. These brands are known for their quality, durability, and timeless style. They are often associated with celebrities and fashion icons, and are a must-have accessory for anyone who wants to make a fashion statement.

Ray-Ban: The Iconic Classic Sunglasses Brand

When it comes to classic sunglasses brands, Ray-Ban is one of the most iconic. Founded in 1937, Ray-Ban has been a favorite among celebrities and fashionistas for decades. Their most popular styles include the Aviator and Wayfarer, both of which have become synonymous with the brand.

Personally, I've been a fan of Ray-Ban sunglasses for years. I own a pair of Aviators that I wear all the time. They're comfortable, stylish, and provide great sun protection. Plus, they go with just about any outfit.

Maui Jim: The Classic Sunglasses Brand for Outdoor Enthusiasts

If you're looking for classic sunglasses that are perfect for outdoor activities like hiking, fishing, and boating, Maui Jim is the brand for you. Founded in Hawaii in 1980, Maui Jim is known for their polarized lenses, which provide superior glare protection.

I recently purchased a pair of Maui Jim sunglasses for a beach vacation and they were a game-changer. The polarized lenses made it so much easier to see in the bright sunlight and the frames were lightweight and comfortable to wear all day.

Polarized Lenses: What They Are and Why They Matter

Polarized lenses are a type of sunglass lens that reduce glare and improve visibility in bright sunlight. They work by filtering out horizontal light waves, which are the main cause of glare. This makes them ideal for outdoor activities like driving, boating, and fishing.

When shopping for classic sunglasses, it's important to consider whether or not you want polarized lenses. While they do offer superior sun protection, they can be more expensive than non-polarized lenses.

Caring for Your Classic Sunglasses

Once you've found the perfect pair of classic sunglasses, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your sunglasses:

  • Store them in a case when not in use to prevent scratches
  • Clean them regularly with a microfiber cloth and lens cleaner
  • Avoid leaving them in hot cars or exposing them to extreme temperatures

Questions and Answers about Classic Sunglasses Brands

Q: How do I know if a pair of sunglasses is high-quality?

A: Look for sunglasses that have a sturdy frame, high-quality lenses, and a warranty or return policy.

Q: Are expensive sunglasses worth the price?

A: It depends on what you're looking for. Expensive sunglasses are often made with higher-quality materials and offer better sun protection. However, there are also many affordable options that are just as good.

Q: Can I wear classic sunglasses with any outfit?

A: Yes! Classic sunglasses are versatile and can be dressed up or down. They go well with everything from a casual t-shirt and shorts to a formal suit and tie.

Q: What should I look for when choosing a pair of classic sunglasses?

A: Consider factors like the shape and size of the frames, the color and type of lenses, and any special features like polarized lenses or UV protection.

Conclusion of Classic Sunglasses Brands

When it comes to classic sunglasses brands, there are many great options to choose from. Whether you prefer the iconic style of Ray-Ban or the polarized lenses of Maui Jim, there's a pair of sunglasses out there that's perfect for you.

When shopping for classic sunglasses, be sure to consider factors like quality, style, and sun protection. And don't forget to take care of your sunglasses once you've found the perfect pair!
