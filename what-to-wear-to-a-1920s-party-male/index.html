What To Wear To A 1920S Party Male A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you excited for an upcoming 1920s party, but not sure what to wear? Don't worry, we have got you covered! Dressing up for a 1920s party can be tricky, but with the right guidance, you can look like a dapper gentleman from the era.

Attending a 1920s party can be stressful when you are not familiar with the fashion trends of the era. You may feel confused about what to wear, what accessories to pair with your outfit, and how to stay comfortable while also looking fashionable.

The key to dressing up for a 1920s party is to embrace the fashion trends of the era. Men's fashion during the 1920s was all about being sleek, stylish, and sophisticated. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about what to wear to a 1920s party male.

To summarize, in this guide, we will cover the fashion trends of the 1920s, the essential pieces you need for your outfit, and how to accessorize your outfit to complete your 1920s look. Let's dive in!

Understanding the Fashion Trends of the 1920s

Before you start planning your outfit, it is essential to understand the fashion trends of the 1920s. The 1920s was a decade of change and innovation, and this reflected in the fashion trends of the era.

Men's fashion during the 1920s was all about being sophisticated and stylish. The clothing was designed to be sleek, with clean lines and minimal embellishments. The suits were tailored to fit the body perfectly, with high-waisted pants and narrow lapels.

The fabrics used for men's clothing during the 1920s were lightweight and breathable. Wool, linen, and cotton were popular choices, and the colors were muted and subdued. Gray, brown, and navy were the most common colors used in men's clothing during the 1920s.

Essential Pieces for Your 1920s Outfit

Now that you understand the fashion trends of the 1920s, it's time to start planning your outfit. Here are the essential pieces you need for your 1920s outfit:

Suit: A suit is the most important part of your 1920s outfit. Look for a suit that is tailored to fit your body perfectly, with high-waisted pants and narrow lapels. The suit should be made of lightweight and breathable fabric, such as wool, linen, or cotton.

Shirt: Choose a shirt that is made of lightweight fabric, such as cotton or linen. The shirt should have a pointed collar and French cuffs.

Tie: A tie is an essential accessory for any 1920s outfit. Choose a tie that is made of silk or cotton and has a muted pattern or solid color.

Hat: A hat is a stylish accessory that can complete your 1920s look. Look for a fedora or a newsboy cap.

Accessorize Your 1920s Outfit

Once you have the essential pieces for your 1920s outfit, it is time to accessorize your look. Here are some accessories that you can add to your outfit:

Cufflinks: Cufflinks are an essential accessory for any 1920s outfit. Choose cufflinks that are made of gold or silver and have a simple design.

Pocket Square: A pocket square is a stylish accessory that can add a pop of color to your outfit. Choose a pocket square that complements the color of your suit.

Shoes: Look for shoes that are made of leather and have a classic design, such as oxfords or brogues.

Tips for a Perfect 1920s Look

Now that you know what to wear to a 1920s party male, here are some tips to help you create a perfect 1920s look:

Fit is Everything: The fit of your outfit is essential when it comes to creating a perfect 1920s look. Make sure your suit is tailored to fit your body perfectly.

Accessorize Wisely: Accessories can make or break your 1920s look, so choose them wisely. Stick to simple and classic accessories that complement your outfit.

Don't Forget the Details: Pay attention to the details of your outfit, such as the style of your tie, the color of your pocket square, and the design of your cufflinks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear a three-piece suit to a 1920s party?
A: Yes, a three-piece suit is a great option for a 1920s party. Look for a suit that is tailored to fit your body perfectly, with high-waisted pants and narrow lapels.

Q: What color suit should I wear to a 1920s party?
A: Gray, brown, and navy were the most common colors used in men's clothing during the 1920s. Choose a suit in one of these colors for a classic 1920s look.

Q: Can I wear suspenders with my suit?
A: Yes, suspenders are a stylish accessory that can complete your 1920s look. Choose suspenders that are made of leather or fabric and have a classic design.

Q: What type of shoes should I wear to a 1920s party?
A: Look for shoes that are made of leather and have a classic design, such as oxfords or brogues.

Conclusion

Now that you know what to wear to a 1920s party male, you can create a perfect 1920s look that will turn heads at the party. Remember to focus on the fit of your outfit, choose accessories wisely, and pay attention to the details. With these tips and tricks, you will look like a dapper gentleman from the 1920s!
