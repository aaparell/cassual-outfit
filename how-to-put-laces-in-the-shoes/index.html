Step-by-Step Guide on How to Put Laces in Your Shoes

Step 1: Thread the lace through the bottom eyelet of the shoe. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 2: Pull the lace tight to ensure that it is secure in the eyelet. This will prevent the lace from slipping out of the eyelet when you are lacing up your shoes.

Step 3: Take the left lace and cross it over the right lace.

Step 4: Take the left lace and thread it through the second eyelet on the right side of the shoe.

Step 5: Pull the left lace tight to ensure that it is secure in the second eyelet.

Step 6: Take the right lace and cross it over the left lace.

Step 7: Take the right lace and thread it through the second eyelet on the left side of the shoe.

Step 8: Pull the right lace tight to ensure that it is secure in the second eyelet.

Step 9: Repeat steps 3-8 until you reach the top of the shoe.

Step 10: Tie the laces in a knot to secure them in place. You're done!

Q: How many eyelets should my shoes have?

Q: How tight should I tie my laces?
