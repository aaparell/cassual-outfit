Question and Answer

What size should I order when buying faux leather leggings?

It's important to check the sizing chart for each brand before purchasing faux leather leggings. Some brands may run small or large, so be sure to measure yourself and consult the sizing chart to ensure a proper fit.

Can I dress faux leather leggings up or down?

Yes! Faux leather leggings can be dressed up with a blouse or blazer for a dressy look, or dressed down with a t-shirt and sneakers for a more casual outfit.

Are faux leather leggings comfortable to wear?
