Types of Cloth Diapers

There are several types of cloth diapers available in India, each with its own set of benefits and drawbacks. The most common types include:

Flat diapers: These diapers are the most basic and affordable option. They're made from a single layer of cotton or bamboo and require folding to fit your baby.

Prefold diapers: These diapers are similar to flat diapers but have added layers in the middle for extra absorbency. They also require folding to fit your baby.

Fitted diapers: These diapers are shaped like disposable diapers and have elastic around the legs and waist for a snug fit. They also have added absorbency in the middle.

Pocket diapers: These diapers have a pocket between the outer waterproof layer and the inner absorbent layer where you can insert an absorbent pad. They're easy to use and adjust, making them a popular choice for parents.

All-in-one diapers: These diapers are the most convenient option as they have everything you need in one diaper. They have an outer waterproof layer, an absorbent inner layer, and a lining that touches your baby's skin.

How to Choose the Right Cloth Diaper for Your Baby

Choosing the right cloth diaper for your baby depends on several factors, including your budget, your baby's size and shape, and your personal preferences. Consider the following:

Budget: Cloth diapers can be a significant investment upfront, but they save you money in the long run. Consider how many diapers you'll need and how much you're willing to spend.

Size and shape: Cloth diapers come in different sizes and shapes to fit your baby's needs. Consider your baby's weight and shape when choosing a diaper.

Material: Cloth diapers are made from various materials, including cotton, bamboo, and microfiber. Consider the absorbency, softness, and durability of the material.
