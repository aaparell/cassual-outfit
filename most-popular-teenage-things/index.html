Social media is one of the most popular teenage things. Teenagers use it to communicate with their friends and peers, share photos and videos, and express themselves. Social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat are among the most popular among teenagers. Instagram is a photo-sharing app that allows users to post photos and videos, and it also has a messaging feature. TikTok is a video-sharing app that allows users to create short videos with music and effects. Snapchat is a messaging app that allows users to send photos and videos that disappear after being viewed.
