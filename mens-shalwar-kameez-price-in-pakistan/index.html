Mens Shalwar Kameez Price In Pakistan A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and comfortable outfit that reflects your cultural roots? Look no further than the mens shalwar kameez, a traditional Pakistani dress that is both fashionable and practical. However, one of the biggest concerns for buyers is the price of these outfits. In this guide, we will explore mens shalwar kameez price in Pakistan and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed purchase decision.

When it comes to buying mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan, the price can vary widely depending on multiple factors such as the quality of the fabric, the intricacy of the embroidery, and the reputation of the designer. Additionally, the price can also vary depending on the region in which you are shopping. For example, mens shalwar kameez can be pricier in urban areas like Lahore and Karachi compared to rural areas.

Overall, you can expect to pay anywhere from PKR 1,500 to PKR 50,000 for a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan. This wide range of prices can make it difficult to narrow down your options, but it's important to remember that a higher price tag doesn't always equate to better quality. It's important to do your research and shop around to find the best deals.

The Factors Affecting Mens Shalwar Kameez Price in Pakistan

As mentioned earlier, several factors can impact the price of mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan. Here are some of the most significant factors:

1. Fabric Quality

The quality of the fabric used in making the shalwar kameez is a huge factor that can determine its price. Generally, higher-quality fabrics such as silk and chiffon will be more expensive than cotton or lawn. Additionally, the type of embroidery or embellishment used on the fabric can also impact the price.

2. Designer Brand

The designer brand is another factor that can affect the price of mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan. If you opt for a more well-known designer brand, you can expect to pay a premium price.

3. Region

The region in which you are shopping can also impact the price of mens shalwar kameez. For example, if you shop in a more urban area, such as Lahore or Karachi, you can expect higher prices than if you shop in a rural area.

4. Customization

If you choose to have your mens shalwar kameez customized, this can also impact the price. Customizations such as embroidery, fitting, and design changes can add to the overall cost of the outfit.

Personal Experience with Mens Shalwar Kameez Price in Pakistan

As a Pakistani native, I have purchased several mens shalwar kameez outfits over the years. While the price can vary widely, I have found that the best way to get a good deal is to shop around and compare prices. Additionally, I have found that purchasing from smaller, less well-known designers can sometimes result in a better price without sacrificing quality.

Tips for Buying Mens Shalwar Kameez in Pakistan

1. Do your research on the designer and the fabric quality before making a purchase.

2. Shop around and compare prices from different stores and designers to get the best deal.

3. Consider purchasing from smaller, less well-known designers to save money without sacrificing quality.

4. If you are on a tight budget, consider purchasing a ready-to-wear shalwar kameez rather than a customized one.

Question and Answer

Q1: Is it better to purchase a ready-to-wear or customized mens shalwar kameez?

A1: It depends on your budget and personal preferences. Ready-to-wear shalwar kameez outfits can be more affordable, but customized outfits allow for more personalization and unique design options.

Q2: Can you negotiate the price of a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan?

A2: In some cases, you may be able to negotiate the price of a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan, especially if you are purchasing from a smaller, less well-known designer. However, it's important to be respectful and courteous when negotiating.

Q3: What is the average price range for a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan?

A3: The average price range for a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan is between PKR 1,500 and PKR 50,000, but prices can vary depending on several factors such as fabric quality, designer brand, and customization options.

Q4: How do I know if I am getting a good deal on a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan?

A4: The best way to know if you are getting a good deal on a mens shalwar kameez in Pakistan is to do your research and compare prices from multiple stores and designers. Additionally, make sure to consider factors such as fabric quality, designer brand, and customization options when making your purchase decision.

Conclusion of Mens Shalwar Kameez Price in Pakistan

In conclusion, mens shalwar kameez price in Pakistan can vary widely depending on several factors such as fabric quality, designer brand, and region. By doing your research, shopping around, and considering different designers and customization options, you can find the perfect mens shalwar kameez outfit without breaking the bank.
