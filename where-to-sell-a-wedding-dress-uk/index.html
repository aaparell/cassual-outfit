Where to Sell a Wedding Dress UK

There are various platforms where you can sell your wedding dress in the UK. Some of the popular ones include:

1. Online Marketplaces

Online marketplaces like eBay, Preloved, and Gumtree are great platforms to sell your wedding dress. They have a large audience and offer a range of options to list your dress. You can also set your price and negotiate with potential buyers.

2. Specialist Bridal Shops

You can also sell your wedding dress through specialist bridal shops. They have a dedicated audience looking for wedding dresses and can offer a professional service to sell your dress. However, they may take a commission on the sale.

3. Facebook Marketplace and Groups

Facebook Marketplace and Groups are also great places to sell your wedding dress. You can reach a targeted audience and sell your dress locally. You can also join specific groups dedicated to wedding dresses and showcase your dress to potential buyers.

4. Wedding Dress Resale Sites

There are various wedding dress resale sites like Still White and Sell My Wedding Dress that are dedicated to selling pre-owned wedding dresses. They offer a secure platform to list your dress and connect with potential buyers. However, they may take a commission on the sale.

When selling your wedding dress, remember to take clear pictures of your dress, provide accurate measurements, and be honest about any wear and tear. This will help you get the best value for your dress and ensure a smooth transaction.

Tips for Selling a Wedding Dress in the UK

Here are a few tips to help you sell your wedding dress in the UK:

1. Price Your Dress Competitively

Research the market and price your dress competitively. Consider the condition of your dress, the designer, and the original price when setting your price.

2. Take Clear Pictures

Take clear pictures of your dress, both front and back, and include any details like the train and embellishments. This will help potential buyers get a better idea of your dress and increase the chances of a sale.

3. Be Honest About Any Wear and Tear

Be honest about any wear and tear on your dress, including any stains, rips, or alterations. This will avoid any disappointment from the buyer and ensure a smooth transaction.

4. Use a Secure Payment System

Use a secure payment system like PayPal or Escrow to ensure a smooth transaction and protect yourself from any fraud.

Question and Answer

Q: How much commission do specialist bridal shops and wedding dress resale sites take?

A: It varies depending on the platform, but it can range from 10-30% commission on the sale.

Q: Can I negotiate the price with potential buyers on online marketplaces?

A: Yes, you can set your price and negotiate with potential buyers on online marketplaces like eBay and Gumtree.

Q: Can I sell my wedding dress if it has been altered?

A: Yes, you can sell your wedding dress if it has been altered. Just make sure to include accurate measurements and be honest about any alterations.

Q: How long does it take to sell a wedding dress on average?

A: It varies depending on the platform and the demand for your dress, but it can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to sell your wedding dress.
