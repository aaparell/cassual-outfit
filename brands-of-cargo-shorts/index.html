Dickies

Dickies is a brand that has been around for nearly 100 years and has a reputation for creating high-quality workwear. Their cargo shorts are no exception as they are made with durable materials that can withstand even the toughest jobs. The shorts are designed to be comfortable and functional with multiple pockets that can hold all your essentials. I personally own a pair of Dickies cargo shorts and love how they fit and feel. They are perfect for outdoor activities and have held up well over time.

Columbia Sportswear

Columbia Sportswear is a brand that is known for creating high-performance clothing that can handle any outdoor activity. Their cargo shorts are no exception as they are made with moisture-wicking fabric that will keep you cool and dry all day long. The shorts also have multiple pockets that are perfect for holding all your gear. I have worn Columbia Sportswear cargo shorts on hikes and camping trips and have been impressed with how comfortable and functional they are.

Carhartt

Carhartt is a brand that is known for creating rugged workwear that is built to last. Their cargo shorts are no exception as they are made with heavyweight cotton that is both comfortable and durable. The shorts also have multiple pockets that are perfect for holding all your tools and gear. If you're looking for a pair of cargo shorts that can handle even the toughest jobs, Carhartt is a great brand to consider.

How to Choose the Right Pair of Cargo Shorts

When it comes to choosing the right pair of cargo shorts, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your needs and what activities you'll be wearing the shorts for. If you'll be doing a lot of outdoor activities, look for shorts that are made with moisture-wicking fabric and have multiple pockets for holding all your gear. Second, consider the fit and style of the shorts. Look for shorts that fit well and have a style that matches your personal taste. Finally, consider the quality of the materials and construction. Look for shorts that are made with high-quality materials and are built to last.

Question and Answer

Q: Can cargo shorts be worn in a business casual setting?

A: It depends on the dress code of the company, but in general, cargo shorts are not considered appropriate for a business casual setting.

Q: Can cargo shorts be dressed up?

A: While cargo shorts are typically a more casual style, they can be dressed up with the right accessories and clothing. Pair them with a nice shirt and shoes for a more polished look.

Q: How many pockets should cargo shorts have?

A: Cargo shorts typically have multiple pockets, ranging from four to six. The number of pockets you need depends on your personal preferences and what you'll be using the shorts for.

Q: Can cargo shorts be worn in the winter?

A: Cargo shorts are typically a summer style, but they can be worn in the winter with the right layers. Pair them with a sweater and boots for a cozy winter look.

Conclusion of Brands of Cargo Shorts

When it comes to choosing the right pair of cargo shorts, there are many factors to consider. From the fit and style to the quality of the materials, finding the right pair of cargo shorts can be a challenge. However, by considering the top brands of cargo shorts on the market and what sets them apart, you can make an informed decision and find a pair of shorts that will keep you comfortable and stylish all season long.
