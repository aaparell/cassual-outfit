Where To Buy Vintage Style Wedding Dresses A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Nov 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a bride-to-be looking for a unique and timeless wedding dress? Vintage style wedding dresses may be just what you're looking for! With their delicate lace, intricate beading, and timeless silhouettes, vintage style wedding dresses can add a touch of old-world glamour to your special day. But where can you find these beautiful dresses? In this guide, we'll explore the best places to buy vintage style wedding dresses.

Searching for the perfect vintage style wedding dress can be overwhelming. You may not know where to start, and you may have concerns about finding a dress that fits your budget and style preferences. We understand the pain points associated with this search, but don't worry - we've got you covered.

So, where can you buy vintage style wedding dresses? There are several options to consider, including online retailers, vintage shops, and bridal boutiques. Let's take a closer look at each option.

Overall, when looking to buy vintage style wedding dresses, you have three main options: online retailers, vintage shops, and bridal boutiques. Each option has its own benefits and drawbacks, but by exploring them all, you're sure to find the perfect dress for your special day.

Online Retailers

Online retailers such as Etsy, BHLDN, and ModCloth offer a wide range of vintage style wedding dresses at a variety of price points. One of the benefits of shopping online is that you can browse a large selection of dresses from the comfort of your own home. Plus, many online retailers offer customization options, so you can tailor your dress to your specific preferences.

Personally, I found my dream vintage style wedding dress on Etsy. The seller was able to customize the dress to my measurements and style preferences, and I was thrilled with the end result. Just be sure to read reviews and check return policies before making a purchase.

Vintage Shops

If you're looking for an authentic vintage dress, a vintage shop may be the way to go. Vintage shops offer a curated selection of vintage dresses from different eras, so you can find a dress that truly speaks to you. Plus, you'll be able to try on dresses in person and get a sense of how they fit and feel.

I had a great experience at a vintage shop in my hometown. The owner was knowledgeable about different eras and was able to help me find a dress that fit my style and budget.

Bridal Boutiques

Finally, bridal boutiques often carry vintage inspired dresses that combine modern and vintage elements. These dresses can be a great option if you're looking for a dress that has a vintage feel but is still brand new. Plus, you'll be able to work with a consultant to find a dress that fits your specific style and body type.

While I didn't end up finding my dress at a bridal boutique, I did have a great experience trying on dresses and getting a sense of what I liked and didn't like.

Things to Consider When Buying a Vintage Style Wedding Dress

No matter where you decide to buy your vintage style wedding dress, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, vintage dresses may require alterations to fit properly. Be sure to budget for alterations when considering the overall cost of the dress.

Additionally, vintage dresses may have wear and tear due to their age. Be sure to inspect the dress carefully before making a purchase, and don't be afraid to ask questions about its condition.

Tips for Shopping for a Vintage Style Wedding Dress

Finally, here are a few tips to keep in mind when shopping for a vintage style wedding dress:

  • Do your research before making a purchase
  • Read reviews and check return policies
  • Try on dresses in person if possible
  • Be open to customization options
  • Consider the overall cost, including alterations

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can vintage style wedding dresses be altered?

A: Yes, vintage style wedding dresses can be altered to fit your body properly. Be sure to budget for alterations when considering the overall cost of the dress.

Q: How do I know if a vintage dress is in good condition?

A: Be sure to inspect the dress carefully before making a purchase, and don't be afraid to ask questions about its condition. Look for any signs of wear and tear and ask about any repairs that have been made.

Q: How do I find a vintage shop near me?

A: You can search online for vintage shops in your area, or check with local antique stores and consignment shops.

Q: Can I return a vintage style wedding dress?

A: It depends on the seller's return policy. Be sure to check the policy before making a purchase.

Conclusion

Overall, buying a vintage style wedding dress can be a great way to add a unique and timeless touch to your special day. Whether you choose to shop online, at a vintage shop, or at a bridal boutique, be sure to do your research and consider all of your options. With a little patience and persistence, you're sure to find the perfect dress for your wedding day.
