Discover Unique Shopping In Indianapolis

Written by Eveline Jun 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old shopping experiences? Do you want to find something unique and special in Indianapolis? Look no further! Indianapolis offers a variety of shopping experiences that are sure to satisfy your desire for something different.

Many people struggle to find unique shopping experiences in Indianapolis. They often end up at the same big-box stores and malls, feeling disappointed and uninspired. But there are plenty of hidden gems in the city that offer truly unique items.

If you're looking for one-of-a-kind items and a shopping experience that is unlike any other, Indianapolis has plenty to offer. From vintage clothing to handmade crafts, there are shops that cater to every taste and interest.

In this article, we'll explore some of the best places to find unique shopping experiences in Indianapolis. We'll cover everything from boutiques to markets, and give you tips on how to make the most of your shopping trip.

Discovering Broad Ripple Village

Broad Ripple Village is a neighborhood in Indianapolis that is known for its eclectic mix of shops, restaurants, and bars. It's a great place to spend an afternoon or evening, browsing the many unique shops that line the streets.

One of the best shops in Broad Ripple is Homespun: Modern Handmade. This store features handmade goods from over 300 artists and makers from around the country. You'll find everything from jewelry and clothing to home decor and paper goods.

If you're looking for vintage clothing, check out Rag-O-Rama. This store offers a huge selection of gently used clothing and accessories, from vintage band t-shirts to designer handbags.

Exploring the Indianapolis City Market

The Indianapolis City Market is a historic landmark that has been serving the city since 1886. Today, it is a hub for local food and culture, with dozens of vendors offering a variety of unique items.

One of the best shops at the Indianapolis City Market is Circle City Sweets. This bakery offers a variety of delicious treats, including handcrafted macarons and artisanal breads.

If you're looking for unique gifts, check out Silver in the City. This shop offers a variety of quirky and fun items, including jewelry, home decor, and accessories.

Visiting Mass Ave

Mass Ave is a cultural district in downtown Indianapolis that offers a variety of unique shopping experiences. It's a great place to find handmade goods, vintage clothing, and local art.

If you're a fan of vintage clothing, check out Retro 101. This store offers a huge selection of vintage clothing and accessories, from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge.

For handmade goods, visit Silver in the City's sister store, Decorate. This shop offers a variety of home decor items, including handmade pottery and unique wall art.

Tips for Making the Most of Your Shopping Trip

When you're shopping for unique items in Indianapolis, it's important to plan ahead. Make a list of the shops you want to visit and the items you're looking for. Many of the best shops are tucked away in neighborhoods, so be sure to bring a map or use your GPS.

It's also a good idea to bring cash, as many of the smaller shops may not accept credit cards. And don't be afraid to ask the shop owners for recommendations – they are often the best source of information about the unique items in their store.
