Who Is The Greatest Female Fighter Of All Time

Written by Frank Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered who is the greatest female fighter in history? It's a tough question to answer because there have been so many talented fighters throughout the years. Whether you're a fan of boxing, MMA, kickboxing, or any other combat sport, there have been some amazing female athletes who have dominated their divisions.

When it comes to determining the greatest female fighter of all time, there are a few factors to consider. These include the fighter's record, the level of competition they faced, their championship reigns, and their overall impact on the sport.

The title of greatest female fighter of all time is a highly debated topic, but many experts and fans agree that the answer is none other than the legendary mixed martial artist, Amanda Nunes.

So why is Amanda Nunes considered the greatest female fighter of all time? Let's take a closer look at her career and accomplishments.

The Dominance of Amanda Nunes

As of 2021, Amanda Nunes has a professional record of 21 wins and 4 losses. She is a two-division champion in the UFC, holding titles in both the bantamweight and featherweight divisions. She has also held titles in Strikeforce and Invicta FC.

Nunes has defeated some of the biggest names in women's MMA, including Ronda Rousey, Miesha Tate, Cris Cyborg, Valentina Shevchenko, and Holly Holm. Her victories over Cyborg and Rousey are particularly impressive, as both fighters were considered unbeatable at the time.

Another reason why Amanda Nunes is considered the greatest female fighter of all time is her longevity. She has been competing at the highest level for over a decade and shows no signs of slowing down. Her ability to stay on top of the sport for so long is a testament to her skill and dedication.

The Legacy of Amanda Nunes

Amanda Nunes' impact on women's MMA cannot be overstated. She has shattered many barriers and proven that female fighters can be just as exciting and talented as their male counterparts.

Her victories over Ronda Rousey and Cris Cyborg helped to bring more attention to women's MMA and showed that female fighters can be legitimate superstars. Her success has also inspired a new generation of female fighters who are looking to follow in her footsteps.

What Makes Amanda Nunes the Greatest Female Fighter of All Time?

There are several factors that contribute to Amanda Nunes' status as the greatest female fighter of all time. These include her record, the level of competition she has faced, her championship reigns, and her impact on the sport.

However, what truly sets Nunes apart is her ability to finish fights. Of her 21 wins, 14 have come by way of knockout or submission. Her finishing rate is one of the highest in women's MMA history and is a testament to her incredible power and skill.

How to Watch Amanda Nunes in Action

If you're interested in watching Amanda Nunes in action, you can catch her fights on UFC pay-per-view events. She is scheduled to defend her featherweight title against Megan Anderson on March 6, 2021.

The Future of Women's MMA

As women's MMA continues to grow and evolve, it's likely that we will see new fighters emerge who can challenge Amanda Nunes for the title of greatest female fighter of all time. However, for now, Nunes' dominance and impact on the sport make her the clear frontrunner for the title.
