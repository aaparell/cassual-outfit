Discover The Best Clothing Brands In Mexico A Guide To Relaxed Style

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to embrace a more relaxed and effortless style in your wardrobe? Perhaps you're tired of fast fashion and want to support brands that prioritize quality and sustainability. Whatever your reason, exploring the best clothing brands in Mexico is a great place to start.

Mexico has a rich and diverse fashion scene, with brands that range from high-end designer labels to affordable streetwear. However, navigating this market can be challenging, particularly for non-Spanish speakers. That's why we've put together this guide to help you discover the top clothing brands in Mexico.

In this article, we'll cover the most popular clothing brands in Mexico, from established names like Pineda Covalin and Yakampot to rising stars like Lorena Saravia and Kris Goyri. We'll also highlight the unique qualities that make each brand stand out, such as their use of traditional textiles, contemporary designs, and ethical production practices.

Top Clothing Brands in Mexico

One of the best things about Mexican fashion is its diversity. From vibrant colors and bold prints to minimalist designs and neutral palettes, there's something for everyone. Here are some of the best clothing brands in Mexico you should know about:

1. Pineda Covalin

Pineda Covalin is a luxury fashion brand that celebrates Mexican culture through its use of traditional textiles and motifs. Founded in 1996 by Cristina Pineda and Ricardo Covalin, the brand has become known for its silk scarves, ties, and accessories that feature intricate designs inspired by pre-Hispanic art, flora, and fauna. Pineda Covalin has stores throughout Mexico and in cities like New York, Paris, and Tokyo.

2. Yakampot

Yakampot is a contemporary fashion brand that combines modern design with traditional Mexican craftsmanship. Founded in 2011 by Francisco Cancino and Constanza von Niederhäusern, the brand uses natural fabrics and dyes to create minimalist yet bold pieces that are both timeless and sustainable. Yakampot has been featured in Vogue, Elle, and other international publications.

3. Lorena Saravia

Lorena Saravia is a fashion designer who has become one of the most recognized names in Mexican fashion. Her eponymous brand offers a range of clothing and accessories that blend elegance with ease, from tailored blazers and dresses to oversized knitwear and graphic tees. Saravia is also committed to sustainability and ethical production practices, using eco-friendly materials and supporting local artisans.

4. Kris Goyri

Kris Goyri is a rising star in the Mexican fashion scene, known for his avant-garde designs and playful use of color and texture. His brand offers a mix of high-end and streetwear pieces that are both edgy and wearable. Goyri has collaborated with brands like Nike and Coca-Cola and has shown his collections at New York Fashion Week.

The Unique Qualities of Mexican Fashion

What sets Mexican fashion apart from other markets is its fusion of tradition and innovation. Many Mexican designers use traditional textiles and techniques, such as embroidery, weaving, and beading, to create contemporary pieces that showcase the country's cultural heritage. This approach not only honors Mexico's rich history but also supports local artisans and promotes sustainable practices.

Another distinguishing feature of Mexican fashion is its playfulness and colorfulness. Mexican designers are not afraid to experiment with bold prints, bright hues, and eclectic textures, creating pieces that exude joy and vitality. This spirit of creativity and experimentation is what makes Mexican fashion so unique and exciting.

How to Shop for Mexican Fashion

If you're interested in exploring Mexican fashion, there are several ways to do so. One option is to visit the country and go shopping in its major cities, such as Mexico City, Guadalajara, and Monterrey. Here, you'll find a diverse range of boutiques and department stores that offer a mix of established and emerging brands.

Another option is to shop online, either through the brands' official websites or through e-commerce platforms that specialize in Mexican fashion, such as Yakampot, Fashion Compassion, and The Folklore. Shopping online allows you to access a wider selection of brands and styles, as well as compare prices and read reviews from other customers.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some of the most popular traditional textiles used in Mexican fashion?

A: Some of the most popular traditional textiles in Mexican fashion include Oaxacan embroidery, Chiapas weaving, and Jalisco beading. These techniques have been passed down through generations and are celebrated for their intricate designs and vibrant colors.

Q: How do Mexican fashion brands prioritize sustainability?

A: Many Mexican fashion brands prioritize sustainability by using natural and eco-friendly materials, supporting local artisans, and promoting ethical production practices. Some brands also focus on upcycling and recycling, transforming waste materials into new designs.

Q: What is the price range for Mexican fashion brands?

A: The price range for Mexican fashion brands varies widely, depending on the brand, the type of clothing or accessory, and the materials used. Some brands, such as Pineda Covalin and Yakampot, are considered luxury brands and have higher price points, while others, such as Kris Goyri and Lorena Saravia, offer more affordable options.

Q: How can I incorporate Mexican fashion into my wardrobe?

A: There are many ways to incorporate Mexican fashion into your wardrobe, depending on your personal style and preferences. You can start by adding a statement piece, such as a colorful scarf or embroidered blouse, to a neutral outfit. You can also experiment with mixing prints and textures, or layering different pieces for a more eclectic look.

Conclusion of Best Clothing Brands in Mexico

Exploring the best clothing brands in Mexico is a great way to discover new styles and support sustainable fashion. Whether you're drawn to traditional textiles, contemporary designs, or a mix of both, Mexican fashion offers something for everyone. By shopping from these brands, you're not only investing in your wardrobe but also in the future of fashion.
