Best Sustainable Clothing Brands For Men

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of buying fast fashion items that fall apart after a few wears? Are you concerned about the impact of your clothing choices on the environment and on the people who make them? If so, you may be interested in exploring the world of sustainable clothing.

Sustainable clothing brands for men are becoming increasingly popular as people become more aware of the negative impact of fast fashion on the environment and on workers in the garment industry. These brands offer high-quality, durable clothing made from sustainable materials and produced in ethical and environmentally-friendly ways.

If you're looking for the best sustainable clothing brands for men, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll introduce you to some of the top brands in this space and explain what makes them special.

Patagonia

Patagonia is one of the most well-known sustainable clothing brands for men. They offer a wide range of outdoor clothing made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and hemp. They are also committed to using environmentally-friendly production processes and reducing their carbon footprint.

Personally, I have been a fan of Patagonia for years. Their clothing is durable, comfortable, and stylish. I feel good knowing that I am supporting a company that is doing its part to protect the planet.

Outerknown

Outerknown is a sustainable clothing brand founded by professional surfer Kelly Slater. They offer a range of men's clothing made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and linen. They also focus on transparency and ethical production practices.

I recently purchased a shirt from Outerknown and was impressed by the quality and attention to detail. It's clear that this brand cares about the environment and the people who make their clothes.

Organic Basics

Organic Basics is a sustainable clothing brand that focuses on basics such as t-shirts, underwear, and socks. They use sustainable materials such as organic cotton, Tencel, and recycled nylon, and they are committed to ethical production practices.

I appreciate the simplicity and versatility of Organic Basics' clothing. Their t-shirts are some of the softest and most comfortable I've ever worn, and I feel good knowing that they are made in an environmentally-friendly and ethical way.

Everlane

Everlane is a sustainable clothing brand that focuses on transparency and ethical production practices. They offer a range of men's clothing made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and Tencel. They also provide information on the factories where their clothes are made and the cost breakdown of each item.

I appreciate the transparency and honesty of Everlane's approach. It's refreshing to know exactly where my clothes are coming from and how much each part of the production process costs.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some other sustainable clothing brands for men?

A: Some other sustainable clothing brands for men include Pact, Prana, and Nudie Jeans.

Q: Is sustainable clothing more expensive than fast fashion?

A: Sustainable clothing can be more expensive than fast fashion, but it's important to remember that you are paying for higher quality, longer-lasting clothing that is made in an environmentally-friendly and ethical way.

Q: How can I make my existing wardrobe more sustainable?

A: You can make your existing wardrobe more sustainable by buying secondhand clothing, repairing clothes instead of throwing them away, and choosing clothes made from sustainable materials.

Q: Why is sustainable clothing important?

A: Sustainable clothing is important because it helps to reduce the negative impact of the fashion industry on the environment and on workers in the garment industry. By choosing sustainable clothing, you are supporting a more ethical and environmentally-friendly way of producing and consuming clothing.

Conclusion of Best Sustainable Clothing Brands for Men

If you're looking to make more sustainable choices when it comes to your clothing, there are a number of great sustainable clothing brands for men to choose from. Brands like Patagonia, Outerknown, Organic Basics, and Everlane are leading the way in sustainable fashion and offer high-quality, durable clothing made from sustainable materials and produced in ethical and environmentally-friendly ways.

By supporting these brands, you can feel good knowing that you are doing your part to protect the planet and support ethical production practices.
