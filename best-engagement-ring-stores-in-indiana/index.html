What to Look for When Shopping for an Engagement Ring

When shopping for an engagement ring, there are a few things you should keep in mind to ensure that you find the perfect ring that fits your budget and style preferences. You should also consider the style of the ring, including the type of metal, the setting, and the shape of the diamond. Think about your partner's style preferences and what type of ring they would like to wear for the rest of their life.
