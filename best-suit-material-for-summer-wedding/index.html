Best Suit Material For Summer Weddings Stay Cool And Stylish

Written by Frank Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Summer weddings are a great way to celebrate love and joy in the warm months. However, dressing for a summer wedding can be a challenge, especially if you want to look stylish and stay cool at the same time. Choosing the right suit material can make all the difference, and in this post, we'll explore the best suit materials for summer weddings.

When it comes to summer weddings, one of the biggest pain points is finding a suit that keeps you cool and comfortable in the heat. You don't want to be sweating and feeling uncomfortable throughout the entire ceremony and reception. Additionally, you want to look your best and stand out in the sea of guests.

The target of this post is to explore and recommend the best suit materials for summer weddings. We'll cover the materials that are breathable, lightweight, and stylish, so you can look and feel your best on the big day.

In summary, the best suit materials for summer weddings are those that are lightweight, breathable, and comfortable. They should be able to keep you cool in the heat while also being stylish and appropriate for the occasion.

Linen Suits

One of the best suit materials for summer weddings is linen. Linen is a lightweight and breathable fabric that is perfect for warm weather. It has a natural texture that adds a touch of elegance to any outfit, and it comes in various colors and patterns to suit your style and taste.

Personally, I wore a linen suit to my friend's beach wedding, and it was perfect for the occasion. It kept me cool and comfortable in the heat, and I received many compliments on the stylish and laid-back look.

If you decide to wear a linen suit to a summer wedding, keep in mind that it wrinkles easily. However, this is part of the fabric's charm and adds to the relaxed, casual vibe.

Cotton Suits

Cotton is another excellent suit material choice for summer weddings. It's lightweight, breathable, and easy to care for, making it a practical option for warm weather. Cotton suits are available in a range of colors and patterns, from classic navy to bold prints, so you can find one that fits your style and personality.

For my cousin's outdoor wedding, I opted for a cotton suit in light gray. It was comfortable to wear all day long and looked sharp in photos. I paired it with a patterned tie and pocket square to add a pop of color and personality.

Seersucker Suits

Seersucker is a classic summer fabric that is perfect for weddings. It's lightweight, breathable, and has a distinctive texture that adds visual interest to any outfit. Seersucker suits come in different colors and patterns, from traditional blue and white stripes to bold hues like pink and green.

For my sister's garden wedding, I wore a seersucker suit in light blue. It was comfortable and stylish, and I received many compliments on the unique fabric. I paired it with a white shirt and brown leather shoes for a classic and timeless look.

Suit Material Maintenance Tips

Regardless of the suit material you choose, it's essential to take care of it properly to maintain its quality and longevity. Here are some basic maintenance tips:

  • Always follow the care instructions on the label.
  • Hang your suit on a wooden hanger to maintain its shape.
  • Use a steamer or iron on low heat to remove wrinkles.
  • Avoid dry cleaning too often, as it can be harsh on the fabric.

Conclusion of Best Suit Material for Summer Weddings

Choosing the right suit material for a summer wedding can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge and guidance, it can be a fun and enjoyable process. Linen, cotton, and seersucker are all great options that offer comfort, style, and breathability. Remember to take care of your suit properly to ensure it lasts for many summers to come.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a wool suit to a summer wedding?

A: While wool is a classic suit material, it's not the best choice for a summer wedding. Wool is heavier and less breathable than linen, cotton, or seersucker, which can make you feel hot and uncomfortable in the heat. If you must wear wool, opt for a lightweight and breathable wool blend instead.

Q: What colors should I choose for a summer wedding suit?

A: Lighter colors are ideal for summer weddings, as they reflect the sun's rays and keep you cool. Ivory, beige, light gray, and pastel shades like pink and blue are all great options. Avoid dark colors like black and navy, as they absorb heat and can make you feel uncomfortable.

Q: Can I wear a short-sleeved shirt with a suit to a summer wedding?

A: Short-sleeved shirts are not recommended for formal occasions like weddings. Instead, opt for a lightweight and breathable long-sleeved shirt in a light color. You can roll up the sleeves if you feel too warm.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a suit to a summer wedding?

A: Sandals are too casual for a wedding, even if it's in the summer. Instead, opt for dress shoes like loafers or oxfords in a light color. Make sure they're comfortable to wear all day long.
