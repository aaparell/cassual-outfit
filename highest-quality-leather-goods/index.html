How to Care for Your Highest Quality Leather Goods

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between full-grain leather and top-grain leather?

A: Full-grain leather is made from the top layer of the hide, which is the most durable and highest-quality part of the leather. Top-grain leather, on the other hand, is made from the second layer of the hide and is slightly less durable and lower in quality.

Q: How do I know if a leather product is well-made?

A: Look for products that have been crafted with care and attention to detail, with strong stitching and high-quality hardware. You should also pay attention to the overall design and construction of the product, looking for items that are designed to last.

Q: Can I clean my leather products with water?

A: It's generally not recommended to clean leather products with water, as this can cause the leather to dry out and crack. Instead, use a leather cleaner or conditioner to help remove dirt and moisturize the leather.

Q: How often should I condition my leather products?

A: This will depend on how often you use your leather products and how dry they become. As a general rule, you should condition your leather products every few months to keep them moisturized and looking their best.
