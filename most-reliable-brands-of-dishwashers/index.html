Energy Efficiency in Dishwashers

When it comes to dishwasher energy efficiency, there are a few things to keep in mind. Look for a dishwasher that has an Energy Star rating, which means it has been certified to be energy-efficient by the US Environmental Protection Agency. You should also look for a dishwasher with a delay start feature, which allows you to schedule when the dishwasher runs, so you can take advantage of off-peak energy rates.

Tips for Maintaining Your Dishwasher

To keep your dishwasher running smoothly, there are a few things you can do. First, make sure to clean the dishwasher filter regularly to prevent clogs.
