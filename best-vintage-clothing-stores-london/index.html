Discover The Best Vintage Clothing Stores In London

Written by Frank Sep 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a vintage fashion enthusiast looking for the best vintage clothing stores in London? Shopping for vintage clothes can be a daunting task, especially if you are new to the scene. You might not know where to start, what to look for, or how to differentiate between authentic vintage pieces and reproductions. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

In this post, we will guide you through the best vintage clothing stores in London, where you can find unique and high-quality vintage pieces that will add character and style to your wardrobe.

London is home to a vibrant vintage fashion scene, with a plethora of vintage stores scattered across the city. From vintage boutiques to flea markets and thrift stores, there's something for every vintage lover in London.

Here are some of the best vintage clothing stores in London:

1. Beyond Retro

I first discovered Beyond Retro when I was a student in London, and it quickly became my go-to vintage store. With multiple locations across the city, Beyond Retro is one of the largest and most popular vintage chains in London. The store offers a vast selection of vintage clothing, from the 1920s to the 1990s, for both men and women. The prices are reasonable, and the quality of the clothes is excellent.

2. Rokit

Rokit is another popular vintage chain in London, with several stores in different neighborhoods. The store has a vast collection of vintage clothing, shoes, and accessories, from the 1920s to the 1990s. What sets Rokit apart is its commitment to sustainability, as the store upcycles and reworks vintage pieces to give them a new lease of life. The prices are also affordable, and the staff are friendly and knowledgeable.

3. Camden Market

If you're looking for a unique vintage shopping experience, head to Camden Market. The market has several vintage stalls and shops selling everything from vintage clothing to antiques and collectibles. You can easily spend a whole day browsing through the different stalls and soaking up the vibrant atmosphere of Camden.

4. Blitz London

Blitz London is a massive vintage department store located in the trendy East End neighborhood of London. The store spans over two floors and offers a vast selection of vintage clothing, accessories, and homeware. The prices are slightly higher than other vintage stores, but the quality of the pieces is exceptional. The store also has a café where you can grab a coffee and recharge between shopping sprees.

What to Look for When Shopping for Vintage Clothing

When shopping for vintage clothing, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that you are getting a good deal:

1. Check the condition of the piece. Vintage clothing is often pre-worn, so it's essential to check for any signs of wear and tear, such as stains, holes, or fading.

2. Look for quality materials. Vintage clothing was often made from high-quality materials, such as silk, wool, and cotton, so make sure to check the fabric composition of the piece you're interested in.

3. Check the sizing. Sizing has changed over the years, so it's essential to try on the piece or check the measurements before purchasing.

4. Buy what you love. Vintage clothing is all about individuality, so don't be afraid to follow your heart and buy what you truly love.

How to Style Vintage Clothing

Styling vintage clothing can be challenging, but it's all about finding the right balance between retro and modern. Here are some tips to help you style vintage pieces:

1. Mix and match. Don't be afraid to mix and match vintage pieces with modern items to create a unique and personalized look.

2. Keep it simple. Vintage clothing is often bold and colorful, so it's essential to keep the rest of your outfit simple and understated to avoid looking too busy.

3. Accessorize. Vintage accessories, such as hats, scarves, and jewelry, can add a retro touch to any outfit.

Question and Answer

1. What is the best vintage clothing store in London?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as it depends on your personal style and preferences. However, some of the best vintage clothing stores in London include Beyond Retro, Rokit, Camden Market, and Blitz London.

2. How do I know if a vintage piece is authentic?

Authentic vintage pieces often have specific features, such as union labels, metal zippers, and hand-sewn finishes, that distinguish them from modern reproductions. It's also essential to do your research and educate yourself on the different eras and styles of vintage fashion.

3. How do I care for vintage clothing?

Vintage clothing requires special care to ensure its longevity. Always follow the care instructions on the garment's label, and avoid harsh detergents and washing machines. Store vintage clothing in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture.

4. Can I sell my vintage clothes to vintage stores?

Yes, many vintage stores buy vintage clothing from individuals. However, the store will likely only accept high-quality and desirable pieces that fit their current inventory.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Clothing Stores in London

London is a vintage lover's paradise, with a vast selection of vintage stores, markets, and boutiques to choose from. Whether you're a seasoned vintage shopper or a newbie, there's something for everyone in London's vintage scene. Remember to keep an open mind, follow your instincts, and have fun exploring the city's vintage treasures!
