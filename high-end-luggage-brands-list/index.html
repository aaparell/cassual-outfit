Why High End Luggage Brands Matter

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a frequent traveler, you understand the importance of having durable and reliable luggage that can withstand the wear and tear of constant travel. Investing in high end luggage brands can save you from the headache of constantly replacing your luggage due to wear and tear. However, with so many brands to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to buy.

Whether you're a business traveler or a leisure traveler, your luggage is an essential item for your trip. No one wants to deal with a broken zipper or a torn handle during a trip. High end luggage brands offer the best quality and craftsmanship to ensure that your luggage will last for years to come.

If you're looking for a high end luggage brand, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your budget. High end luggage can be expensive, so you'll want to make sure you're getting the best value for your money. Second, think about your travel needs. Do you need a carry-on or a larger suitcase? Do you want a hard or soft shell? These are important factors to consider when choosing a high end luggage brand.

In this article, we'll dive into some of the best high end luggage brands on the market and what makes them stand out. We'll also provide you with some tips on how to choose the right high end luggage brand for your travel needs.

The Best High End Luggage Brands List

One of the most popular high end luggage brands is Rimowa. The German brand is known for its sleek aluminum suitcases that are both durable and stylish. Their luggage is perfect for business travelers who want to make a statement. Another popular brand is Tumi. The American brand offers a wide range of luggage options, from carry-ons to large suitcases. Their luggage is made with high-quality materials and is designed to last.

Briggs & Riley is another high end luggage brand that is perfect for frequent travelers. Their luggage is designed with the traveler in mind, with features like expandable compartments and TSA locks. Samsonite is another popular brand that offers high quality luggage at an affordable price point. Their luggage is perfect for travelers who want a durable suitcase without breaking the bank.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a High End Luggage Brand

When choosing a high end luggage brand, there are a few key factors to consider. First, think about your travel needs. Do you need a carry-on or a larger suitcase? Do you want a hard or soft shell?

Second, consider your budget. High end luggage can be expensive, so it's important to choose a brand that offers the best value for your money. Look for brands that offer warranties or guarantees on their luggage.

Finally, consider the features that are important to you. Do you need a suitcase with an expandable compartment? Do you want a suitcase with a built-in charger for your devices? Think about the features that will make your travel experience easier and more enjoyable.

How to Care for Your High End Luggage

Investing in high end luggage is a big investment, so it's important to take care of your luggage to ensure that it lasts for years to come. One of the most important things you can do is to clean your luggage regularly. Use a mild soap and warm water to clean the exterior of your suitcase, and wipe down the interior with a damp cloth.

Another important tip is to store your luggage properly. Don't store your luggage in a damp or humid area, as this can cause mold or mildew to grow. Instead, store your luggage in a dry, cool place.
