Best Sustainable Mens Clothing Brands A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on fast fashion that doesn't last? Do you want to make a difference in the world by supporting sustainable fashion brands? Look no further than this comprehensive guide to the best sustainable mens clothing brands.

Today, many consumers are becoming more conscious about the impact of their purchases on the environment and the people who make them. Unfortunately, the fashion industry has been known for its contribution to climate change, labor abuses, and waste. This is where sustainable fashion comes in - it's a movement towards more ethical and eco-friendly clothing that benefits everyone involved in the supply chain.

If you're looking for high-quality clothing that's made with the environment and workers in mind, sustainable menswear is the way to go. In this guide, we'll cover the top brands that prioritize sustainability without sacrificing style or quality.

Patagonia

Patagonia is a well-known brand that has been leading the way in sustainable fashion for decades. They prioritize using organic cotton, recycled materials, and fair labor practices in their production processes. Their clothing is designed to last for years, reducing the need for constant replacements and waste. I personally love their fleece jackets, which are made from recycled plastic bottles and keep me warm on outdoor adventures.

Outerknown

Outerknown is a newer brand founded by pro surfer Kelly Slater. They focus on creating timeless, high-quality clothing that's made with sustainable materials like organic cotton, hemp, and recycled polyester. I've purchased a few t-shirts from Outerknown and they're incredibly comfortable and durable - I know they'll last me for years to come.

Everlane

Everlane is a brand that prides itself on transparency and ethical production practices. They offer a wide range of men's clothing, from basic t-shirts to dress shoes, all made with a focus on sustainability and quality. I appreciate that they provide a breakdown of the costs that go into making each item, so I know exactly what I'm paying for and where my money is going.

Organic Basics

Organic Basics is a Danish brand that's committed to creating sustainable underwear and basics for men. They use organic cotton, recycled materials, and ethical production practices to create comfortable and stylish clothing that's good for you and the planet. I've tried their boxers and socks and they're some of the softest and most comfortable I've ever worn.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are sustainable menswear brands more expensive than fast fashion brands?

A: Yes, sustainable fashion can be more expensive upfront than fast fashion. However, investing in high-quality clothing that's made to last can save you money in the long run by reducing the need for constant replacements.

Q: How can I tell if a brand is sustainable?

A: Look for brands that prioritize using sustainable materials like organic cotton, hemp, and recycled polyester. Also, check if they have certifications from organizations like Fair Trade or Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) that ensure ethical production practices.

Q: What can I do with my old clothing?

A: Instead of throwing away your old clothing, consider donating it to a secondhand store or a charity. You can also repurpose it into rags or cleaning cloths or recycle it through programs like H&M's garment collection.

Q: Can sustainable fashion be stylish?

A: Absolutely! Sustainable fashion brands prioritize both style and sustainability, so you don't have to sacrifice one for the other. Many sustainable brands offer trendy and classic styles that cater to a variety of tastes.

Conclusion of Best Sustainable Mens Clothing Brands

Sustainable menswear is a movement towards more ethical and eco-friendly clothing that benefits everyone involved in the supply chain. By supporting brands that prioritize sustainability, you can make a difference in the world and look good while doing it. The brands we've highlighted in this guide offer high-quality clothing that's made with the environment and workers in mind, so you can feel good about your purchases. Happy shopping!
