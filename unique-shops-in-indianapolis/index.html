Discover The Unique Shops Of Indianapolis A Guide

Written by Frank Jun 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of visiting the same old chain stores and malls? Do you crave a shopping experience that is more unique and personalized? Look no further than Indianapolis, where you can discover a variety of one-of-a-kind shops that offer everything from vintage clothing to handmade jewelry. In this guide, we will explore the best unique shops in Indianapolis and what makes them stand out.

If you're looking for a shopping experience that is outside of the box, Indianapolis has plenty of options to choose from. Whether you're a fashionista searching for the perfect statement piece or a collector of unique home decor, there's a shop in Indianapolis that caters to your needs. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

Our guide to the unique shops of Indianapolis is here to help. We've scoured the city to find the most interesting and one-of-a-kind shops, and we're excited to share them with you. Keep reading to discover the best places to shop in Indianapolis.

The Best Vintage Clothing Shops in Indianapolis

As the saying goes, everything old is new again. Vintage clothing has become increasingly popular in recent years, and Indianapolis has some of the best vintage shops in the Midwest. One of our favorites is Broad Ripple Vintage, located in the trendy Broad Ripple neighborhood. This shop offers a wide selection of vintage clothing and accessories, including funky 70s patterns and classic 90s grunge.

Another great option is Retro 101, which specializes in vintage clothing from the 1920s to the 1980s. You'll find everything from flapper dresses to disco jumpsuits at this charming shop, which is located in the historic Fountain Square neighborhood.

The Best Handmade Jewelry Shops in Indianapolis

If you're looking for a unique piece of jewelry, Indianapolis has several shops that specialize in handmade and artisanal pieces. Silver in the City is a local favorite, offering a variety of quirky and playful jewelry designs that are perfect for adding a pop of personality to any outfit.

Another great option is Homespun: Modern Handmade, which features a variety of handmade jewelry from local and national artisans. You'll find everything from delicate gold pieces to bold statement necklaces at this charming shop, which also offers a variety of other handmade goods.

The Best Bookstores in Indianapolis

For book lovers, Indianapolis has a variety of unique bookstores that offer more than just bestsellers. Bookmamas is a cozy and intimate shop that specializes in indie and self-published books, with a focus on women and minority authors. You'll find everything from poetry to cookbooks at this charming shop, which also hosts author events and book clubs.

Another great option is Indy Reads Books, which is more than just a bookstore. This non-profit shop supports adult literacy programs in Indianapolis, and all proceeds from book sales go towards this mission. You'll find a wide selection of new and used books at Indy Reads, as well as a variety of literary events and classes.

The Best Home Decor Shops in Indianapolis

If you're looking to spruce up your home, Indianapolis has a variety of unique home decor shops that offer everything from vintage furniture to handmade ceramics. Haus Love is a local favorite, offering a variety of stylish and eclectic home goods that are perfect for adding a touch of personality to any space.

Another great option is Homespun: Modern Handmade (yes, they do more than just jewelry!), which features a variety of handmade home decor items from local and national artisans. You'll find everything from quirky wall art to handmade candles at this charming shop, which also offers a variety of other handmade goods.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes the unique shops in Indianapolis stand out?

A: The unique shops in Indianapolis offer a personalized and one-of-a-kind shopping experience that is hard to find at chain stores and malls. These shops often specialize in handmade or artisanal goods, and many of them are located in charming and historic neighborhoods.

Q: Are the unique shops in Indianapolis expensive?

A: It depends on the shop and the item. Some shops may be more expensive than others, but many offer affordable options as well. One thing to keep in mind is that the items you'll find at these shops are often one-of-a-kind or handmade, so they may be a bit pricier than mass-produced items.

Q: Are the unique shops in Indianapolis easy to find?

A: Some of the unique shops in Indianapolis are located in trendy and popular neighborhoods, like Broad Ripple and Fountain Square, while others may be a bit more off the beaten path. However, with a little research and a sense of adventure, you're sure to discover some hidden gems.

Q: Can I find unique gifts at the shops in Indianapolis?

A: Absolutely! Many of the unique shops in Indianapolis offer a variety of gift options, from handmade jewelry to quirky home decor. These items make great gifts for birthdays, holidays, or just because.

Conclusion of Unique Shops in Indianapolis

Indianapolis may not be known as a shopping destination, but it should be. With a variety of unique shops that offer everything from vintage clothing to handmade home decor, there's something for everyone in this Midwestern city. So next time you're in Indianapolis, skip the mall and explore the charming and one-of-a-kind shops that make this city special.
