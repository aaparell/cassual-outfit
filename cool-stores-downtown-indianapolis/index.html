The Coolest Stores In Downtown Indianapolis

Written by Petter May 19, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of shopping at the same old stores and looking for something unique? Look no further than downtown Indianapolis! From trendy boutiques to vintage shops, downtown Indy has it all.

If you're looking for a unique shopping experience, downtown Indianapolis is the place to be. With a variety of stores, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for clothes, accessories, or home decor, you'll find it here.

So, what are some of the coolest stores in downtown Indianapolis? Let's take a look.

Crimson Tate

As a fabric store and sewing studio, Crimson Tate is a must-visit for any crafters out there. The store has a wide selection of fabrics, patterns, and notions, and also offers classes and workshops for all skill levels. Plus, the staff is always happy to help with any questions or project ideas.

Silver in the City

If you're looking for unique jewelry and gifts, Silver in the City is the place to go. The store features a variety of handmade and locally sourced items, including jewelry, home decor, and accessories. And with a quirky and fun atmosphere, shopping at Silver in the City is always a blast.

Indy CD & Vinyl

Music lovers will definitely want to check out Indy CD & Vinyl. The store carries a wide selection of new and used vinyl, CDs, and cassette tapes, as well as turntables and other music-related accessories. And with frequent in-store performances and events, Indy CD & Vinyl is more than just a record store.

The Best Chocolate in Town

If you have a sweet tooth, The Best Chocolate in Town is a must-visit. The store features handmade chocolates, truffles, and other confections, all made with high-quality ingredients. And with unique flavors like lavender and chili pepper, there's always something new to try.

Q&A

Q: What is the best time to visit downtown Indianapolis for shopping?

A: Downtown Indianapolis is a great place to shop year-round, but if you're looking for the best deals, visit during the holiday season or during one of the city's many festivals and events.

Q: Are the stores in downtown Indianapolis expensive?

A: It depends on the store, but there are stores in downtown Indianapolis for every budget. Some stores, like The Best Chocolate in Town, have affordable options, while others, like Silver in the City, have higher-end items.

Q: Is parking difficult in downtown Indianapolis?

A: Parking can be a challenge in downtown Indianapolis, but there are plenty of parking garages and lots available. Some stores also offer valet parking for an additional fee.

Q: Can I bring my dog into the stores in downtown Indianapolis?

A: It depends on the store, but many stores in downtown Indianapolis are dog-friendly. It's always a good idea to call ahead or check the store's website to be sure.

Conclusion

Downtown Indianapolis is home to some of the coolest stores around, and with a variety of options, there's something for everyone. So next time you're in the area, be sure to check out Crimson Tate, Silver in the City, Indy CD & Vinyl, and The Best Chocolate in Town for a shopping experience you won't forget.
