What Do Hindu Grooms Wear At Weddings

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Weddings are a big deal in Hindu culture, and grooms take their attire just as seriously as brides. If you're a Hindu groom-to-be, you might be wondering what to wear on your big day. In this post, we'll guide you through the various options and styles that Hindu grooms typically wear at weddings.

Planning a wedding can be stressful, and choosing the right outfit for your big day can add to the pressure. You want to look your best and feel comfortable at the same time. It's also essential to choose an outfit that complements your bride's attire. These factors can make the process overwhelming, but don't worry, we are here to help.

The Target of What Do Hindu Grooms Wear at Weddings

When it comes to choosing what to wear, Hindu grooms have a wide range of options. The outfit you select will depend on your personal style, the wedding theme, and cultural traditions. Some grooms prefer a traditional look, while others opt for a more modern style. Whatever your preference, here are some of the outfits that Hindu grooms typically wear at weddings:

Sherwani

The sherwani is a popular choice for Hindu grooms. It's a long coat that is usually worn with a churidar or dhoti. Sherwanis come in various colors and designs, from classic gold and red to more contemporary shades like blue and green. The embroidery work on the sherwani is usually intricate and detailed, giving it an elegant look.

Kurta Pyjama

Another traditional outfit choice for Hindu grooms is the kurta pyjama. It's a comfortable and relaxed outfit that is perfect for a daytime wedding. The kurta is a long shirt that is paired with loose-fitting pants called pyjamas. The kurta pyjama is usually made of silk or cotton and comes in various colors and designs.

Dhoti Kurta

The dhoti kurta is a classic Indian outfit that is often worn by Hindu grooms. This outfit consists of a long shirt and a dhoti, which is a long piece of cloth draped around the waist and legs. The dhoti kurta is usually made of silk or cotton, and the shirt can be plain or embroidered.

Turban

A turban is an essential accessory that completes the Hindu groom's look. It's a long piece of cloth that is wrapped around the head and comes in various colors and materials. The turban adds a touch of elegance to the groom's outfit and is usually matched to the bride's attire.

Conclusion of What Do Hindu Grooms Wear at Weddings

Choosing the right outfit for your wedding day is crucial, and as a Hindu groom, you have a wide range of options to choose from. Whether you opt for a traditional sherwani or a more relaxed kurta pyjama, make sure you feel comfortable and confident in your outfit. Don't forget to complete your look with a turban, which adds a touch of elegance to your attire.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a western suit for my Hindu wedding?

A: While it's not traditional, some Hindu grooms do choose to wear a western suit for their wedding day. However, if you want to respect cultural traditions, it's best to opt for a traditional outfit.

Q: Can I wear a sherwani for a daytime wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a sherwani for a daytime wedding. However, you might want to choose a lighter color and fabric to ensure you feel comfortable in the heat.

Q: Should I match my outfit to my bride's?

A: It's not necessary to match your outfit to your bride's, but it's a nice touch. You can coordinate your outfit with your bride's by choosing a similar color or embroidery work.

Q: Can I add accessories to my outfit?

A: Yes, you can add accessories to your outfit, such as a brooch or a watch. Just make sure they complement your attire and don't overpower it.
