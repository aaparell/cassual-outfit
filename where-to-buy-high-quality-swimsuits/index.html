Where To Buy High Quality Swimsuits

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is just around the corner and you're probably thinking about hitting the beach or lounging by the pool. However, finding the perfect swimsuit can be a daunting task. You want something that fits well, looks good, and won't fall apart after a few wears. But where do you even begin?

It's no secret that buying swimsuits can be stressful. Between finding the right size, style, and quality, the process can be overwhelming. Not to mention, there are countless options out there, making it difficult to know where to start.

The good news is that there are plenty of places where you can buy high quality swimsuits. From department stores to online retailers, there are a variety of options that cater to different styles, budgets, and body types.

When it comes to buying high quality swimsuits, it's important to consider a few things. Firstly, you want to make sure the material is durable and won't wear down easily. Secondly, you want to find a style that flatters your body type and makes you feel confident. Finally, you want to make sure you're getting your money's worth by investing in swimsuits that will last you multiple summers.

Department Stores

Department stores are a great place to start when looking for high quality swimsuits. Brands like Nordstrom, Bloomingdale's, and Macy's offer a wide range of styles and sizes, making it easy to find something that fits and flatters your body. Plus, department stores often have sales and promotions, which can help you save money on your purchase.

Personally, I've had great luck finding high quality swimsuits at Nordstrom. Their swimwear section is well-stocked and the sales associates are always helpful when it comes to finding the right size and style. Additionally, they offer a variety of brands, from high-end designers to more affordable options.

Online Retailers

If you prefer to shop from the comfort of your own home, there are plenty of online retailers that offer high quality swimsuits. Websites like Revolve, ASOS, and Zappos have a wide range of styles and sizes, and often offer free shipping and returns. Additionally, many online retailers have customer reviews, which can help you determine the quality and fit of a swimsuit before making a purchase.

One online retailer that I've had a great experience with is Revolve. They have a large selection of swimsuits from both established and up-and-coming designers, and their website is easy to navigate. Plus, they offer free shipping and returns, which makes it easy to try on multiple swimsuits and find the perfect one.

Swimwear Boutiques

If you're looking for something more unique and boutique-like, there are plenty of swimwear boutiques that offer high quality swimsuits. These stores often have a more curated selection of styles and brands, and can help you find something that fits your individual style.

One swimwear boutique that I've had a great experience with is Everything But Water. They have a variety of high-end brands and unique styles, and their sales associates are knowledgeable when it comes to finding the right fit. Additionally, they offer a variety of cover-ups and accessories, making it easy to put together a complete beach or poolside look.

Custom Swimsuits

For those who want something truly one-of-a-kind, there are plenty of options for custom swimsuits. These swimsuits are made to your exact measurements and specifications, ensuring a perfect fit and unique design.

One custom swimsuit company that I've heard great things about is Summersalt. They use 3D body scanning technology to create swimsuits that fit like a glove, and offer a variety of styles and colors. Additionally, they use sustainable materials in their swimsuits, making them a great option for those who are environmentally conscious.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I determine the quality of a swimsuit?
A: Look for swimsuits made from high-quality materials, such as nylon or spandex. Additionally, check the seams and stitching to make sure they're secure and won't unravel easily.

Q: Should I buy a swimsuit in my usual size?
A: It's important to try on swimsuits before buying, as sizes can vary between brands and styles. Additionally, consider buying multiple sizes so you can find the perfect fit.

Q: Can I find high quality swimsuits at discount stores?
A: While it's possible to find high quality swimsuits at discount stores, it can be hit or miss. It's important to check the quality and fit of the swimsuit before buying.

Q: How can I make my swimsuit last longer?
A: Rinse your swimsuit with cold water after each wear to remove chlorine and saltwater. Additionally, avoid wringing out your swimsuit and never put it in the dryer.

Conclusion of Where to Buy High Quality Swimsuits

When it comes to buying high quality swimsuits, there are plenty of options available. From department stores to online retailers to swimwear boutiques, there's something for everyone. By considering the quality, style, and fit of a swimsuit, you can find something that will last you multiple summers and make you feel confident and comfortable.
