Mens Sandals Brands List In India The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of browsing through countless websites and stores trying to find the perfect sandals for men? Do you want to know the best mens sandals brands in India? If yes, then you’ve come to the right place.

When it comes to shopping for sandals, men often struggle to find the right balance between style and comfort. With so many brands and styles available in the market, it can be overwhelming to make a decision. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive list of mens sandals brands in India. We will also discuss the features, price range, and where to buy them.

So, let's dive in and explore the best mens sandals brands in India.

Mens Sandals Brands List in India:

1. Bata - Bata is one of the most popular footwear brands in India. They offer a wide range of sandals for men that are both stylish and comfortable. The price range of Bata sandals starts from Rs. 499.

2. Red Tape - Red Tape is known for its premium quality footwear. Their sandals are perfect for those who want to make a style statement. The price range of Red Tape sandals starts from Rs. 1,299.

3. Woodland - Woodland is a brand that is known for its rugged and durable footwear. Their sandals are perfect for outdoor activities and adventure sports. The price range of Woodland sandals starts from Rs. 1,295.

4. Hush Puppies - Hush Puppies is a brand that is known for its stylish and comfortable footwear. Their sandals are perfect for those who want to stay comfortable while looking fashionable. The price range of Hush Puppies sandals starts from Rs. 1,799.

5. Adidas - Adidas is a brand that needs no introduction. They offer a wide range of sports sandals for men that are perfect for outdoor activities. The price range of Adidas sandals starts from Rs. 1,999.

My Personal Experience:

When I was looking for sandals for my brother, I was overwhelmed with the options available in the market. That's when I came across Bata sandals. They had a wide range of sandals that were both stylish and affordable. My brother loved the sandals, and they have lasted for a long time.

Where to Buy Mens Sandals Brands in India:

You can buy mens sandals brands in India from various online and offline stores. Some popular online stores include Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and Jabong. Offline stores such as Bata, Woodland, and Adidas stores are also a great option.

Features to Look for in Mens Sandals:

1. Comfort - The most important feature to look for in mens sandals is comfort. Make sure that the sandals you buy have good cushioning and support.

2. Style - Mens sandals come in various styles, such as casual, sports, and formal. Choose a style that suits your needs and preferences.

3. Material - The material of the sandals is also an important factor to consider. Leather and synthetic materials are the most common options.

How to Choose the Right Size:

Choosing the right size is crucial to ensure comfort and prevent any foot-related problems. Measure your foot length and width and refer to the size chart provided by the brand. It is also recommended to try the sandals before making a purchase.

FAQs:

Q. Which is the best brand for mens sports sandals?

A. Adidas and Reebok are the best brands for mens sports sandals.

Q. Which is the most affordable mens sandals brand in India?

A. Sparx and Relaxo are the most affordable mens sandals brands in India.

Q. Can I wear sandals to a formal event?

A. It is not recommended to wear sandals to a formal event. Stick to closed-toe shoes.

Q. How often should I replace my sandals?

A. It is recommended to replace your sandals every six months to a year, depending on usage and wear and tear.

Conclusion:

Now that you know the best mens sandals brands in India, you can make an informed decision and choose the perfect pair of sandals for yourself. Remember to consider the features, price, and where to buy them before making a purchase. Happy shopping!
