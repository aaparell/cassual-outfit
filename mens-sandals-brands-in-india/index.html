Top Mens Sandals Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in search of the perfect pair of sandals for your summer wardrobe? Are you tired of browsing through endless options online and in stores? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we will cover the top mens sandals brands in India to help you make an informed decision and find the perfect pair of sandals for you.

When it comes to purchasing sandals, there are a few pain points that many people face. Some common concerns include finding a pair that is comfortable, durable, stylish, and affordable. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start your search. That's where this guide comes in.

The target of this guide is to provide you with a list of the top mens sandals brands in India, along with information on their unique features, price range, and where to purchase them. We hope that by the end of this guide, you will have a better understanding of the mens sandals market in India and be able to make a confident purchase.

In summary, this guide will cover the top mens sandals brands in India, their unique features, price range, and where to purchase them, to help you make an informed decision when purchasing sandals.

Metro Shoes

My personal experience with Metro Shoes has been nothing but positive. Their sandals are not only comfortable but also stylish and durable. They offer a wide range of options, from casual to formal, making it easy to find a pair for any occasion. With prices ranging from Rs. 600 to Rs. 3000, they can fit any budget.

Bata

Bata is another popular brand that offers a wide range of sandals for men. Their sandals are known for their durability and comfort, making them a great option for everyday wear. With prices ranging from Rs. 500 to Rs. 2500, they are affordable and offer great value for money.

Hush Puppies

Hush Puppies is a premium brand that offers high-quality sandals for men. Their sandals are known for their comfort and durability, making them a great investment. With prices ranging from Rs. 2000 to Rs. 5000, they are a bit more expensive than other brands, but their quality makes them worth the investment.

How to Choose the Right Pair of Sandals

When choosing the right pair of sandals, it's important to consider factors such as comfort, durability, style, and price. Look for sandals that are made from high-quality materials and offer good support for your feet. Consider the occasion you will be wearing them for, and choose a style that fits the dress code. Finally, set a budget and stick to it to ensure you find a pair that fits your needs and your wallet.

Puma

Puma is a popular brand for athletic wear, but they also offer a range of sandals for men. Their sandals are known for their comfort and support, making them a great option for outdoor activities. With prices ranging from Rs. 1000 to Rs. 5000, they offer a range of options to fit any budget.

Conclusion of Mens Sandals Brands in India

When it comes to purchasing sandals, there are a variety of options available in India. From affordable options like Bata to premium brands like Hush Puppies, there is a pair of sandals for every budget and style. Consider factors such as comfort, durability, style, and price when making your purchase, and don't be afraid to try out different brands to find the perfect pair for you.
