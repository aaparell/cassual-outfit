The Most Expensive Designer Brands 2021: A Closer Look

Let's take a closer look at some of the most expensive designer brands 2021 and what makes them so special:

1. Chanel

Chanel is one of the most iconic and recognizable luxury brands in the world. Founded in 1909 by Coco Chanel, the brand is known for its classic designs, high-quality materials, and attention to detail. From the famous Chanel No. 5 perfume to the iconic quilted handbags, Chanel is a symbol of luxury and sophistication.

How to Spot a Real Chanel Bag

One of the most popular items from Chanel is their quilted handbags. However, with the popularity of the brand comes the risk of counterfeit items. To spot a real Chanel bag, look for the following:

- The stitching should be straight and even

- The hardware should be high-quality and heavy

- The logo should be centered and symmetrical

2. Louis Vuitton

Another iconic luxury brand, Louis Vuitton is known for its monogrammed bags and luggage. The brand was founded in 1854 and has since become a symbol of luxury and sophistication. Louis Vuitton offers a wide range of products, from handbags to clothing to accessories.

How to Spot a Real Louis Vuitton Bag

Like Chanel, Louis Vuitton bags are often counterfeited. To spot a real Louis Vuitton bag, look for the following:

- The stitching should be straight and even

- The hardware should be high-quality and heavy

- The monogram should be symmetrical and not cut off at the seams
