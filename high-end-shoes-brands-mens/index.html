The Ultimate Guide To High End Shoes Brands For Men

Written by Oliver May 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of buying cheap shoes that don't last? Do you want to invest in a high quality pair of shoes that will last for years to come? Look no further than high end shoes brands for men. These brands offer the best quality materials and craftsmanship, ensuring that your shoes will not only look great, but also stand the test of time.

When it comes to high end shoes brands for men, it can be overwhelming to choose the right brand. With so many options available, it's important to consider your individual needs and preferences to find the perfect pair of shoes. Whether you're looking for dress shoes for work or casual shoes for the weekend, there's a high end brand out there for you.

In this guide, we'll explore some of the top high end shoes brands for men and what makes them stand out. We'll also provide tips for choosing the right brand and caring for your high end shoes to ensure they last as long as possible.

Allen Edmonds

One of the most well-known high end shoes brands for men is Allen Edmonds. This brand has been around since 1922 and is known for its timeless designs and high quality materials. Allen Edmonds shoes are made in the USA and are crafted with meticulous attention to detail. The brand offers a wide range of shoes, from dress shoes to casual shoes, and even offers a custom shoe program for those looking for something truly unique.

Personally, I own a pair of Allen Edmonds Park Avenue dress shoes and they are hands down the best dress shoes I have ever owned. They are comfortable, durable, and look great with any suit.

John Lobb

John Lobb is another top high end shoes brand for men. This brand is known for its exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each pair of John Lobb shoes is made by hand in Northampton, England, using only the finest materials. The brand offers a range of shoes, from dress shoes to boots, and even offers a bespoke service for those looking for a truly one-of-a-kind pair of shoes.

While I don't own a pair of John Lobb shoes myself, I have heard nothing but great things about the brand. If you're looking for a truly luxurious pair of shoes, John Lobb is definitely worth checking out.

Common Projects

If you're looking for a more modern high end shoes brand for men, Common Projects is a great option. This brand is known for its minimalist designs and high quality materials. Each pair of Common Projects shoes is made in Italy and features a unique gold-stamped serial number, adding a touch of luxury to each pair. The brand offers a range of sneakers, boots, and dress shoes, all with the signature minimalist design.

I recently purchased a pair of Common Projects Achilles sneakers and they have quickly become my go-to casual shoes. They are incredibly comfortable and look great with any outfit.

Caring for Your High End Shoes

Once you've invested in a pair of high end shoes, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your high end shoes:

  • Invest in some high quality shoe trees to help maintain the shape of your shoes when not in use.
  • Use a shoe horn when putting on your shoes to prevent the back from getting crushed.
  • Store your shoes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Clean your shoes regularly with a soft cloth and high quality shoe cleaner.
  • Consider taking your shoes to a professional cobbler for repairs or maintenance.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end shoes really worth the investment?

A: Absolutely. While high end shoes may be more expensive upfront, they are made with the best quality materials and craftsmanship, ensuring that they will last for years to come. Plus, they often look better and are more comfortable than cheaper shoes.

Q: How do I know which high end shoe brand to choose?

A: It's important to consider your individual needs and preferences when choosing a high end shoe brand. Think about what type of shoes you need (dress shoes, casual shoes, etc.), as well as your personal style and budget. Do some research online and read reviews from other customers to get an idea of which brands are highly rated.

Q: How often should I replace my high end shoes?

A: With proper care, high end shoes can last for years. However, it's a good idea to replace them when they start to show signs of wear and tear, such as holes in the soles or leather that is cracking.

Q: Can I wear high end shoes with casual outfits?

A: Absolutely. Many high end shoe brands offer casual shoes that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For example, Common Projects offers a range of sneakers that look great with jeans or chinos.

Conclusion of High End Shoes Brands for Men

Investing in a pair of high end shoes is a smart decision for any man looking for quality, comfort, and style. With so many great brands to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the right one. By considering your needs and preferences, doing some research, and taking care of your shoes, you can find the perfect pair of high end shoes that will last for years to come.
