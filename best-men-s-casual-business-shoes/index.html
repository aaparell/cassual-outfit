Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear sneakers to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If sneakers are allowed, consider opting for a sneaker-style shoe that has a dressier look.

Q: Are leather shoes more durable than synthetic materials?

A: Generally, leather shoes are more durable than synthetic materials. However, there are high-quality synthetic materials that can be just as durable as leather.

Q: Can I wear casual shoes to a job interview?

A: It's always best to err on the side of caution and wear dress shoes to a job interview. However, if you know the workplace dress code is more casual, a dressier sneaker-style shoe may be appropriate.

Q: How do I make sure my shoes fit properly?

A: When trying on shoes, make sure there is about a half-inch of space between your toes and the end of the shoe. The shoe should also fit snugly but not be too tight. Walk around in the shoes to ensure they are comfortable and don't rub in any areas.
