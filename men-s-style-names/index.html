Men s Style Names A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Have you ever found yourself staring at your closet, unsure of what to wear? Do you sometimes feel like you're speaking a different language when your stylish friend starts talking about "chinos" and "oxfords"? Understanding men's style names can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be.

Knowing men's style names is essential because it helps you communicate what you want to wear, understand what you're buying, and avoid fashion faux pas.

In this article, we'll break down some of the most common men's style names and explain what they mean. By the end of this guide, you'll be able to confidently navigate your wardrobe and talk fashion with your stylish friends.

Casual Style

When it comes to casual style, comfort is key. This style is all about keeping it relaxed and effortless. Think of pieces like t-shirts, hoodies, and sneakers.

Personally, I love pairing a simple white t-shirt with a pair of light-wash jeans and classic Chuck Taylors. It's an easy, comfortable look that never goes out of style.

Preppy Style

Preppy style is all about looking polished and put-together. This style is often associated with classic East Coast universities and features pieces like blazers, button-downs, and khakis.

One of my favorite preppy looks is a navy blazer paired with a crisp white button-down and slim-fit khakis. It's a timeless look that always makes me feel confident.

Dress Shoes

Dress shoes are essential for any formal occasion like weddings, job interviews, and funerals. When it comes to dress shoes, it's important to invest in a high-quality pair that's comfortable and versatile.

My go-to dress shoes are a classic pair of black oxfords. They're timeless and can be worn with a variety of outfits.

Tips for Finding Your Style

Finding your personal style can take time, but it's worth the effort. Here are some tips to help you find your style:

1. Take inspiration from others: Look to stylish friends, celebrities, and fashion bloggers for inspiration.

2. Experiment with different styles: Try out different styles to see what feels most comfortable and authentic.

3. Don't be afraid to mix and match: Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles to create a unique look.

Sporty Style

Sporty style is perfect for guys who love to stay active. This style features pieces like athletic shorts, joggers, and sneakers.

My go-to sporty look is a pair of black joggers, a simple white t-shirt, and my favorite pair of running shoes. It's comfortable and perfect for a quick workout or running errands.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between chinos and khakis?

A: Chinos are typically made of a lighter-weight cotton twill fabric and have a slimmer fit than khakis. Khakis are usually made of a heavier-weight cotton and have a looser fit.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a suit?

A: It depends on the occasion. Sneakers can be worn with a suit for a more casual event, but it's generally not appropriate for formal occasions like weddings or job interviews.

Q: What is a pocket square?

A: A pocket square is a small piece of fabric that's placed in the pocket of a suit jacket or blazer. It's a stylish accessory that can add a pop of color and personality to any outfit.

Q: What are oxfords?

A: Oxfords are a type of dress shoe that features closed lacing and a low heel. They're typically made of leather and are a classic, versatile shoe that can be worn with a variety of outfits.

Conclusion of Men's Style Names

Understanding men's style names can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. By familiarizing yourself with common style names like casual, preppy, and sporty, you can confidently navigate your wardrobe and dress for any occasion.
