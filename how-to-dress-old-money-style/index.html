How To Dress Old Money Style A Guide To Timeless Elegance

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to exude an air of sophistication and refinement in your fashion choices? Do you want to know how to dress old money style without looking outdated or stuffy? Many people aspire to the timeless elegance of those born into wealth and privilege, but achieving this look can be daunting. From the right fabrics to the perfect accessories, there are many factors to consider when dressing in the old money style.

If you want to dress like old money, you need to prioritize quality over quantity. This means investing in high-quality fabrics, tailored fits, and classic styles that will stand the test of time. It's not about following every fashion trend or filling your closet with fast fashion. Instead, it's about curating a wardrobe full of timeless pieces that will never go out of style.

One of the keys to dressing like old money is to keep it simple. Stick to neutral colors like black, white, navy, and beige, and avoid loud prints or patterns. This doesn't mean you have to be boring, however. You can still add interest to your outfits with luxurious textures like silk, cashmere, and wool.

In summary, to dress old money style, prioritize quality over quantity, keep it simple with neutral colors and luxurious textures, and invest in classic styles that will never go out of fashion.

Invest in Timeless Pieces

When it comes to dressing like old money, the key is to invest in timeless pieces that will never go out of style. This means choosing classic styles that have been around for decades, like a well-tailored blazer or a pair of leather loafers. These pieces may be more expensive upfront, but they will last for years and can be worn in a variety of ways.

For example, a classic trench coat is a must-have for anyone looking to dress like old money. It's a versatile piece that can be worn with everything from jeans to a cocktail dress. Look for a high-quality coat made from a sturdy fabric like cotton gabardine, and opt for a neutral color like beige or navy.

Accessorize Thoughtfully

Accessories are an important part of any outfit, but when it comes to dressing like old money, it's important to choose them thoughtfully. Stick to simple, classic pieces like a leather belt or a silk scarf, and avoid anything too flashy or trendy. Look for accessories made from high-quality materials like leather, silk, or cashmere.

When it comes to jewelry, less is often more. Stick to simple pieces like a pair of diamond stud earrings or a delicate gold necklace. Avoid anything too chunky or trendy, and opt for pieces that can be worn with a variety of outfits.

Choose the Right Fabrics

The right fabrics are essential when it comes to dressing like old money. Look for high-quality materials like silk, cashmere, wool, and leather. These fabrics not only look luxurious, but they also feel amazing to wear. They also tend to last longer than cheaper materials, so investing in them can save you money in the long run.

When it comes to choosing fabrics, it's also important to think about the season. In the winter, look for warm, cozy materials like wool and cashmere. In the summer, opt for lightweight, breathable fabrics like cotton and linen.

Dress for the Occasion

Another important factor to consider when dressing like old money is the occasion. Different events call for different dress codes, so it's important to dress appropriately. For example, a black-tie event requires a formal evening gown or a tuxedo, while a casual brunch with friends calls for something more relaxed like a sundress or a pair of khakis.

When in doubt, it's always better to err on the side of overdressing. It's better to be the most elegant person in the room than the most underdressed.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it necessary to spend a lot of money to dress like old money?

A: While investing in high-quality pieces is important, it's not necessary to spend a fortune to dress like old money. You can often find classic pieces at thrift stores or on sale at high-end retailers.

Q: Can I incorporate modern trends into my old money style?

A: While it's important to prioritize classic styles over trendy pieces, you can still incorporate modern trends into your old money style. Just be sure to choose trendy pieces that still have a classic feel to them.

Q: How can I make my old money style look more casual?

A: To make your old money style look more casual, opt for relaxed fits and more casual fabrics like denim or cotton. You can also add interest to your outfits with accessories like a brightly colored scarf or a statement handbag.

Q: Can men dress like old money too?

A: Absolutely! The old money style is not just for women. Men can dress like old money by investing in classic pieces like a well-tailored suit or a pair of leather dress shoes.

Conclusion of how to dress old money style

Dressing like old money is all about prioritizing quality over quantity, investing in timeless pieces, and keeping it simple with neutral colors and luxurious textures. By following these tips, you can achieve a timeless elegance that will never go out of style.
