Exploring Different International Helmet Brands

Written by Oliver Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a motorcycle enthusiast looking for a new helmet? Or maybe you’re a beginner rider and not sure where to start? Whatever the case may be, finding the perfect helmet is crucial for both safety and comfort. With so many international helmet brands on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the top international helmet brands and what makes them stand out.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to choosing an international helmet brand is the overwhelming amount of options available. Each brand offers different features, styles, and price points, making it difficult to narrow down the choices. Additionally, some riders may have concerns about the quality and safety of international helmet brands compared to those in their home country.

The target of international helmet brands is to provide riders with a wide range of options that cater to different preferences and needs. Whether you’re looking for a helmet with advanced safety features, sleek design, or affordable pricing, there is a brand out there for you. It’s important to do your research and read reviews to ensure you’re choosing a reputable brand that meets safety standards.

In summary, international helmet brands offer a diverse selection of helmets to cater to different rider preferences and needs. It’s important to choose a reputable brand that meets safety standards to ensure the best protection while riding.

Arai

Arai is a Japanese helmet brand that has been around since 1926. The brand is known for its handcrafted helmets that prioritize safety and quality. I personally own an Arai helmet and can attest to the comfort and fit. Arai offers a range of helmets for both street and race use, with features such as ventilation systems and anti-fog shields.

Shoei

Another Japanese helmet brand, Shoei is popular among riders for its sleek design and advanced safety features. The brand offers a range of helmets for different riding styles, from touring to racing. Shoei helmets also come with a five-year warranty, providing peace of mind for riders.

HJC

HJC is a South Korean brand that offers affordable helmets without sacrificing safety and quality. The brand offers a range of helmets for different riding styles and preferences, with features such as sun visors and moisture-wicking interiors. I personally started with an HJC helmet and found it to be a great beginner option.

Nolan

Nolan is an Italian brand that offers helmets with unique designs and features. The brand is known for its modular helmets, which allow riders to flip up the chin bar for added convenience. Nolan also offers helmets with built-in communication systems for easy communication while riding.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a helmet?

A: When choosing a helmet, it’s important to prioritize safety features such as impact-absorbing materials and proper fit. It’s also important to consider your riding style and preferences for features such as ventilation and sun visors.

Q: Are international helmet brands safe?

A: Yes, international helmet brands can be just as safe as those in your home country as long as they meet safety standards. It’s important to do your research and read reviews before purchasing a helmet.

Q: How do I know if a helmet fits properly?

A: A properly fitting helmet should feel snug but not uncomfortable. It should not move around on your head or obstruct your vision. You can use a sizing chart provided by the brand or visit a local shop to try on helmets in person.

Q: How often should I replace my helmet?

A: It’s recommended to replace your helmet every five years or after any impact or accident. The materials in the helmet can deteriorate over time and impact can compromise the safety features.

Conclusion of International Helmet Brands

Choosing the right helmet is crucial for both safety and comfort while riding. International helmet brands offer a diverse selection of helmets to cater to different preferences and needs. By doing your research and choosing a reputable brand that meets safety standards, you can ensure the best protection while riding. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced rider, there is an international helmet brand out there for you.
