What Do Male Guests Wear To Indian Weddings

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Attending an Indian wedding can be a daunting experience, especially if you are not familiar with the customs and traditions. One of the most common questions that arise is what do male guests wear to Indian weddings. It can be confusing to figure out the appropriate attire, but it is essential to dress appropriately to show respect for the couple and their families.

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance, and the attire for male guests is no different. The dress code for Indian weddings can vary depending on the region, religion, and customs. The last thing you want to do is show up in the wrong attire, so it's essential to understand the dress code and dress accordingly.

The dress code for male guests at Indian weddings typically includes a traditional outfit, such as a sherwani or a kurta pajama. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar or pajama. It is typically made of silk or other rich fabrics and is adorned with embroidery, sequins, or other embellishments. A kurta pajama is a two-piece outfit consisting of a long tunic-like top and loose-fitting pants. It can be made of cotton, silk, or other materials, and is often paired with a dupatta or shawl.

In addition to the traditional outfit, you can also opt for a western-style suit or a blazer with a pair of trousers. However, it is essential to keep in mind that the wedding is a formal occasion, and your outfit should reflect that. Avoid wearing jeans, t-shirts, or sneakers, as they are not appropriate for the occasion.

Personal Experience

As someone who has attended several Indian weddings, I can attest to the importance of dressing appropriately. At my first Indian wedding, I was unsure of what to wear and ended up wearing a suit. While I received compliments on my outfit, I felt out of place as most of the other guests were dressed in traditional attire. Since then, I always make sure to wear a traditional outfit or a fusion outfit that combines traditional and western elements.

Tips for Choosing the Right Attire

1. Research the dress code: As mentioned earlier, the dress code for Indian weddings can vary depending on the region, religion, and customs. Do some research on the couple's background and the wedding's location to get an idea of what would be appropriate attire.

2. Opt for traditional attire: While a western-style suit may be acceptable, it is always better to opt for traditional attire. It shows respect for the couple and their families and adds to the overall festive atmosphere of the wedding.

3. Choose the right fabric: Indian weddings are typically held in the summer months, so it is essential to choose the right fabric that is breathable and lightweight. Cotton, silk, and linen are ideal fabrics for Indian weddings.

4. Accessorize wisely: Accessories can add a touch of elegance to your outfit, but it is essential not to go overboard. A simple watch, cufflinks, or a pocket square can complement your outfit and add a touch of sophistication.

What Not to Wear

1. Jeans and t-shirts: Indian weddings are formal occasions, and jeans and t-shirts are not appropriate attire. Avoid wearing casual clothes and opt for traditional or formal attire.

2. Athletic shoes: Sneakers and athletic shoes are not suitable for Indian weddings. Opt for dress shoes or sandals instead.

3. Over the top outfits: While it is essential to dress appropriately, it is also essential not to go overboard. Avoid wearing outfits with too many embellishments or bright colors that may be distracting.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a western-style suit to an Indian wedding?

A: Yes, a western-style suit is acceptable, but it is always better to opt for traditional attire.

Q: Can I wear jeans and a t-shirt to an Indian wedding?

A: No, jeans and t-shirts are not appropriate attire for an Indian wedding.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to an Indian wedding?

A: No, athletic shoes and sneakers are not suitable for Indian weddings. Opt for dress shoes or sandals instead.

Q: What kind of accessories should I wear to an Indian wedding?

A: Accessories can add a touch of elegance to your outfit, but it is essential not to go overboard. A simple watch, cufflinks, or a pocket square can complement your outfit and add a touch of sophistication.

Conclusion

Attending an Indian wedding is a unique and exciting experience, but it can be overwhelming to figure out what to wear. The dress code for male guests typically includes a traditional outfit, such as a sherwani or a kurta pajama, or a western-style suit. It is essential to dress appropriately and avoid casual attire such as jeans and t-shirts. By following these tips and doing some research on the dress code, you can ensure that you are dressed appropriately and show respect for the couple and their families.
