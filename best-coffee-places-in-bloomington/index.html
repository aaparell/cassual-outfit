Discovering The Best Coffee Places In Bloomington

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a coffee lover who recently moved to Bloomington or just someone who wants to explore the city's coffee scene? Finding the best coffee places in Bloomington can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

When it comes to finding the best coffee places in Bloomington, there are a few things you need to consider. Firstly, the quality of coffee and the variety of drinks offered. Secondly, the ambiance and the overall atmosphere of the place. Lastly, the location and convenience of the coffee shop.

After thorough research and personal experiences, we have compiled a list of the top coffee places in Bloomington that meet all the criteria mentioned above. So, let's dive in and explore these coffee gems in Bloomington.

The Inkwell

If you're looking for a cozy and intimate coffee spot, The Inkwell is the place to be. Located in the heart of downtown Bloomington, this coffee shop serves some of the best espresso drinks in town. The Inkwell's ambiance is perfect for working or studying, with quiet background music and comfortable seating. They offer a wide variety of coffee drinks, teas, and pastries that are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Soma Coffeehouse and Juice Bar

Soma Coffeehouse and Juice Bar is a popular spot among the locals, and for good reason. Their coffee is top-notch, and they offer a variety of unique drinks like turmeric lattes and matcha green tea. Soma also has a full menu of smoothies and juices, making it the perfect spot for a healthy breakfast or lunch. The spacious and bright interior of the shop provides a refreshing ambiance, and the outdoor seating is perfect for warm days.

Hopscotch Coffee

If you're looking for a coffee shop with a unique and quirky vibe, Hopscotch Coffee is the place to be. Located in a renovated garage, this coffee shop offers a cozy and rustic ambiance that is perfect for catching up with friends or getting work done. Their coffee is excellent, and they offer a variety of drinks, including pour-over coffee and specialty lattes. Hopscotch also has a small menu of pastries and sandwiches, making it a great spot for a quick breakfast or lunch.

Crumble Coffee & Bakery

Crumble Coffee & Bakery is a locally-owned coffee shop that offers some of the best baked goods in Bloomington. Their coffee is excellent, and they offer a variety of unique drinks like lavender lattes and rose petal cappuccinos. The interior of the shop is cozy and welcoming, with plenty of seating options. Crumble also has a full menu of breakfast and lunch items, including sandwiches, salads, and quiches.

Question and Answer Section

A: Prices vary from shop to shop, but you can expect to pay around $4-5 for a coffee drink at most of these places.

Q: Do these coffee shops have free Wi-Fi?

A: Yes, all of the coffee shops mentioned in this post offer free Wi-Fi.

Q: Are these coffee shops child-friendly?

A: Yes, all of the coffee shops mentioned in this article are child-friendly and provide ample seating options.

Q: Do these coffee shops offer vegan options?

A: Yes, all of the coffee shops mentioned in this post offer vegan options.

Conclusion of Best Coffee Places in Bloomington

The coffee scene in Bloomington is thriving, with plenty of unique and cozy coffee shops to explore. Whether you're looking for a place to work, catch up with friends, or grab a quick bite, these coffee spots are sure to please. So, grab your laptop or a book, and head out to discover the best coffee places in Bloomington.
