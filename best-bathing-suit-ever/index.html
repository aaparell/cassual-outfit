Question and Answer

Q: What is the most flattering bathing suit style?

A: The most flattering bathing suit style depends on your body shape. If you have an hourglass figure, a high-waisted bikini or a one-piece with cutouts can be flattering. If you have a pear-shaped body, a halter top or a one-piece with ruching can be flattering.

Q: What color bathing suit is the most slimming?

A: Dark colors such as black, navy, and dark green can be slimming. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel good.

Q: How often should I wash my bathing suit?

A: It's recommended to wash your bathing suit after every use to remove salt, sand, and chlorine. Hand wash your swimwear in cold water with a mild detergent and lay it flat to dry.

Q: How can I prevent my bathing suit from fading?

A: To prevent your bathing suit from fading, avoid exposing it to direct sunlight for long periods. Rinse your swimwear in cold water after every use and avoid using harsh detergents or fabric softeners.
