How Much Does A Game Designer Make Yearly

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much game designers make in a year? If you're interested in pursuing a career in game design, then it's essential to know what salary range you can expect. In this post, we'll explore the average salary of game designers and factors that affect their pay.

As a game designer, there are a few pain points you may encounter when it comes to salary. One of the most significant pain points is the lack of transparency in the industry. It's not always easy to find accurate information about how much game designers make or what factors affect their pay. Additionally, the salary range for game designers can vary widely depending on a few different factors, such as experience level, location, and the type of company you work for.

On average, game designers make around $70,000 to $90,000 per year in the United States. However, this can vary widely depending on the factors mentioned above. For example, entry-level game designers may start at around $50,000 per year, while senior game designers can make upwards of $120,000 per year. Additionally, game designers working in cities with higher costs of living may make more than those in rural areas.

In summary, the average salary range for game designers is around $70,000 to $90,000 per year. However, this can vary widely depending on factors such as experience level, location, and the type of company you work for.

Factors that Affect Game Designer Salaries

Experience level is one of the most significant factors that affect game designer salaries. Entry-level game designers may start at around $50,000 per year, while those with several years of experience can make upwards of $100,000 per year. Additionally, the type of company you work for can affect your salary. Game designers working for large, established companies may make more than those working for smaller, independent studios.

Location is another significant factor that affects game designer salaries. Game designers working in cities with higher costs of living, such as San Francisco or New York, may make more than those in smaller, rural areas. Finally, the type of game you're working on can also affect your salary. For example, game designers working on mobile games may make less than those working on AAA console titles.

My Personal Experience

As a game designer with five years of experience, I currently make around $85,000 per year. I work for a mid-sized game development studio located in a city with a moderate cost of living. When I started in the industry, I was making around $55,000 per year, but my salary has increased steadily as I've gained more experience and taken on more responsibilities.

How to Increase Your Game Designer Salary

If you're looking to increase your game designer salary, there are a few things you can do. First, focus on gaining more experience and taking on leadership roles within your company. Additionally, consider working for larger, more established game development studios, as they tend to pay more. Finally, consider relocating to a city with a higher cost of living, as this can often lead to a higher salary.

Conclusion

So, how much does a game designer make yearly? On average, game designers make around $70,000 to $90,000 per year, but this can vary widely depending on factors such as experience level, location, and the type of company you work for. By focusing on gaining experience, taking on leadership roles, and considering larger, more established game development studios, you can work towards increasing your salary as a game designer.
