Ralph Lauren

When it comes to classic American fashion brands, Ralph Lauren is one of the most well-known. This brand offers a wide range of clothing and accessories that are perfect for any occasion. The brand is known for its preppy style, which includes polo shirts, blazers, and chinos.

I have personally owned a few Ralph Lauren polos for years, and they still look great. The quality of the material is excellent and has held up well over time. Ralph Lauren is a great brand to invest in if you are looking for classic pieces that will last you for years to come.

Levi's

Levi's is another classic American brand that has been around for over 150 years. The brand is known for its high-quality denim, which is perfect for anyone who wants a pair of jeans that will last them for years to come.

I have owned a few pairs of Levi's jeans over the years, and they have always been my go-to brand for denim. The quality of the material is excellent, and the fit is always perfect. If you are looking for a pair of jeans that will last you for years to come, then Levi's is the brand for you.

Tommy Hilfiger
