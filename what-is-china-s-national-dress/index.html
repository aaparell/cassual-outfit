What Is China s National Dress

Written by Eveline Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered what the traditional dress of China is? Are you planning to visit China and want to know what to wear? Look no further! In this article, we will explore China's national dress and its significance.

China has a rich cultural heritage, and clothing is an essential part of it. However, with the western influence, most Chinese people wear western-style clothing. Nonetheless, many still wear traditional dress on special occasions.

China's national dress is called the Hanfu. It is a type of clothing worn by the Han Chinese, the largest ethnic group in China. The Hanfu is known for its flowing lines, intricate patterns, and vibrant colors. It is often worn on occasions like New Year celebrations, weddings, and other formal events.

In summary, China's national dress is the Hanfu, a traditional dress worn by the Han Chinese. It is known for its flowing lines, intricate patterns, and vibrant colors.

The History of Hanfu

The Hanfu has a long and rich history dating back to the ancient times. It was worn during the Han dynasty (206 BC to 220 AD) and continued to be worn until the Ming (1368-1644) and Qing (1644-1912) dynasties. However, during the Qing dynasty, the Manchu-style clothing became popular, and the Hanfu gradually disappeared.

But in recent years, there has been a renewed interest in traditional Chinese culture, including the Hanfu. Many young people in China are now wearing the Hanfu as a way to connect with their cultural heritage.

Styles of Hanfu

The Hanfu comes in different styles, each with its unique characteristics. Some of the most popular styles include the Zhongshan suit, the Tang suit, and the Qipao.

The Zhongshan suit is a modern style of the Hanfu worn by men. It features a jacket with a high collar, a four-button front, and a pair of trousers. The Tang suit, on the other hand, is a traditional style of the Hanfu worn by both men and women. It features a jacket with a stand-up collar, a diagonal front closing, and a pair of trousers or a skirt.

The Qipao is a traditional style of the Hanfu worn by women. It is a figure-hugging dress that features a high collar, a slit skirt, and short sleeves. The Qipao became popular in the 1920s and 1930s and is still worn today on formal occasions.

How to Wear Hanfu

Wearing the Hanfu is not just about putting on a piece of clothing; it is a way of expressing respect for traditional Chinese culture. Here are some tips on how to wear the Hanfu:

1. Choose the right style for the occasion.

2. Choose the right fabric, color, and pattern.

3. Pay attention to the details, such as the collar, sleeves, and hemline.

4. Accessorize with traditional jewelry and hair ornaments.

Where to Buy Hanfu

If you are interested in buying a Hanfu, there are many online and offline stores that sell them. However, it is essential to buy from a reputable seller who uses high-quality fabric and pays attention to the details.

Conclusion of China's National Dress

In conclusion, China's national dress is the Hanfu, a traditional dress worn by the Han Chinese. It has a long and rich history and comes in different styles. Wearing the Hanfu is a way of expressing respect for traditional Chinese culture, and it is essential to choose the right style, fabric, and accessories.
