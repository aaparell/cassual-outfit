The Best Curvy Swimsuit Brands You Need To Know About

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find a swimsuit that fits your curves? Do you dread swimsuit shopping because you can never find anything that flatters your body? You're not alone. Finding a swimsuit that fits well and makes you feel confident can be a challenge, but there are brands out there that cater specifically to curvy women.

If you're looking for the best curvy swimsuit brands, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll share our top picks and explain why they're worth checking out.

When it comes to swimsuits for curvy women, there are a few key factors to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want a suit that fits well and provides support where you need it. You also want a suit that flatters your body and makes you feel confident. And let's not forget about style – you want a suit that reflects your personal taste and makes you excited to hit the beach or pool.

In this post, we'll cover some of the best curvy swimsuit brands on the market and why we love them. We'll also share some tips for finding the perfect swimsuit for your body type.

1. Swimsuits For All

Swimsuits For All is a brand that truly lives up to its name. They offer a huge selection of swimsuits in sizes ranging from 4 to 40, so you're sure to find something that fits your curves. In addition to a wide range of sizes, Swimsuits For All also offers a variety of styles, from classic one-pieces to trendy bikinis. Their suits are designed with features like tummy control and underwire support to flatter and support your curves.

Personally, I've had great experiences with Swimsuits For All. Their suits are well-made, comfortable, and stylish. I love that they offer a variety of styles to choose from, so I can find something that suits my personal taste. And of course, the fact that they offer such a wide range of sizes is a major plus.

2. Lane Bryant

Lane Bryant is a brand that's been around for decades, and for good reason. They specialize in clothing and lingerie for plus-size women, and their swimwear is no exception. Lane Bryant offers a variety of swimsuit styles, from simple one-pieces to trendy bikinis. Their suits are designed with features like built-in bras and control panels to provide support and flatter your curves.

I recently purchased a swimsuit from Lane Bryant and was really impressed with the quality and fit. The suit was comfortable, supportive, and flattering. I also appreciated that they offer a variety of styles to choose from, so there's something for everyone.

3. Torrid

Torrid is a brand that's known for its trendy, youthful clothing for plus-size women. Their swimwear is no exception – they offer a variety of stylish suits in sizes up to 6X. Torrid's swimsuits feature details like ruching, cutouts, and fun prints to add interest and flatter your curves. They also offer a variety of styles, from one-pieces to bikinis to swim dresses.

While I haven't personally tried any swimsuits from Torrid, I've heard great things about their quality and fit. I love that they offer such a wide range of sizes and styles, so there's something for everyone.

4. Aerie

Aerie is a brand that's known for its inclusive sizing and body-positive messaging. While they don't specialize in plus-size clothing, they do offer a range of sizes up to XXL. Aerie's swimsuits are designed to be comfortable, flattering, and on-trend. They offer a variety of styles, including bikinis, one-pieces, and high-waisted bottoms.

I've tried several swimsuits from Aerie and have always been impressed with the fit and quality. Their suits are comfortable, supportive, and stylish. I also appreciate that they offer a range of sizes and styles, so there's something for everyone.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a swimsuit for curvy women?

A: When shopping for a swimsuit as a curvy woman, look for features like tummy control, underwire support, and adjustable straps. You'll also want to consider the style – some curvy women prefer one-pieces for extra coverage and support, while others feel more comfortable in a bikini.

Q: How do I find the right size swimsuit?

A: The best way to find the right size swimsuit is to take your measurements and consult the brand's size chart. Don't be afraid to order multiple sizes to try on at home – it's better to find the perfect fit than to settle for something that doesn't flatter your curves.

Q: What if I don't feel comfortable in a swimsuit?

A: Remember that confidence comes from within. If you're not feeling great in a swimsuit, try to focus on the things you love about yourself instead of fixating on your perceived flaws. You can also try experimenting with different styles or coverups until you find something that makes you feel comfortable and confident.

Q: Can I wear a bikini if I'm a curvy woman?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of bikinis out there that are designed to flatter curvy figures. Look for styles with high-waisted bottoms or tops with underwire support to provide extra coverage and support.

Conclusion of Best Curvy Swimsuit Brands

When it comes to finding the perfect swimsuit for your curves, it's all about finding a brand that understands your body and offers styles that flatter and support you. Whether you prefer classic one-pieces or trendy bikinis, there are brands out there that cater specifically to curvy women. With the right swimsuit, you can feel confident and comfortable hitting the beach or pool this summer.
