How Does The Mother Of The Groom Dress A Guide For Every Wedding

Written by Bardi Jun 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Weddings are joyous occasions that bring together families and friends to celebrate the love between two people. However, figuring out what to wear to a wedding can be stressful, especially if you're the mother of the groom. You want to look your best without overshadowing the bride or violating any dress codes. In this guide, we'll answer the question: how does the mother of the groom dress?

As the mother of the groom, you want to look elegant and sophisticated without stealing the show. You also want to avoid any fashion faux pas like wearing white or black. Additionally, you want to dress appropriately for the time of day and the venue of the wedding. All of these factors can make finding the perfect dress seem like a daunting task.

What to Wear

First and foremost, you should consult with the bride and groom regarding the dress code. If they haven't provided one, look to the invitation for clues. If it's a formal or black-tie affair, a long gown in a neutral color like navy or champagne is appropriate. If it's a semi-formal or cocktail attire wedding, you can opt for a knee-length dress in a bright color or floral pattern. For a casual or beach wedding, a sundress or flowy maxi dress is perfect.

Another factor to consider is the time of day. For daytime weddings, pastels and light colors are appropriate, while darker colors are better for evening weddings. You should also dress for the venue. For example, if the wedding is at a church, it's best to cover your shoulders and wear a dress that hits below the knee. If it's an outdoor wedding, don't wear heels that will sink into the grass.

Accessorizing

Once you've found the perfect dress, it's time to accessorize. You should choose shoes that are comfortable and won't hurt your feet after hours of dancing. Avoid wearing anything too flashy or attention-grabbing. Instead, opt for simple jewelry like a pair of earrings and a bracelet. If the wedding is outdoors, bring a shawl or jacket to keep warm.

Makeup and Hair

Your makeup and hair should complement your dress and accessories. You can ask a professional hairstylist and makeup artist for advice, or you can watch tutorials on YouTube. Keep in mind that you want to look natural and polished, not overdone.

Dos and Don'ts

Do wear comfortable shoes that you can dance in. Don't wear white or black. Do dress appropriately for the time of day and venue. Don't wear anything too revealing or attention-grabbing. Do consult with the bride and groom regarding the dress code. Don't wear anything too casual or too formal.

Personal Experience

When my son got married, I wanted to look my best without upstaging the bride. I chose a knee-length dress in a bright coral color with a floral pattern. I paired it with nude heels and simple jewelry. I kept my makeup and hair natural and polished. I felt comfortable and elegant, and I received many compliments throughout the wedding.

Conclusion

As the mother of the groom, you want to look your best while adhering to the dress code and avoiding any fashion faux pas. By choosing a dress that is appropriate for the time of day and venue, accessorizing with simple jewelry, and keeping your makeup and hair natural and polished, you can look elegant and sophisticated without stealing the show. Remember to consult with the bride and groom for any dress code guidelines and have fun celebrating this joyous occasion!

Question and Answer

Q: Can the mother of the groom wear a long dress to a daytime wedding?
A: It depends on the dress code and venue. If it's a formal or black-tie affair, a long gown is appropriate regardless of the time of day. However, if it's a semi-formal or casual wedding, a long dress may be too formal for a daytime event.

Q: Can the mother of the groom wear black to a wedding?
A: While black is a chic and versatile color, it's generally not recommended to wear to a wedding. Black is associated with mourning and can be seen as inappropriate for a joyous occasion like a wedding.

Q: Can the mother of the groom wear a pantsuit to a wedding?
A: A pantsuit can be a stylish and comfortable option for the mother of the groom. However, it's important to ensure that the pantsuit is appropriate for the dress code and venue. A formal or black-tie wedding may require a more formal option like a gown.

Q: Can the mother of the groom wear a fascinator to a wedding?
A: A fascinator can be a fun and festive accessory for a wedding. However, it's important not to overshadow the bride or wear anything too attention-grabbing. A simple fascinator that complements your dress and accessories is a great option.

Conclusion

Dressing as the mother of the groom is an important task, but it doesn't have to be stressful. By following a few simple guidelines and consulting with the bride and groom, you can find the perfect dress for the occasion. Remember to dress appropriately for the time of day and venue, accessorize with simple jewelry, and keep your makeup and hair natural and polished. With these tips, you can look elegant and sophisticated while celebrating the love between two people.
