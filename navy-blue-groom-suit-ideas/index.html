Navy Blue Groom Suit Ideas A Guide To Looking Sharp On Your Big Day

Written by Frank Aug 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Planning a wedding can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to deciding on the perfect suit for the groom. With so many options available, it can be difficult to know where to start. Fortunately, navy blue groom suit ideas are a popular choice that can help simplify the process. In this post, we'll explore the benefits of choosing a navy blue suit for your wedding day and provide some inspiration to help you create a stylish and cohesive look.

When it comes to wedding attire, the groom often gets overshadowed by the bride. However, choosing the right suit is just as important for the groom as it is for the bride's dress. A well-fitted suit can help the groom feel confident and stylish, while also complementing the overall aesthetic of the wedding. Unfortunately, selecting a suit can also be stressful, especially for grooms who may not have a lot of experience in formal wear. This is where navy blue groom suit ideas can be helpful.

If you're considering a navy blue suit for your wedding day, you're in luck. Navy is a versatile and classic color that can be dressed up or down depending on the formality of your wedding. It's also a great alternative to traditional black or grey suits, which can sometimes feel too formal or stuffy. Additionally, navy blue suits look great on a variety of skin tones and can be paired with a range of accessories to create a cohesive look.

To summarize, choosing a navy blue suit for your wedding day can help simplify the process of selecting a suit while also providing a stylish and versatile option. Now, let's explore some specific navy blue groom suit ideas to help you create your ideal look.

1. The Classic Navy Suit

For a timeless and elegant look, consider a classic navy suit. This style works well for both formal and semi-formal weddings and can be paired with a range of accessories to create your desired aesthetic. To add a touch of personality, consider choosing a suit with a subtle pattern or texture, such as a herringbone or pinstripe.

Personally, my husband opted for a classic navy suit for our wedding day. He paired it with a white dress shirt, black dress shoes, and a navy blue tie. The result was a clean and sophisticated look that complemented my white wedding dress perfectly.

2. The Navy Blue Tuxedo

If you're looking to add a touch of glamour to your wedding day, consider a navy blue tuxedo. This style is a more formal option that works well for black-tie weddings or evening events. To keep the look cohesive, consider pairing the tuxedo with black dress shoes and a black bow tie.

For my sister's wedding, her husband opted for a navy blue tuxedo. He paired it with a white dress shirt, black patent leather dress shoes, and a black bow tie. The result was a sophisticated and stylish look that was perfect for their evening wedding reception.

3. Navy Blue Suit with Brown Shoes

If you're looking to add some warmth and texture to your wedding day look, consider pairing a navy blue suit with brown dress shoes. This combination creates a stylish contrast that can help elevate your overall look. To keep the look cohesive, consider adding a brown leather belt and a patterned tie or pocket square.

For a friend's wedding, the groom opted for a navy blue suit paired with brown dress shoes. He also added a brown leather belt and a floral patterned tie to complete the look. The result was a stylish and modern look that complemented the rustic decor of their outdoor wedding venue.

4. Navy Blue Suit with Grey Vest

If you're looking to add some dimension to your navy blue suit, consider pairing it with a grey vest. This combination creates a stylish contrast that can help break up the monotony of a single color. To keep the look cohesive, consider pairing the suit with a white dress shirt and a navy blue tie.

For a client's wedding, the groom opted for a navy blue suit paired with a grey vest. He also added a navy blue tie and brown dress shoes to complete the look. The result was a stylish and modern look that complemented the industrial decor of their wedding venue.

Question and Answer

Q: Can navy blue suits work for outdoor weddings?

A: Yes, navy blue suits can work well for outdoor weddings. The color is versatile enough to be dressed up or down depending on the formality of the event. Consider pairing the suit with brown dress shoes and a patterned tie to create a cohesive look that complements the natural surroundings.

Q: What color shirt should I wear with a navy blue suit?

A: A white dress shirt is a classic option that works well with navy blue suits. However, you can also experiment with other colors such as light blue or grey to create a more personalized look.

Q: Can I wear a navy blue suit to a black-tie wedding?

A: Yes, a navy blue tuxedo can be a stylish alternative to a traditional black tuxedo. Pair the suit with black dress shoes and a black bow tie to create a cohesive and formal look.

Q: How can I add some personality to my navy blue suit?

A: Consider accessorizing your navy blue suit with patterned ties, pocket squares, or cufflinks. You can also experiment with different textures such as velvet or tweed to add some dimension to your look.

Conclusion of Navy Blue Groom Suit Ideas

Choosing a suit for your wedding day can be a daunting task, but navy blue groom suit ideas can help simplify the process. By opting for a navy blue suit, you can create a stylish and versatile look that complements a range of wedding aesthetics. Whether you choose a classic navy suit or a navy blue tuxedo, there are plenty of ways to add your own personal flair to your wedding day look. By following these navy blue groom suit ideas, you'll be sure to look sharp and sophisticated on your big day.
