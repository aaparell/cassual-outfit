Types of African Clothing

African clothing comes in many different styles and designs. Some of the more popular types of African clothing include:

  • Dashiki - a loose-fitting tunic worn by men and women
  • Kaftans - a long, loose-fitting robe worn by men and women
  • Boubou - a long, flowing robe worn by men and women
  • Kitenge - a colorful fabric often used for dresses, skirts, and blouses
  • Kente - a vibrant, colorful fabric often used for formal wear
