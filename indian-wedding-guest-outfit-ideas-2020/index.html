Indian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and opulence. They are a perfect blend of traditions and modernity, making them an unforgettable experience. However, choosing the right outfit for a wedding can be a daunting task, especially for guests. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what to wear. In this blog post, we will provide you with some Indian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020 that will make you stand out from the crowd.

Attending an Indian wedding can be a great experience, but it can also be a stressful one. With so many ceremonies, events, and traditions, it can be challenging to figure out what to wear. Moreover, you have to take into account the theme, the venue, and the season. All of these factors can make the process of choosing an outfit overwhelming for guests.

If you are attending an Indian wedding and are unsure about what to wear, don't worry. We have got you covered. In this blog post, we will provide you with some Indian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020 that will help you look your best and feel confident.

In summary, Indian weddings are a celebration of love, traditions, and culture. Choosing the right outfit for such an occasion can be a challenging task, but with our Indian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020, you will be able to find the perfect outfit that will make you stand out from the crowd.

Outfit Idea 1: Saree

I have always been a fan of sarees. They are elegant, timeless, and can be styled in many different ways. A saree is a perfect choice for an Indian wedding, especially if you want to embrace the traditional side of the occasion. There are many different types of sarees available, such as Banarasi, Kanjeevaram, and Chanderi. You can choose a saree that suits your personality, style, and budget.

Outfit Idea 2: Lehenga

Lehenga is another popular choice for Indian weddings. It is a versatile outfit that can be worn for both traditional and modern weddings. A lehenga consists of a long skirt, a blouse, and a dupatta. It is available in many different colors, designs, and fabrics. You can choose a lehenga that complements your body type and skin tone.

Outfit Idea 3: Anarkali

Anarkali is a traditional outfit that has made a comeback in recent years. It is a long frock-style dress that is paired with a dupatta. Anarkalis are available in many different styles, such as floor-length, knee-length, and asymmetrical. They are perfect for those who want to look elegant and sophisticated.

How to Accessorize

Accessories are an essential part of any outfit. They can elevate your look and add a touch of glamour. When it comes to Indian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020, you can never go wrong with statement jewelry. A pair of chandelier earrings, a maang tikka, or a statement necklace can add a pop of color and sparkle to your outfit. You can also carry a clutch or a potli bag to complete your look.

Outfit Idea 4: Sharara

Sharara is another traditional outfit that has gained popularity in recent years. It consists of a long top, a pair of wide-legged pants, and a dupatta. Shararas are available in many different styles, such as embroidered, printed, and embellished. They are perfect for those who want to look chic and trendy.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to an Indian wedding?

A: It is not recommended to wear black to an Indian wedding. Black is traditionally associated with mourning and is considered inauspicious.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: Jeans are not considered appropriate for an Indian wedding. It is best to opt for traditional outfits such as sarees, lehengas, or shararas.

Q: Can I wear a sleeveless blouse to an Indian wedding?

A: It is best to avoid sleeveless blouses for an Indian wedding. You can opt for a three-quarter or full-sleeved blouse instead.

Q: Can I wear white to an Indian wedding?

A: White is not traditionally worn to Indian weddings as it is associated with mourning. However, you can opt for off-white, cream, or beige.

Conclusion of Indian Wedding Guest Outfit Ideas 2020

Attending an Indian wedding can be a memorable experience, and choosing the right outfit is crucial. With our Indian wedding guest outfit ideas for 2020, you can find the perfect outfit that suits your style, personality, and budget. Whether you choose a saree, lehenga, anarkali, or sharara, don't forget to accessorize with statement jewelry and a clutch. With these tips in mind, you will be able to make a lasting impression at any Indian wedding you attend.
