What Booties To Wear With Dresses A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying on different booties with your dresses and never knowing which one looks best? Do you spend hours scrolling through social media to find inspiration, only to end up more confused? If so, you’re not alone!

Choosing the right booties to wear with dresses can be a daunting task. You want to look stylish and put-together, but you also want to feel comfortable and confident in your outfit.

In this guide, we’ll answer the question of what booties to wear with dresses and provide you with tips and ideas to help you create the perfect look for any occasion.

So, let’s dive in!

What Booties to Wear with Dresses: Casual Look

When it comes to creating a casual look with booties and dresses, the key is to keep it simple. Opt for ankle booties in neutral colors such as black, brown, or beige, and pair them with a flowy dress or a denim dress. This will create a relaxed, yet stylish look that you can wear on a day out with friends or running errands.

Personally, I love wearing my brown ankle booties with a white flowy dress. It’s a comfortable and effortless look that makes me feel great and confident.

What Booties to Wear with Dresses: Formal Look

If you’re attending a formal event, you can still wear booties with your dress. The key is to choose a dressier pair of booties, such as those made of leather or suede, and pair them with a dress in a similar color or a complementary shade.

For example, you could wear black suede booties with a black dress or brown leather booties with a green dress. This will create a sophisticated and elegant look that’s perfect for a wedding or a dinner party.

I recently attended a wedding where I wore my black leather booties with a navy blue dress. The combination was a hit, and I received so many compliments on my look.

How to Choose the Right Booties for Your Dress

When choosing booties to wear with dresses, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider the occasion: Is it a casual or formal event?
  • Choose the right color: Neutral colors such as black, brown, and beige are versatile and can be paired with any dress color.
  • Think about the length of the dress: Ankle booties work best with shorter dresses, while knee-high boots are better suited for longer dresses.
  • Consider the style of the booties: Do you want something casual or dressy? Do you prefer leather or suede?

How to Style Your Booties with Dresses

Now that you’ve chosen the right booties for your dress, it’s time to style them. Here are some tips:

  • Pair ankle booties with a flowy dress or a denim dress for a casual look.
  • Choose knee-high boots for a more formal look.
  • Match the color of your booties to your dress or choose a complementary shade.
  • Accessorize with a belt or a statement necklace to complete your outfit.

What Booties to Wear with Dresses: Trendy Look

If you’re looking to try something new and trendy, consider pairing your dress with white booties or animal print booties. These statement pieces will add a pop of color and texture to your outfit and make you stand out.

I recently bought a pair of snake print booties that I wear with my black midi dress. It’s a bold look that I love, and I always receive compliments on my boots.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear ankle booties with a maxi dress?

A: Yes, you can wear ankle booties with a maxi dress. Choose a pair of booties that are low-cut and have a pointed toe to elongate your legs. Make sure the dress doesn’t cover the top of the booties.

Q: Can I wear knee-high boots with a mini dress?

A: Yes, knee-high boots look great with a mini dress. Choose a pair of boots with a platform heel to balance out the short length of the dress.

Q: Can I wear suede booties with a summer dress?

A: Yes, you can wear suede booties with a summer dress. Opt for lighter colors such as beige or taupe and pair them with a floral or pastel dress.

Q: Can I wear white booties with a black dress?

A: Yes, white booties can be paired with a black dress. This will create a striking contrast and make your outfit stand out.

Conclusion of What Booties to Wear with Dresses

Choosing the right booties to wear with dresses can be a challenge, but with the right tips and ideas, you can create a stylish and comfortable look for any occasion. Consider the occasion, color, length, and style of the booties, and accessorize with a belt or statement necklace to complete your outfit. Whether you’re going for a casual, formal, or trendy look, there’s a pair of booties out there that will work perfectly with your dress.
