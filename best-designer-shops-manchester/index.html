Harvey Nichols

Harvey Nichols is a luxury department store located in the city center of Manchester. It boasts an impressive range of designer brands such as Gucci, Saint Laurent, and Balenciaga, making it the perfect destination for those who want to indulge in high-end fashion. With its elegant decor and attentive staff, Harvey Nichols offers an unparalleled shopping experience.

Flannels

Flannels is a luxury boutique store located in the heart of Manchester's city center. It offers a unique and exclusive shopping experience, with a range of designer brands such as Off-White, Givenchy, and Versace. During my visit to Flannels, I was impressed with the range of designer pieces available. The staff were knowledgeable about the brands and offered helpful styling advice. The store's exclusive and intimate atmosphere made me feel like a VIP.

Oi Polloi

Oi Polloi is an independent boutique store located in the Northern Quarter of Manchester. It offers a range of unique and eclectic designer pieces that are perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd. During my visit to Oi Polloi, I was impressed with the range of designer pieces available. The store's staff were approachable and friendly, offering helpful styling advice. The store's unique selection of designer pieces made me feel like I had discovered a hidden gem in the city.

COW Manchester is a sustainable and ethical fashion store located in the Northern Quarter of Manchester. It offers a range of designer pieces that are both stylish and environmentally conscious.
