NIFT Fashion Designing Eligibility: Everything You Need to Know

Are you interested in pursuing a career in fashion designing? Do you want to get admitted to the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)? If so, you may have some questions about NIFT fashion designing eligibility. This article will provide you with all the information you need to know about NIFT fashion designing eligibility requirements, so that you can take the first step towards your dream career.

The eligibility criteria for NIFT fashion designing may seem daunting, but it is necessary to ensure that only the most talented and qualified candidates are selected. The eligibility criteria include age limits, educational qualifications, and other requirements that must be met before you can apply for admission.

If you want to pursue a Bachelor's Degree in Fashion Design, you must have completed your 10+2 examination from a recognized board. For Master's Degree in Fashion Design, you must have a Bachelor's Degree in Fashion Design or any other equivalent degree such as BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts), or a diploma in fashion design. The age limit for undergraduate courses is 23 years, while for postgraduate courses, it is 28 years. However, there is a relaxation of five years for candidates belonging to SC/ST/PWD categories.
