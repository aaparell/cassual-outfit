Where Can I Sell Men s Dress Shoes

Written by Christine Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to make some extra cash by selling your old men's dress shoes? Maybe you're hoping to declutter your closet or just need to make room for new shoes. Whatever your reason, selling your dress shoes can be a great way to make some money. But where can you sell them?

If you've never sold men's dress shoes before, you might feel a bit overwhelmed. There are so many different places to sell your shoes, and each option has its pros and cons. You might be wondering if you should sell them online or in person, or if you should go through a consignment shop or sell them yourself.

Fortunately, there are plenty of options available when it comes to selling men's dress shoes. In this post, we'll explore some of the most popular places to sell your shoes, the pros and cons of each option, and some tips for getting the most money for your shoes.

Online Marketplaces

One of the most popular places to sell men's dress shoes is online marketplaces like eBay or Amazon. These platforms allow you to list your shoes for sale and reach a large audience of potential buyers. However, there are some downsides to selling on these platforms. For example, you'll need to pay a fee to list your shoes, and you'll also need to ship them to the buyer once they sell. Additionally, you'll need to be prepared to deal with returns and customer service issues.

Personally, I have had a lot of success selling men's dress shoes on eBay. I find that I can get a good price for my shoes, and I like the convenience of being able to sell from home. However, it's important to do your research and make sure you're pricing your shoes competitively.

Consignment Shops

Another option for selling men's dress shoes is to go through a consignment shop. These shops will typically take a percentage of the sale price in exchange for selling your shoes. The benefit of going through a consignment shop is that they will handle all of the sales and customer service for you. However, you might not get as much money for your shoes as you would if you sold them yourself.

I've had mixed experiences with consignment shops. Some shops have been great to work with and have sold my shoes quickly, while others have been slow to sell my shoes or have taken a large percentage of the sale price.

Social Media

Another option for selling men's dress shoes is to use social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram. You can post pictures of your shoes and let your friends and followers know that they're for sale. This option is convenient because you don't need to pay any fees to sell your shoes, and you can sell them to people that you know and trust.

I've sold a few pairs of shoes on Facebook before, and I found it to be a pretty easy process. However, you'll need to be prepared to handle the logistics of selling, such as coordinating payment and shipping.

Tips for Selling Men's Dress Shoes

No matter where you decide to sell your men's dress shoes, there are a few tips that can help you get the most money for your shoes:

  • Clean your shoes before you sell them. Nobody wants to buy dirty shoes!
  • Take good pictures of your shoes. You'll want to make sure that potential buyers can see all of the details of your shoes.
  • Be honest about the condition of your shoes. Don't try to hide any flaws or damage.
  • Price your shoes competitively. Do your research and see what other people are selling similar shoes for.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I sell my shoes on multiple platforms at once?

A: Yes, you can sell your shoes on multiple platforms at once. However, you'll need to make sure that you're keeping track of your listings and taking them down as soon as your shoes sell.

Q: What types of shoes sell the best?

A: Generally, men's dress shoes from high-end brands like Allen Edmonds or Johnston & Murphy will sell for the most money. However, there is also a market for vintage or unique shoes.

Q: Do I need to have the original box for my shoes?

A: While having the original box for your shoes can make them more valuable, it's not always necessary. As long as your shoes are in good condition and are accurately described, you should be able to sell them without the box.

Q: How should I ship my shoes?

A: When shipping your shoes, you'll want to make sure that they're protected from damage. Use a sturdy box and plenty of packing material to cushion the shoes. You should also consider using a shipping service that provides tracking and insurance.

Conclusion of Where Can I Sell Men's Dress Shoes

Selling your men's dress shoes can be a great way to make some extra money and declutter your closet. Whether you decide to sell online, through a consignment shop, or on social media, be sure to follow these tips to get the most money for your shoes.
