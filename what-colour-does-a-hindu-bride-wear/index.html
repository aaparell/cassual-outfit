The colour of the bride's attire is one of the major pain points for many people. Everyone wants to know what colour does a Hindu bride wear on her wedding day. There are several reasons behind this, including cultural and religious beliefs.

In Hindu culture, the most common colour for a bride's wedding outfit is red. Red is considered a symbol of love, purity, and prosperity.
