Do you have a bunch of clothes in your closet that you no longer wear? Are you looking to make some extra cash by selling them? If yes, then you might be wondering how much money do consignment shops give you for clothes. This is a common question among people who are considering selling their clothes through a consignment shop, and it's important to understand how the process works so you can make the most informed decision.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to selling clothes through a consignment shop is that you might not get as much money as you would like for your items. Consignment shops typically take a percentage of the selling price as their commission, which means you won't receive the full price for your items. Additionally, the amount of money you receive will depend on the demand for your items, the condition they are in, and the type of clothing you are selling.

The amount of money you can expect to receive from a consignment shop will vary depending on the store and their policies. Generally, consignment shops will give you between 30-50% of the selling price for your items. This means that if your item sells for $100, you can expect to receive between $30-$50. It's important to note that some consignment shops may offer a higher percentage if you have high-end designer clothing or if you bring in a large quantity of items.

In summary, when it comes to how much money do consignment shops give you for clothes, the answer is that it varies. You can expect to receive between 30-50% of the selling price for your items, but this will depend on the store and the demand for your items.
