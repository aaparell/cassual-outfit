Compression pants are a popular choice of clothing for athletes, gym-goers, and those who want to improve their blood circulation. However, one question that often arises is whether or not to wear underwear under compression pants. It can be a tricky question to answer, but in this article, we will explore the topic in detail and provide you with an answer.

Many people may feel uncomfortable going commando under compression pants, but wearing underwear can also cause discomfort and chafing. This can be a pain point for many people who want to wear compression pants but are unsure about whether they should wear underwear or not.

The answer to this question is simple - it is entirely up to you. Wearing underwear under compression pants is a matter of personal preference. Some people find it more comfortable to wear underwear, while others prefer to go without. It depends on how comfortable you feel and what makes you feel the most confident.

In summary, whether or not to wear underwear under compression pants is a personal choice. There is no right or wrong answer, and it depends on what you feel comfortable with. However, there are a few things to consider before deciding.

Why Do People Wear Compression Pants?

Compression pants are designed to improve blood circulation, reduce muscle fatigue and soreness, and enhance athletic performance. They are also worn as a fashion statement and provide support and comfort during workouts.

Personally, I always wear compression pants without underwear. I find it more comfortable and less restrictive during workouts. However, it is essential to choose the right type of compression pants that fit well and provide enough support.

What Are the Pros and Cons of Wearing Underwear Under Compression Pants?

One advantage of wearing underwear under compression pants is that it can provide an extra layer of protection against chafing and irritation. It can also help with sweat absorption and keeping you feeling fresh during workouts. However, underwear can also bunch up and cause discomfort, especially during intense exercise.

What Type of Underwear Should You Wear with Compression Pants?

If you decide to wear underwear under compression pants, it is essential to choose the right type of underwear. Avoid cotton underwear as it does not wick away sweat and can cause discomfort. Opt for moisture-wicking, breathable underwear made from synthetic materials like nylon or polyester.

How to Prevent Chafing When Wearing Underwear Under Compression Pants?

To prevent chafing when wearing underwear under compression pants, apply an anti-chafing balm or cream to your inner thighs and groin area. You can also wear compression shorts or briefs that have a built-in anti-chafing layer.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, whether or not to wear underwear under compression pants is a personal choice. It depends on what makes you feel comfortable and confident during workouts. If you do decide to wear underwear, choose the right type of moisture-wicking, breathable underwear that fits well and provides enough support. Remember to take care of your skin by applying anti-chafing balm or cream to prevent irritation and discomfort.
