What To Wear To A Country Club Wedding 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a wedding can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to deciding what to wear. And when it's a country club wedding, the confusion only gets amplified. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll guide you through what to wear to a country club wedding in 2021.

The first thing that comes to mind when deciding what to wear to a country club wedding is the dress code. Generally, country club weddings have a formal dress code, which means you need to dress up. But, at the same time, you don't want to overdress and look out of place. So, what do you do?

Firstly, make sure you check the invitation for any specific dress code instructions. If there are none, you can assume that the dress code is formal. For men, a dark suit with a tie is the perfect choice. Women can wear a formal cocktail dress or a floor-length gown. But, make sure you don't go overboard with the accessories and keep them minimal.

To sum up, when deciding what to wear to a country club wedding in 2021, it's essential to keep the dress code in mind. Choose a formal outfit, but not something that looks too overdressed. Keep the accessories minimal, and you're good to go.

Accessorizing for a Country Club Wedding

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and when it comes to a country club wedding, it's crucial to get them right. Here's my personal experience:

At my friend's country club wedding, I wore a simple black cocktail dress that fit me perfectly. But, I felt like something was missing, so I added a statement necklace and a pair of earrings. The accessories elevated my outfit, making it look more put together and elegant. So, when accessorizing for a country club wedding, don't be afraid to add a statement piece, but make sure it complements your outfit.

Shoes for a Country Club Wedding

Choosing the right pair of shoes is crucial when it comes to attending a country club wedding. You'll be on your feet for an extended period, so comfort should be a priority. But, that doesn't mean you have to compromise on style. Here are my recommendations:

For women, a pair of comfortable heels or wedges would be perfect. But, if you're not comfortable wearing heels, opt for dressy flats or sandals. For men, a classic pair of leather dress shoes would be perfect. Make sure they're polished and match your suit.

Colors to Avoid at a Country Club Wedding

When it comes to choosing colors for a country club wedding, it's essential to avoid certain colors that may be deemed inappropriate. Here are some colors you should avoid:

- White or Ivory (unless you're the bride)
- Black (unless it's an evening wedding)
- Red (it's too bold and attention-seeking)
- Neon colors (they're too casual and not appropriate for a formal event)

What to Wear to a Daytime Country Club Wedding

If the country club wedding you're attending is during the day, you can opt for a lighter-colored suit or a floral dress. Men can ditch the tie and go for a more relaxed look. But, remember, it's still a formal event, so make sure you're not too casual.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Country Club Wedding 2021

Attending a country club wedding can be stressful, but it doesn't have to be. Keep the dress code in mind, accessorize wisely, choose the right shoes, and avoid inappropriate colors. With these tips, you're sure to look your best at the next country club wedding you attend.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I wear a jumpsuit to a country club wedding?

A. It depends on the dress code mentioned on the invitation. If it's a formal wedding, it's better to stick to a dress or a gown. But, if the dress code is semi-formal, you can wear a jumpsuit.

Q. Can I wear jeans to a country club wedding?

A. Absolutely not. A country club wedding is a formal event, and jeans are too casual for the occasion.

Q. Can I wear a hat to a country club wedding?

A. Unless it's a themed wedding or the invitation specifically mentions hats, it's better to avoid wearing one. Hats can be a bit distracting and take the attention away from the bride and groom.

Q. Can I wear a tuxedo to a country club wedding?

A. Yes, you can wear a tuxedo to a country club wedding. It's a formal event, and a tuxedo is the perfect choice for men.
