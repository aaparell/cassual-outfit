What Kind Of Pants Were Popular In The 80S

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you curious about the fashion trends of the past? Do you ever wonder what kind of pants were popular in the 80s? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will take a trip down memory lane and explore the fashion trends of the 80s, specifically the popular pants styles.

The 80s was a decade of bold and colorful fashion. It was a time when people expressed themselves through their clothing choices. However, not everyone was a fan of the fashion trends of the 80s. Some people found it difficult to keep up with the ever-changing styles, while others simply did not like the bold and loud designs.

The most popular pants styles of the 80s were the high-waisted jeans, parachute pants, and acid-washed jeans. These styles were seen everywhere, from the streets to the runways, and were worn by both men and women.

To summarize, the three main pants styles that were popular in the 80s were high-waisted jeans, parachute pants, and acid-washed jeans. These styles were bold, colorful, and expressive, and were a reflection of the fashion trends of the decade.

High-Waisted Jeans

High-waisted jeans were a staple of the 80s fashion. They were popular among both men and women and were often paired with crop tops, oversized sweaters, or blazers. High-waisted jeans were designed to sit above the waistline, giving the illusion of longer legs. They were often made of denim or corduroy and came in a variety of colors.

Personally, I remember wearing high-waisted jeans during my high school days. I loved how they made my legs look longer and how they accentuated my curves. I would often pair them with a crop top and a denim jacket for a classic 80s look.

Parachute Pants

Parachute pants were another popular pants style of the 80s. They were originally designed for breakdancers but soon became a fashion trend. Parachute pants were made of lightweight and durable nylon and often came in bright colors such as neon green, pink, and orange. They featured a baggy fit with tapered ankles and were often paired with high-top sneakers.

I remember my brother wearing parachute pants in the 80s. He was a breakdancer and loved the comfort and flexibility that the pants offered. He would often wear them to dance battles and performances.

Acid-Washed Jeans

Acid-washed jeans were a popular trend in the 80s. They were made by washing denim with bleach and were often paired with oversized sweatshirts or denim jackets. Acid-washed jeans came in a variety of styles, including skinny, straight-leg, and baggy.

Personally, I was not a fan of acid-washed jeans. I found them too loud and bold for my taste. However, many people loved the trend and wore them as a statement piece.

How to Style 80s Pants Today

The 80s pants styles have made a comeback in recent years. High-waisted jeans, parachute pants, and acid-washed jeans are now being worn by a new generation of fashion enthusiasts. To style these pants today, you can pair them with modern pieces such as crop tops, oversized blazers, or graphic tees. You can also add a modern twist to the 80s look by wearing sneakers or boots.

Conclusion of What Kind of Pants Were Popular in the 80s

The 80s was a decade of bold and colorful fashion, and the pants styles of the time reflected that. High-waisted jeans, parachute pants, and acid-washed jeans were the most popular pants styles of the 80s. Although the fashion trends of the 80s may not be for everyone, they were a reflection of the cultural and social changes of the decade.

Question and Answer

A: Other popular pants styles of the 80s included leggings, stirrup pants, and tapered jeans.

Q: Were men and women wearing the same pants styles in the 80s?

A: Yes, high-waisted jeans, parachute pants, and acid-washed jeans were popular among both men and women in the 80s.

Q: Why were high-waisted jeans so popular in the 80s?

A: High-waisted jeans were popular in the 80s because they elongated the legs and accentuated the waistline.

Q: Can you still buy 80s pants styles today?

A: Yes, many fashion brands have brought back 80s pants styles in recent years, and they are now available for purchase online and in stores.
