Q: What is the difference between a square toe and a pointed toe cowboy boot?

A: The main difference between these two styles is in the toe shape. Square toe cowboy boots have a more angular, boxy shape, while pointed toe boots have a more tapered, narrow shape. Both styles are popular choices for men's cowboy boots.

Q: Can square toe cowboy boots be worn with dress pants?

A: While cowboy boots are traditionally seen as casual footwear, they can certainly be dressed up for more formal occasions. Square toe cowboy boots look great with dress pants or a suit, especially when paired with a matching belt.

Q: How do I care for my square toe cowboy boots?
