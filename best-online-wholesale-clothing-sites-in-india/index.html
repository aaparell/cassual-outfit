Best Online Wholesale Clothing Sites In India

Do you want to start a clothing business or expand it? Are you looking for the best online wholesale clothing sites in India? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the top online wholesale clothing sites that offer quality products at affordable prices.

As a clothing business owner, finding the right supplier can be a challenging task. You need a supplier that offers a wide range of products, good quality, and competitive prices. Moreover, you need a supplier that can deliver the products on time and meet your business requirements.

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of the best online wholesale clothing sites in India that can fulfill your business needs.

In summary, this article will discuss the best online wholesale clothing sites in India, their features, and how to choose the right supplier for your business needs.

1. Wholesalebox

Wholesalebox is one of the best online wholesale clothing sites in India. It offers a wide range of clothing for men, women, and kids. Moreover, it provides accessories, footwear, and cosmetics at wholesale prices. The site offers various payment options, including COD, credit/debit cards, and net banking. The minimum order value is INR 5,000.

I have been using Wholesalebox for my clothing business for the past year, and I am satisfied with their products and services. The site is user-friendly, and the delivery is prompt. Moreover, their customer support is excellent.

2. Suratfabric

Suratfabric is another popular online wholesale clothing site in India. It specializes in ethnic wear, including sarees, salwar suits, lehengas, and kurtis. The site offers free shipping on orders above INR 999. Moreover, it provides worldwide shipping at affordable rates.

I have ordered sarees from Suratfabric for my boutique, and I am impressed with their quality and designs. The site offers a vast collection of sarees at reasonable prices. Furthermore, their customer support is responsive and helpful.

3. Textilebuzz

Textilebuzz is a leading online wholesale clothing site in India. It offers a wide range of clothing, including sarees, salwar suits, lehengas, kurtis, and gowns. Moreover, it provides fabrics and dress materials at wholesale prices. The site offers worldwide shipping at affordable rates.

I have ordered dress materials from Textilebuzz for my clothing business, and I am satisfied with their quality and prices. The site is easy to navigate, and the delivery is timely. Furthermore, their customer support is friendly and helpful.

4. Indiamart

Indiamart is a popular online marketplace that connects buyers and suppliers. It offers a vast range of products, including clothing, accessories, electronics, and machinery. The site provides secure payment options, and it guarantees buyer protection. Moreover, it offers customized solutions for businesses.

I have used Indiamart to find suppliers for my clothing business, and I am impressed with their services. The site has a vast network of suppliers, and it provides competitive prices. Moreover, their customer support is responsive and helpful.

  • Delivery time

  • Customer support

Suratfabric is the best online wholesale clothing site for ethnic wear. It offers a vast range of sarees, salwar suits, lehengas, and kurtis at reasonable prices.

3. Can I buy clothing in bulk from these sites?

Yes, you can buy clothing in bulk from these sites. They offer wholesale prices for bulk orders.

Yes, most of these sites provide worldwide shipping at affordable rates.

Conclusion of Best Online Wholesale Clothing Sites in India

Choosing the right supplier is essential for the success of your clothing business.
