Best Indian Mens Clothing Brands A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of bland and boring menswear options? Do you struggle to find stylish and high-quality Indian clothing brands for men? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the best Indian mens clothing brands that offer a perfect blend of tradition, modernity, and sophistication. Whether you are looking for casual wear, formal wear, or ethnic wear, we have got you covered.

Indian menswear is often overlooked or underrated, with limited options available in the market. However, there are several pain points that Indian men face when it comes to clothing. These can range from finding the right fit, to choosing the right fabric, to staying on top of the latest fashion trends. Moreover, Indian men often face a lack of variety and creativity when it comes to clothing options, with most brands offering generic styles.

The target of this guide is to provide a comprehensive overview of the best Indian mens clothing brands that cater to the diverse needs and preferences of Indian men. We have conducted extensive research and analysis to curate a list of the top brands that offer high-quality, stylish, and affordable menswear options.

In summary, this guide aims to provide a one-stop-shop for Indian men who are looking to revamp their wardrobe and upgrade their fashion game. We will explore the best Indian mens clothing brands that offer a perfect balance of style, comfort, and affordability, with a focus on various types of clothing, including casual wear, formal wear, ethnic wear, and accessories.

1. Manyavar

Manyavar is a popular Indian menswear brand that offers a wide range of ethnic wear options, including sherwanis, kurtas, jackets, and accessories. With over 600 stores across India, Manyavar has become a go-to destination for men who are looking for traditional yet stylish clothing options. The brand is known for its intricate designs, high-quality fabrics, and attention to detail.

Personally, I have had a great experience with Manyavar. I recently purchased a sherwani for my brother's wedding, and I was extremely impressed with the quality and fit of the outfit. The staff at the store were helpful and knowledgeable, and they helped me choose the perfect outfit for the occasion.

2. Raymond

Raymond is a well-known brand in India that offers a range of menswear options, including formal wear, casual wear, and accessories. The brand is known for its high-quality fabrics, expert craftsmanship, and attention to detail. Raymond has a strong reputation for its suits, shirts, and trousers, with a focus on classic styles that stand the test of time.

I have been a loyal customer of Raymond for several years now, and I have always been impressed with the quality and fit of their clothing. I recently purchased a new suit for a job interview, and I received several compliments on my outfit. The suit was comfortable, stylish, and affordable, making it a great investment for my wardrobe.

3. Fabindia

Fabindia is a unique Indian clothing brand that offers a range of ethnic wear options, including kurtas, pyjamas, and accessories. What sets Fabindia apart from other brands is its focus on sustainable and handcrafted clothing, with a commitment to supporting local artisans and craftsmen. Fabindia is also known for its use of natural fabrics, such as cotton, silk, and wool.

I recently purchased a kurta from Fabindia, and I was impressed with the quality and craftsmanship of the outfit. The fabric was soft and comfortable, and the design was intricate and unique. I appreciated the fact that the brand supports local artisans and promotes sustainable fashion practices.

4. Louis Philippe

Louis Philippe is a popular Indian brand that offers a range of formal wear options, including suits, shirts, trousers, and accessories. The brand is known for its classic styles, attention to detail, and high-quality fabrics. Louis Philippe offers a range of fits and sizes to cater to different body types and preferences.

I recently purchased a suit from Louis Philippe for a wedding, and I was impressed with the fit and quality of the outfit. The suit was comfortable and stylish, and it stood out from other generic options. I appreciated the fact that the brand offers a range of fits and sizes, making it easier to find the perfect outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What are the best Indian mens clothing brands for casual wear?

A: Some of the best Indian mens clothing brands for casual wear include Mufti, U.S. Polo Assn., and Levi's. These brands offer a range of stylish and comfortable options, including jeans, t-shirts, and jackets.

Q: What are the best Indian mens clothing brands for formal wear?

A: Some of the best Indian mens clothing brands for formal wear include Raymond, Louis Philippe, and Van Heusen. These brands offer a range of classic and timeless options, including suits, shirts, and trousers.

Q: What are the best Indian mens clothing brands for ethnic wear?

A: Some of the best Indian mens clothing brands for ethnic wear include Manyavar, Fabindia, and Biba. These brands offer a range of traditional and modern options, including sherwanis, kurtas, and pyjamas.

Q: What are the best Indian mens clothing brands for accessories?

A: Some of the best Indian mens clothing brands for accessories include Hidesign, Titan, and Fastrack. These brands offer a range of stylish and functional options, including watches, wallets, and bags.

Conclusion of Best Indian Mens Clothing Brands

In conclusion, Indian menswear has come a long way in recent years, with several brands offering high-quality, stylish, and affordable options. Whether you are looking for casual wear, formal wear, ethnic wear, or accessories, there are several brands that cater to your needs and preferences. By exploring the best Indian mens clothing brands, you can revamp your wardrobe and upgrade your fashion game. So, what are you waiting for? Start exploring these brands today!
