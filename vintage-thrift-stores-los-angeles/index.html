Discover The Best Vintage Thrift Stores In Los Angeles

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of vintage fashion and looking for the best thrift stores in Los Angeles? Do you want to find unique, one-of-a-kind pieces without breaking the bank? Vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles offer a wide range of fashion choices from different eras, making it an exciting experience for fashion enthusiasts.

If you're tired of spending too much money on new clothes or want to stand out from the crowd, vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles are the perfect solution. With a variety of styles and sizes, you can find clothes that fit your personality and budget.

Looking for vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles can be overwhelming, but with a little research, you can find the perfect store to fit your needs. In this post, we'll share our top picks for the best vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles, and what makes each store unique.

Wasteland

Wasteland is a popular vintage thrift store in Los Angeles, known for its unique selection of vintage and designer clothing. With locations in Studio City, Melrose, and Santa Monica, Wasteland offers a variety of styles for men and women, including vintage band t-shirts, leather jackets, and designer dresses.

Personally, I love shopping at Wasteland because of their diverse selection of vintage clothing. I always find something unique and affordable that I can't find anywhere else.

Squaresville

Squaresville is another great vintage thrift store in Los Angeles, located in Los Feliz. What sets Squaresville apart is its focus on vintage clothing from the 1950s to the 1980s. They have a vast selection of vintage dresses, skirts, and blouses, perfect for those looking for a retro look.

I had a great experience shopping at Squaresville, and the staff was friendly and helpful. They helped me find the perfect vintage dress for a themed party, and I received many compliments on it.

The Way We Wore

The Way We Wore is a high-end vintage thrift store in Los Angeles, located in Hollywood. With a focus on vintage designer clothing, The Way We Wore offers a range of styles from the 1920s to the 1980s. They also have a great selection of vintage accessories, including jewelry and hats.

While The Way We Wore may be on the pricier side, the quality of the clothing is exceptional. I love browsing their collection of vintage Chanel and Yves Saint Laurent pieces, even if I can't afford to buy them.

Tips for Vintage Thrift Store Shopping in Los Angeles

Shopping at vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles can be overwhelming, but with these tips, you'll be a pro in no time:

1. Don't be afraid to dig through the racks. You never know what hidden gems you might find.

2. Keep an open mind. Vintage clothing can be altered to fit your style and body type.

3. Check for any damages or stains before purchasing. Most vintage clothing is sold as-is, so it's important to inspect it carefully.

4. Go early. The best vintage finds often go quickly, so it's best to go when the store first opens.

Question and Answer

Q: Are vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles expensive?

A: It depends on the store. Some stores, like Wasteland, offer affordable options, while others, like The Way We Wore, are on the pricier side.

Q: What should I wear when shopping at vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles?

A: Wear comfortable clothing and shoes that are easy to take on and off. Avoid wearing anything too complicated, like a jumpsuit or dress with a lot of buttons or zippers.

Q: Can I sell my vintage clothing to thrift stores in Los Angeles?

A: It depends on the store. Some stores offer cash or store credit for gently used clothing, while others only accept donations.

Q: Can I find vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles that specialize in men's clothing?

A: Yes, many vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles offer a variety of men's clothing, including suits, jackets, and t-shirts.

Conclusion of Vintage Thrift Stores in Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a vintage thrift store lover's paradise. With so many stores to choose from, you're sure to find something that fits your style and budget. Whether you're looking for a retro dress, vintage band t-shirt, or unique accessories, vintage thrift stores in Los Angeles have it all. With a little bit of research and patience, you can find the perfect piece to add to your wardrobe.
