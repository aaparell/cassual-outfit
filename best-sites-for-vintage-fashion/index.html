The Best Sites For Vintage Fashion A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Petter Oct 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you love the style of bygone eras? Are you tired of the same old fast fashion trends? If so, vintage fashion may be just what you need to add some unique flair to your wardrobe. However, finding the best sites for vintage fashion can be a challenge. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the top sites for vintage fashion and help you find the perfect pieces to complement your personal style.

When it comes to shopping for vintage fashion, there are a few pain points that many people experience. One of the biggest challenges is finding pieces that are in good condition, as vintage clothing can be fragile and easily damaged. Additionally, it can be difficult to find pieces that fit well, especially if you're not familiar with the sizing of different eras. Finally, vintage fashion can be expensive, particularly for rare or sought-after pieces.

So, what are the best sites for vintage fashion? There are a number of great options out there, each with their own unique selection and style. Some of the most popular sites include Etsy, Beyond Retro, Rokit, and The RealReal. Let's take a closer look at what each of these sites has to offer.

Etsy

Etsy is a great starting point for anyone looking to dive into the world of vintage fashion. It's a marketplace that connects buyers with independent sellers from around the world, so there's a huge variety of vintage clothing available. You can find everything from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge-inspired pieces. Plus, since each seller is unique, you can often find one-of-a-kind items that you won't see anywhere else.

Personally, I've had great success finding vintage jewelry on Etsy. I love the fact that I can support independent sellers while also finding unique pieces that add a special touch to any outfit.

Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is a UK-based company that specializes in vintage clothing from the 1960s through the 1990s. They have a great selection of both men's and women's clothing, as well as accessories and footwear. Beyond Retro has a strong commitment to sustainability and ethical fashion, so you can feel good about your purchases knowing that they're environmentally conscious.

I recently purchased a pair of jeans from Beyond Retro and was really impressed with the quality of the denim. They fit perfectly and were in great condition, which can be hard to find with vintage clothing.

Rokit

Rokit is another UK-based company that has been around since the 1980s. They have a huge selection of vintage clothing, ranging from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge-inspired pieces. Rokit also has its own line of upcycled clothing, which takes vintage pieces and gives them a modern twist.

I love Rokit's selection of vintage denim, particularly their oversized jackets and high-waisted jeans. They also have a great selection of vintage band t-shirts, which are a staple in my wardrobe.

The RealReal

The RealReal is a luxury consignment website that specializes in high-end vintage fashion. They have a team of experts who authenticate every piece, so you can be sure that you're getting authentic designer items. While The RealReal can be expensive, it's a great option if you're looking for something specific or rare.

I've never personally purchased anything from The RealReal, but I've heard great things about their selection of vintage Chanel bags and other high-end accessories.

Tips for Shopping for Vintage Fashion

When shopping for vintage fashion, there are a few tips that can help you find the perfect pieces. First, make sure you know your measurements so you can find pieces that fit well. Vintage sizing can be different from modern sizing, so it's important to measure yourself and compare to the measurements listed for each item.

Additionally, don't be afraid to ask questions. Many vintage sellers are happy to provide additional information or photos if you're unsure about something. Finally, be patient. Finding the perfect vintage piece can take time, but it's worth it when you find something you love.

In Conclusion

Overall, there are plenty of great sites for vintage fashion out there. Whether you're looking for a unique piece of jewelry, a vintage band t-shirt, or a rare designer handbag, there's something for everyone. By following the tips we've outlined and being patient in your search, you're sure to find the perfect vintage pieces to add to your wardrobe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is vintage fashion expensive?

A: Vintage fashion can be expensive, particularly for rare or sought-after pieces. However, there are also plenty of affordable options out there, especially if you're willing to do some digging.

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is authentic?

A: Authenticating vintage fashion can be tricky, especially if you're not familiar with the brand or era. However, there are a few key things to look for, such as labels and tags, stitching, and materials. If you're unsure, it's always a good idea to ask the seller for additional information or photos.

Q: Can I return vintage clothing if it doesn't fit?

A: It depends on the seller and their return policy. Be sure to read the listing carefully and ask the seller any questions you may have before making your purchase.

Q: What should I look for when shopping for vintage denim?

A: When shopping for vintage denim, look for high-waisted styles, oversized jackets, and unique washes or distressing. Additionally, make sure to check the measurements carefully, as vintage denim can be tricky to size.

Conclusion of Best Sites for Vintage Fashion

In conclusion, vintage fashion is a great way to add some unique flair to your wardrobe. By shopping at some of the best sites for vintage fashion, such as Etsy, Beyond Retro, Rokit, and The RealReal, you're sure to find the perfect pieces to complement your personal style. Remember to take your time, know your measurements, and don't be afraid to ask questions. Happy shopping!
