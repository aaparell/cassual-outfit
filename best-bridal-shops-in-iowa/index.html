Best Bridal Shops In Iowa

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a bride-to-be in Iowa searching for the perfect wedding dress? With so many options available, finding the right bridal shop can be overwhelming. You want to find a shop that understands your style, budget, and vision for your big day.

At the same time, you don't want to spend hours traveling to different bridal shops or sifting through endless racks of dresses that aren't quite what you're looking for.

In this article, we'll guide you through the best bridal shops in Iowa to help make your search a little easier.

When it comes to finding the perfect wedding dress, it's important to choose a bridal shop that offers a wide range of styles, sizes, and prices. You want a shop that caters to your unique needs and preferences, whether you're looking for a traditional ballgown, a sleek and modern sheath dress, or something in between.

Some of the best bridal shops in Iowa that meet these criteria include:

1. Schaffer's

Located in Des Moines, Schaffer's is a family-owned bridal shop that has been helping brides find their dream dresses for over 70 years. They offer a wide range of styles from top designers like Allure Bridals, Maggie Sottero, and Justin Alexander, as well as custom designs. Schaffer's also offers in-house alterations to ensure a perfect fit.

2. Pure Bridal

With locations in Ames and Ankeny, Pure Bridal is a popular choice for Iowa brides. They offer a large selection of designer dresses from top brands like Stella York, Essense of Australia, and Martina Liana, as well as bridesmaid dresses and accessories. Pure Bridal also offers in-house alterations and has a team of experienced stylists to help you find the perfect dress.

3. Bridal Connection

Based in Ankeny, Bridal Connection is a full-service bridal shop that offers a wide range of styles and sizes. They carry dresses from top designers like Mori Lee, Maggie Sottero, and Casablanca Bridal, as well as bridesmaid dresses and tuxedo rentals. Bridal Connection also offers in-house alterations and has a team of experienced stylists to help you find the perfect dress.

If you're looking for a more personalized bridal shopping experience, there are also several boutique bridal shops throughout Iowa that offer a more intimate and personalized experience. These include:

4. Little White Dress Bridal Shop

Located in Cedar Rapids, Little White Dress Bridal Shop offers a curated selection of designer dresses from top brands like Hayley Paige, Liancarlo, and Anne Barge. They also offer custom design services and in-house alterations, as well as personalized styling appointments for a truly one-of-a-kind experience.

5. Modern Dress

With locations in Boone and Algona, Modern Dress is a boutique bridal shop that offers a unique selection of designer dresses, as well as bridesmaid dresses and accessories. They also offer in-house alterations and personalized styling appointments to help you find the perfect dress for your big day.

Choosing the Right Bridal Shop

When it comes to choosing the right bridal shop, there are a few key factors to consider. First and foremost, you want to find a shop that offers a wide range of styles, sizes, and prices to cater to your unique needs and preferences. You also want to find a shop with experienced stylists who can help you navigate the selection and find the perfect dress for your body type and personal style.

Other important factors to consider include the shop's reputation, customer reviews, and in-house alteration services. You want to choose a shop that has a good reputation for quality and customer service, as well as a team of experienced seamstresses to ensure a perfect fit.

Tips for a Successful Bridal Shopping Experience

Before you head out to your first bridal appointment, there are a few tips to keep in mind to ensure a successful shopping experience. First, do your research and come prepared with a list of styles and designers you're interested in. This can help your stylist narrow down the selection and find dresses that fit your personal style.

It's also important to keep an open mind and be willing to try on dresses outside of your comfort zone. You may be surprised by what looks great on you! And finally, don't forget to bring along any accessories or undergarments you plan to wear on your wedding day, such as a veil, shoes, or strapless bra.

Personal Experience

As a former Iowa bride, I had the pleasure of visiting several of the bridal shops mentioned above. I ultimately found my dream dress at Pure Bridal in Ankeny, thanks to the personalized attention and expertise of their stylists. The in-house alterations were also a huge help in ensuring a perfect fit.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I bring to my bridal appointment?

A: It's a good idea to bring along any accessories or undergarments you plan to wear on your wedding day, such as a veil, shoes, or strapless bra. You may also want to bring along pictures of dresses you like to help your stylist narrow down the selection.

Q: How many bridal appointments should I schedule?

A: It's a good idea to schedule at least two to three bridal appointments to get a feel for different styles and shops. However, you don't want to overwhelm yourself with too many appointments, as it can be exhausting and confusing to try on too many dresses at once.

Q: How far in advance should I start shopping for my wedding dress?

A: It's recommended to start shopping for your wedding dress at least six to nine months before your wedding date. This allows plenty of time for alterations and ensures you have enough time to find the perfect dress without feeling rushed.

Q: Do I need to make an appointment to try on dresses?

A: Yes, it's recommended to make an appointment at bridal shops to ensure a personalized and stress-free shopping experience. Walk-ins are often welcome, but appointments ensure that you have a dedicated stylist to help you find the perfect dress.

Conclusion of Best Bridal Shops in Iowa

Overall, Iowa has no shortage of amazing bridal shops to choose from. Whether you're looking for a wide range of styles and prices or a more personalized experience, there's a shop out there that will meet your unique needs and preferences. By doing your research, staying open-minded, and bringing along the right accessories, you're sure to find the dress of your dreams for your special day.
