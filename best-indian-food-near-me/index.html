Question and Answer

Q: What are some popular Indian dishes?

A: Some popular Indian dishes include butter chicken, biryani, samosas, dosas, tikka masala, and vindaloo.

Q: Is Indian food spicy?

A: Indian food is known for its spices, but not all dishes are spicy. Some dishes like biryani and butter chicken are mild, while others like vindaloo and tikka masala are spicy.

Q: What is the best way to eat Indian food?

A: Indian food is best enjoyed with your hands. Use the naan or roti to scoop up the curry or daal. It's also common to share dishes with your dining companions.

Q: Is Indian food healthy?

A: Indian food can be healthy if you choose the right dishes. Look for dishes that are grilled or baked instead of fried. Also, opt for dishes that are made with fresh ingredients and avoid dishes that are high in calories and saturated fat.
