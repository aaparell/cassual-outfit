Men s Indian Bridal Wear A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Oct 02, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to Indian weddings, the bride's attire is often given more importance than the groom's. However, it is equally important for the groom to look his best on his big day. Choosing the right wedding attire can be a daunting task for men, especially when it comes to Indian weddings. From traditional sherwanis to modern suits, the options are endless. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about men's Indian bridal wear.

The first pain point that comes to mind when it comes to men's Indian bridal wear is the lack of options. Often, men are limited to traditional sherwanis or suits. Another pain point is the confusion around what to wear for which occasion. Indian weddings are elaborate affairs with multiple ceremonies, each with its own dress code. It can be overwhelming for men to figure out what to wear for each ceremony.

The target of men's Indian bridal wear is to help grooms look their best on their wedding day. Whether you want to go for traditional attire or modern, there are options available for everyone. The key is to choose something that suits your personality and makes you feel confident.

In this article, we will cover everything from the different types of men's Indian bridal wear to the latest trends in groom's attire. We will also share some tips on how to choose the right attire for your wedding day.

Mens Indian Bridal Wear: Traditional vs Modern

When it comes to men's Indian bridal wear, there are two options to choose from: traditional and modern. Traditional attire includes sherwanis, kurtas, bandhgalas, and dhoti-kurta sets. These outfits are usually made from rich fabrics like silk, brocade, and velvet and are heavily embellished with embroidery and embellishments.

On the other hand, modern attire includes suits, tuxedos, and Indo-western outfits. These outfits are more contemporary and are made from lighter fabrics like linen and cotton. They are also less embellished and have a more minimalist look.

When it comes to choosing between traditional and modern attire, the key is to choose something that suits your personality and makes you feel comfortable. If you are someone who likes to stick to tradition, then traditional attire is the way to go. However, if you prefer a more modern look, then you can opt for an Indo-western outfit or a suit.

Sherwanis: The Classic Choice

Sherwanis are the classic choice when it comes to men's Indian bridal wear. They are a long coat-like garment that is usually worn with a churidar or dhoti. Sherwanis are made from rich fabrics like silk, brocade, and velvet and are heavily embellished with embroidery and embellishments.

When it comes to choosing a sherwani, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, choose a fabric that is comfortable and suitable for the weather. If you are getting married in the summer, then opt for lighter fabrics like cotton or linen. Secondly, choose a color that complements your skin tone. Sherwanis are available in a range of colors, from pastels to bright hues. Lastly, choose an embellishment that suits your style. Sherwanis are available in a range of embellishments, from simple embroidery to heavy embellishments.

Suits: The Modern Choice

Suits are a modern choice when it comes to men's Indian bridal wear. They are a versatile option that can be worn for both the wedding and the reception. Suits are usually made from lighter fabrics like cotton and linen and have a more minimalist look.

When it comes to choosing a suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, choose a color that complements your skin tone. Suits are available in a range of colors, from classic black to pastel shades. Secondly, choose a fit that flatters your body type. Suits come in a range of fits, from slim fit to regular fit. Lastly, choose accessories that complement your suit. A tie, pocket square, and cufflinks can add a touch of sophistication to your look.

Indo-Western Outfits: The Best of Both Worlds

Indo-western outfits are a fusion of Indian and western wear. They are a modern alternative to traditional Indian wear and are perfect for grooms who want to experiment with their look. Indo-western outfits are usually made from lighter fabrics like linen and cotton and have a more minimalist look.

When it comes to choosing an Indo-western outfit, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, choose a color that complements your skin tone. Indo-western outfits are available in a range of colors, from classic black to pastel shades. Secondly, choose a fit that flatters your body type. Indo-western outfits come in a range of fits, from slim fit to regular fit. Lastly, choose accessories that complement your outfit. A scarf, brooch, or turban can add a touch of elegance to your look.

How to Choose the Right Men's Indian Bridal Wear

Choosing the right men's Indian bridal wear can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you choose the right attire for your wedding day:

1. Consider the dress code for each ceremony. Indian weddings are elaborate affairs with multiple ceremonies, each with its own dress code. Make sure you choose an outfit that is appropriate for each ceremony.

2. Choose a fabric that is comfortable and suitable for the weather. If you are getting married in the summer, then opt for lighter fabrics like cotton or linen.

3. Choose a color that complements your skin tone. Indian wear is available in a range of colors, from pastels to bright hues.

4. Choose a fit that flatters your body type. Indian wear comes in a range of fits, from slim fit to regular fit.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I wear a suit for an Indian wedding?

A. Yes, you can wear a suit for an Indian wedding. Suits are a modern alternative to traditional Indian wear and are perfect for grooms who want to experiment with their look.

Q. What is the difference between a sherwani and a kurta?

A. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is usually worn with a churidar or dhoti. A kurta is a long shirt that is usually worn with pyjamas or jeans. Sherwanis are more elaborate and heavily embellished than kurtas.

Q. Can I wear an Indo-western outfit for an Indian wedding?

A. Yes, you can wear an Indo-western outfit for an Indian wedding. Indo-western outfits are a fusion of Indian and western wear and are a modern alternative to traditional Indian wear.

Q. What accessories should I wear with my men's Indian bridal wear?

A. Accessories can add a touch of sophistication to your men's Indian bridal wear. A scarf, brooch, or turban can add a touch of elegance to your look. A tie, pocket square, and cufflinks can add a touch of sophistication to your suit.

Conclusion of Men's Indian Bridal Wear

Choosing the right men's Indian bridal wear can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a fun and enjoyable experience. Whether you opt for traditional attire or modern, the key is to choose something that suits your personality and makes you feel confident. Keep in mind the dress code for each ceremony, choose a fabric that is comfortable and suitable for the weather, and choose accessories that complement your outfit. With these tips in mind, you are sure to look your best on your big day.
