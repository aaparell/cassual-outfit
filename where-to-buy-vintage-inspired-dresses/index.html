Where To Buy Vintage Inspired Dresses

Written by Christine Sep 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a lover of vintage fashion, you probably know how difficult it can be to find the perfect vintage inspired dress. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out where to start. Not to mention, some vintage shops can be expensive, making it hard to find affordable pieces.

Luckily, there are many online shops that specialize in vintage inspired dresses, making it easy to find the perfect dress for any occasion.

If you're looking for a wide variety of vintage inspired dresses, ModCloth is a great place to start. They have a huge selection of dresses in vintage styles, ranging from the 1920s to the 1970s. Another great option is Unique Vintage, which offers vintage inspired dresses in a range of sizes.

In addition to these online shops, Etsy is also a great resource for vintage inspired dresses. Many independent sellers on Etsy specialize in vintage clothing, and you can find unique, one-of-a-kind pieces that you won't find anywhere else.

ModCloth

ModCloth has been a go-to destination for vintage inspired clothing for over a decade. They offer a wide range of dresses, from casual everyday wear to formal occasion dresses. They also have a range of sizes, making it easy to find something that fits your body type.

I recently purchased a vintage inspired dress from ModCloth for a wedding I attended, and I was thrilled with the quality of the dress. It fit perfectly and looked even better in person than it did online.

Unique Vintage

Unique Vintage is another great option for vintage inspired dresses. They offer a wide variety of styles, from swing dresses to pencil dresses, and everything in between. They also have a range of sizes, so you can find something that fits your body type.

I recently purchased a vintage inspired swing dress from Unique Vintage for a summer picnic, and it was the perfect dress for the occasion. It was comfortable to wear and looked great in photos.

Etsy

Etsy is a great resource for vintage inspired dresses, especially if you're looking for something unique and one-of-a-kind. Many independent sellers on Etsy specialize in vintage clothing, and you can find dresses in a range of styles and sizes.

I recently purchased a vintage inspired dress from an independent seller on Etsy for a themed party, and I received so many compliments on the dress. It was a unique piece that I knew no one else would be wearing, and it was affordable compared to some of the other options I had looked at.

Tips for Buying Vintage Inspired Dresses Online

When shopping for vintage inspired dresses online, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, make sure to read the sizing chart carefully, as vintage sizing can be different than modern sizing. Second, check the return policy before making a purchase, in case the dress doesn't fit or isn't what you expected. And finally, don't be afraid to ask the seller questions about the dress before making a purchase.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Vintage Inspired Dresses

There are many great options for buying vintage inspired dresses online, from ModCloth to Unique Vintage to independent sellers on Etsy. Whether you're looking for something casual or formal, there is a vintage inspired dress out there for everyone. Just make sure to do your research and read reviews before making a purchase, to ensure you're getting a quality dress that fits well and is worth the investment.
