The Ultimate Guide To Retro Style Dress Online Shopping

Written by Eveline Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you love the vintage look of retro style dresses but don't know where to start your online shopping? Are you tired of scrolling through endless pages of modern dresses that just don't have that old-school charm? Look no further, because this guide is here to help you find the perfect retro style dress online!

Shopping for retro style dresses online can be overwhelming, especially if you're not sure what to look for. You might find yourself sifting through pages of irrelevant options or struggling to find the right size and fit. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

The target of retro style dress online shopping is to find stylish and unique dresses that are inspired by the past. These dresses often feature vintage-inspired prints, cuts, and details that give them a nostalgic and timeless appeal. They can be worn for a variety of occasions, from casual outings to formal events, and are perfect for anyone who wants to add a touch of retro glamour to their wardrobe.

In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about retro style dress online shopping, from where to find the best deals to how to choose the perfect dress for your body type. We'll also include tips and tricks for styling your retro dress and making it your own.

Finding Your Perfect Retro Dress

When it comes to retro style dress online shopping, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, it's important to know your body type and what styles flatter your figure. Retro dresses come in a variety of cuts and silhouettes, from fit-and-flare to maxi and everything in between.

Personally, I've had the best luck with fit-and-flare styles that cinch in at the waist and flare out at the hips. This style is flattering on a variety of body types and can help create an hourglass shape. I also love dresses with unique details like lace or embroidery, which add a touch of vintage charm.

Where to Shop for Retro Dresses

There are a ton of online retailers that specialize in retro style dresses, so it can be hard to know where to start. Some of my favorite places to shop for retro dresses include ModCloth, Unique Vintage, and Hell Bunny. These retailers offer a wide variety of styles and sizes, so you're sure to find something that fits your taste and budget.

Another great option is to shop on Etsy, where you can find a huge selection of vintage and vintage-inspired dresses from independent sellers. This is a great way to support small businesses and find one-of-a-kind pieces that you won't see anywhere else.

Choosing the Right Size

One of the biggest challenges of retro style dress online shopping is finding the right size. Many vintage-inspired dresses are made with different sizing standards than modern clothing, so it's important to check the sizing chart before making a purchase.

Most retailers will have a sizing chart that you can use to determine your size based on your measurements. It's important to take accurate measurements of your bust, waist, and hips to ensure a good fit. If you're between sizes, it's usually best to size up, as retro dresses tend to have less stretch than modern clothing.

Styling Your Retro Dress

Once you've found your perfect retro style dress online, it's time to accessorize and make it your own. Retro dresses look great with a variety of accessories, from vintage-inspired jewelry to modern statement pieces.

When it comes to shoes, I love pairing my retro dresses with vintage-style heels or flats. A colorful cardigan or cropped jacket can also add a touch of retro flair to your outfit.

Conclusion of Retro Style Dress Online Shopping

Retro style dress online shopping can be a fun and rewarding experience, as long as you know what to look for and where to shop. By following these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect retro dress for your style and body type.

Question and Answer

Q: Can retro style dresses be worn for formal events?

A: Absolutely! Retro style dresses come in a variety of styles and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Q: Are retro style dresses only for vintage enthusiasts?

A: Not at all! Retro style dresses are perfect for anyone who wants to add a touch of timeless glamour to their wardrobe.

Q: What are some common retro style dress fabrics?

A: Retro style dresses often feature lightweight fabrics like cotton, rayon, and chiffon. They may also feature heavier fabrics like wool or tweed for colder weather.

Q: How can I care for my retro style dress?

A: It's important to follow the care instructions on your dress's label, but in general, most retro style dresses can be hand washed or dry cleaned. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, and hang or lay flat to dry.
