Discovering The Best South Indian Meals In Chennai

Written by Luffy Sep 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a food lover looking for the best South Indian meals in Chennai? Do you crave for authentic South Indian cuisine that will tantalize your taste buds? If yes, then keep reading!

Chennai is known for its rich cultural heritage and delicious cuisine. From crispy dosas to spicy sambar, the city has something to offer for everyone. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best places to enjoy South Indian meals in Chennai.

So, to help you out, we have put together a list of the best South Indian meals in Chennai. Whether you are a local or a tourist, this guide will be your ultimate food companion.

Idli, Vada, and Sambar at Murugan Idli Shop:

When it comes to South Indian meals, idli, vada, and sambar are a classic combination that everyone loves. And there is no better place to enjoy this delicious meal than at Murugan Idli Shop. This iconic eatery is known for its fluffy idlis, crispy vadas, and flavorful sambar. The best part? It’s easy on the pocket too!

Filter Coffee and Masala Dosa at Sangeetha Restaurant:

For those who love filter coffee and masala dosa, Sangeetha Restaurant is a must-visit. The restaurant has been serving authentic South Indian food for over three decades and is known for its crispy dosas and aromatic coffee. The restaurant also offers a wide range of South Indian delicacies that will leave you wanting more.

Chettinad Chicken at Anjappar:

If you are a non-vegetarian, then Chettinad chicken is a must-try dish. And there is no better place to enjoy this spicy delicacy than at Anjappar. The restaurant is known for its lip-smacking Chettinad chicken and biryani. The ambiance is also great, making it a perfect place to enjoy a meal with your family or friends.

How to order:

You can either visit the restaurant or order online through their website or food delivery apps like Swiggy and Zomato.

Pongal and Kesari at Saravana Bhavan:

If you want to experience the best Pongal and Kesari in Chennai, then head to Saravana Bhavan. The restaurant chain has become synonymous with South Indian food and is known for its authentic taste. Their Pongal and Kesari are a must-try and will leave you craving for more.

Question and Answer:

Q: What are the must-try South Indian meals in Chennai?

A: Some of the must-try South Indian meals in Chennai are idli, vada, sambar, masala dosa, Chettinad chicken, Pongal, and Kesari.

Q: Where can I find the best South Indian meals in Chennai?

A: Some of the best places to enjoy South Indian meals in Chennai are Murugan Idli Shop, Sangeetha Restaurant, Anjappar, and Saravana Bhavan.

Q: Can I order food online from these restaurants?

A: Yes, you can order food online from these restaurants through their website or food delivery apps like Swiggy and Zomato.

Q: Are these restaurants budget-friendly?

A: Yes, all these restaurants are budget-friendly and offer great value for money.

Conclusion of Best South Indian Meals in Chennai:

Chennai is a food lover’s paradise, and these restaurants are a testament to that fact. From crispy dosas to spicy Chettinad chicken, the city has something to offer for everyone. So, the next time you are in Chennai, make sure to visit these restaurants and enjoy the best South Indian meals in the city.
