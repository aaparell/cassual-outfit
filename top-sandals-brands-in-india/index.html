Bata

One of the most well-known and trusted brands in India, Bata has been a household name for over 80 years. With a wide range of sandals available for men, women, and children, Bata offers something for everyone. The brand is known for its comfortable and durable footwear, making it a go-to choice for daily wear.

Personally, I have been a fan of Bata sandals for years. Their styles are classic and versatile, and the quality is unbeatable for the price. Whether you're looking for a simple pair of flip-flops or something more stylish, Bata has you covered.

Hush Puppies

Another popular brand in India, Hush Puppies is known for its comfortable and stylish footwear. While the brand is perhaps best known for its dress shoes, they also offer a range of sandals that are perfect for everyday wear.

Personally, I have owned a few pairs of Hush Puppies sandals over the years, and they have always been comfortable and durable. The brand is a bit pricier than some others on this list, but the quality is worth the investment.

Paragon

For those on a budget, Paragon is a great option. The brand offers a wide range of affordable sandals that are perfect for daily wear. While the quality may not be as high as some of the other brands on this list, you can't beat the price.

Personally, I have owned a few pairs of Paragon sandals over the years, and while they may not last as long as some other brands, they are comfortable and affordable.

Clarks

Finally, for those who want to invest in a high-quality pair of sandals, Clarks is a great choice. The brand is known for its comfortable and stylish footwear, and their sandals are no exception. While they may be a bit pricier than some other brands on this list, the quality is unbeatable.

Personally, I have never owned a pair of Clarks sandals, but I have heard great things about them from friends and family. If you're looking for a pair of sandals that will last for years and provide both comfort and style, Clarks is definitely worth considering.
