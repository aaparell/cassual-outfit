The Best Men s Extra Wide Shoes The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of squeezing your feet into narrow shoes? Do you suffer from bunions, corns, or other foot conditions that make it difficult to find comfortable footwear? If so, you're not alone. Many men struggle to find shoes that fit properly, especially if they have wide feet or other foot issues. Fortunately, there are plenty of options available for men's extra wide shoes that can provide the comfort and support you need.

When it comes to finding the best men's extra wide shoes, it's important to consider your specific needs. Whether you're looking for dress shoes, athletic shoes, or casual shoes, there are plenty of options available. Some of the most popular brands for men's extra wide shoes include New Balance, Skechers, and Rockport, but there are plenty of other great brands to consider as well.

In this guide, we'll explore some of the best men's extra wide shoes on the market, as well as some tips for choosing the right shoes for your needs. We'll also cover some common foot conditions and how to find shoes that can help alleviate discomfort.

New Balance Men's MW577 Walking Shoe

As one of the most popular brands for athletic shoes, it's no surprise that New Balance offers some great options for men's extra wide shoes. The MW577 walking shoe is a great choice for anyone looking for a comfortable, supportive shoe for everyday wear. With a hook-and-loop closure and ABZORB cushioning in the heel, this shoe offers a snug fit and great shock absorption. It's also available in a range of widths, including extra wide.

Skechers Men's Equalizer Double Play Slip-On Loafer

If you're looking for a casual shoe that's easy to slip on and off, the Skechers Equalizer Double Play loafer is a great choice. This shoe features a memory foam insole and a flexible outsole for comfort and support. It's also available in a wide range of sizes, including extra wide. The slip-on design makes it easy to wear with any outfit, and the stylish design is sure to turn heads.

Rockport Men's Essential Details Waterproof Wingtip Oxford

For those who need dress shoes for work or formal events, the Rockport Essential Details oxford is a great choice. This shoe features a waterproof leather upper and a breathable lining to keep your feet dry and comfortable. The wingtip design adds a touch of elegance, while the EVA footbed provides cushioning and support. This shoe is available in a range of widths, including extra wide.

How to Choose the Right Men's Extra Wide Shoes

When shopping for men's extra wide shoes, it's important to consider a few key factors. First, make sure to measure your feet to ensure you're choosing the right size. You can also look for shoes that offer a range of widths, including extra wide. Consider the type of shoe you need, whether it's for athletics, work, or casual wear. Finally, look for shoes that offer the features you need, such as cushioning, support, and breathability.

Conclusion of Best Men's Extra Wide Shoes

With so many great options available, there's no reason to settle for uncomfortable shoes. Whether you need dress shoes, athletic shoes, or casual shoes, there are plenty of men's extra wide shoes that can provide the comfort and support you need. By considering your specific needs and shopping around for the right shoes, you can find the perfect pair that will keep your feet happy and healthy.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some common foot conditions that can make it difficult to find comfortable shoes?

A: Some common foot conditions include bunions, corns, plantar fasciitis, and flat feet. These conditions can make it difficult to find shoes that fit properly and provide the support you need.

Q: Are there any specific features I should look for in men's extra wide shoes?

A: Some features to consider include cushioning, support, breathability, and adjustable closures. Look for shoes that offer these features to ensure you get the comfort and support you need.

Q: Can I find men's extra wide shoes in stylish designs?

A: Yes! Many brands offer men's extra wide shoes in a range of stylish designs, from dress shoes to casual sneakers. You don't have to sacrifice style for comfort.

Q: Where can I find the best deals on men's extra wide shoes?

A: Many retailers offer sales and discounts on men's extra wide shoes throughout the year. Check online retailers like Amazon, Zappos, and Nordstrom, as well as brick-and-mortar stores like DSW and Foot Locker.
