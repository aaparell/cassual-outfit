Ranking Live Action Spider Man Suits

Written by Aprilia Nov 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a Spider Man fan who has always been curious about how the different live-action Spider Man suits compare to one another? Look no further, because we have ranked them all for you!

While Spider Man has been portrayed in many different ways throughout the years, the live-action versions have always been a fan favorite. However, not all suits are created equal, and some have been received more favorably than others. This can be frustrating for fans who want to see their favorite superhero portrayed in the best way possible.

The purpose of this article is to provide a comprehensive ranking of all the different live-action Spider Man suits, from worst to best. We will take into account factors such as design, functionality, and overall appeal to the audience.

In conclusion, this article will provide Spider Man fans with a definitive ranking of all the different live-action Spider Man suits, so that they can finally settle the age-old debate of which suit is truly the best. Let's dive in!

The Amazing Spider Man (2012) Suit

The Amazing Spider Man suit, worn by Andrew Garfield in the 2012 film of the same name, ranks at the bottom of our list. While the suit was sleek and modern, it lacked the classic red and blue colors that fans have come to associate with Spider Man. Additionally, the suit lacked the webbing design that is a hallmark of Spider Man's suit, making it feel incomplete and unfinished.

Spider Man 2099 Suit

The Spider Man 2099 suit, worn by Miguel O'Hara in the 2017 animated series of the same name, ranks slightly higher on our list. The suit has a unique design that sets it apart from the classic Spider Man suit, with a futuristic look that suits the 2099 setting. However, the suit lacks the webbing design that is a hallmark of Spider Man's suit, making it feel incomplete and unfinished.

The Classic Spider Man Suit

The classic Spider Man suit, worn by Tobey Maguire in the original Spider Man trilogy, is a fan favorite and ranks highly on our list. The suit is faithful to the original comic book design, with the classic red and blue color scheme and webbing design. Additionally, the suit was functional, with the ability to shoot webs from the wrists and a mask that allowed for easy movement and visibility.

The MCU Spider Man Suit

The MCU Spider Man suit, worn by Tom Holland in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, tops our list as the best live-action Spider Man suit. The suit is a modern take on the classic design, with a sleek and functional design that includes many new features such as instant kill mode and a built-in AI assistant. Additionally, the suit retains the classic red and blue colors and webbing design, making it a perfect blend of old and new.

The Black Suit (Spider Man 3)

The black suit worn by Tobey Maguire in Spider Man 3 is a unique entry on our list. While not a traditional Spider Man suit, the black suit featured a sleek design and gave Spider Man new abilities such as increased strength and agility. However, the suit's negative influence on Peter Parker's personality and behavior ultimately led to its downfall.

Question and Answer

Q: Why is the Amazing Spider Man suit ranked so low?

A: The Amazing Spider Man suit lacked the classic red and blue colors and webbing design that fans have come to associate with Spider Man. Additionally, the suit lacked a finished and polished look, making it feel incomplete.

Q: What sets the classic Spider Man suit apart from the others?

A: The classic Spider Man suit is faithful to the original comic book design, with the classic red and blue color scheme and webbing design. Additionally, the suit was functional, with the ability to shoot webs from the wrists and a mask that allowed for easy movement and visibility.

Q: Why is the MCU Spider Man suit ranked the highest?

A: The MCU Spider Man suit is a perfect blend of old and new, with a sleek and functional design that includes many new features such as instant kill mode and a built-in AI assistant. Additionally, the suit retains the classic red and blue colors and webbing design, making it a fan favorite.

Q: What is unique about the Black Suit in Spider Man 3?

A: The Black Suit in Spider Man 3 featured a sleek design and gave Spider Man new abilities such as increased strength and agility. However, the suit's negative influence on Peter Parker's personality and behavior ultimately led to its downfall.

Conclusion of Live Action Spider Man Suits Ranked

After careful consideration, we have ranked all the different live-action Spider Man suits from worst to best. While each suit has its own unique qualities, the MCU Spider Man suit stands out as the best overall, with a perfect blend of old and new that has won over fans. Whether you agree with our rankings or not, one thing is for sure - Spider Man will continue to be a beloved superhero for years to come.
