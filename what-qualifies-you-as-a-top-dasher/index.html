How to Qualify as a Top Dasher and Maximize Your Earnings

As a delivery driver for DoorDash, you may have heard about the Top Dasher program and wondered what it takes to qualify. Whether you're looking to increase your earnings or simply take on more orders, becoming a Top Dasher can open up new opportunities and benefits. However, the requirements and qualifications can be confusing and difficult to navigate on your own.

To qualify as a Top Dasher, you must meet certain criteria, such as completing a certain number of deliveries, maintaining a high customer rating, and having a low completion rate. These requirements can vary by market and change from month to month, making it challenging to stay on top of them. Additionally, you must schedule your shifts in advance and be available during busy times to be eligible.

If you do qualify as a Top Dasher, you can benefit from perks such as priority access to orders, the ability to Dash Now at any time, and higher earnings per order. However, these benefits also come with new expectations, such as maintaining your high rating and completing a certain number of deliveries each month.

My Experience as a Top Dasher

When I first started delivering for DoorDash, I didn't think much about the Top Dasher program. However, as I became more experienced and started to understand the requirements, I began to aim for the status. It took some time and effort, but eventually, I was able to meet the qualifications and become a Top Dasher.

One of the biggest benefits of being a Top Dasher was the ability to Dash Now whenever I wanted, without needing to schedule shifts in advance. This allowed me to be more flexible with my schedule and take advantage of busy times when there were more orders available. I also noticed that I received more higher-paying orders and had a higher earnings per order than before.

Tips for Qualifying as a Top Dasher

If you're interested in becoming a Top Dasher, there are a few strategies you can use to increase your chances:

1. Focus on Customer Service

Your customer rating is one of the most important factors in qualifying as a Top Dasher. Make sure to communicate with customers, deliver orders promptly, and resolve any issues that may arise. Providing excellent customer service can not only improve your rating but also lead to higher tips and more repeat customers.

2. Complete Orders Efficiently

Your completion rate measures how many orders you accept and complete, versus how many you decline or cancel. To maintain a high completion rate, it's important to only accept orders that you know you can complete within the allotted time and distance. If you do need to cancel an order, make sure to do so as soon as possible and provide a valid reason.

Is Top Dasher Status Worth It?

While qualifying as a Top Dasher can offer some benefits, it may not be the right choice for everyone. If you're already earning a steady income and don't have the time or availability to meet the requirements, it may not be worth the effort. Additionally, the program is not available in all markets and may be more competitive in some areas than others.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I qualify as a Top Dasher if I only deliver part-time?

A: Yes, you can still qualify as a Top Dasher even if you only deliver a few hours a week. However, you will need to meet the same requirements as full-time drivers, such as completing a certain number of deliveries and maintaining a high rating.

Q: How often do the requirements for Top Dasher change?

A: The requirements for Top Dasher can change from month to month and may vary by market. It's important to stay up to date on the latest requirements and check your eligibility regularly.

Q: Can I lose my Top Dasher status if I don't meet the requirements one month?

A: Yes, if you don't meet the requirements for Top Dasher for a given month, you may lose your status and the benefits that come with it. However, you can regain your status by meeting the requirements again in a future month.

Q: Can I still choose my orders as a Top Dasher?

A: Yes, even as a Top Dasher, you can still choose which orders to accept or decline. However, you may receive more priority and higher-paying orders than non-Top Dashers.

Conclusion of What Qualifies You as a Top Dasher

Becoming a Top Dasher can be a valuable opportunity for DoorDash drivers looking to maximize their earnings and flexibility. By meeting the requirements and maintaining a high level of customer service and completion rate, you can qualify for benefits such as priority access to orders and higher earnings per order. However, it's important to weigh the costs and benefits and determine if this program is the right fit for your needs and availability.
