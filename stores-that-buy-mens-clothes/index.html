Where To Sell Your Men s Clothes And Get Paid

Written by Oliver Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Selling your old clothes can be a hassle, especially when it comes to men's clothing. It's not always easy to find stores that buy men's clothes, and even when you do, they may not offer a fair price. But don't worry, this tutorial blog post will guide you through the process of finding stores that buy men's clothes and getting the most for your items.

If you're looking to sell your men's clothes, you're not alone. Many people have clothes that they no longer wear or need, and selling them can be a great way to make some extra cash. However, the process of finding stores that buy men's clothes can be frustrating, especially if you're not sure where to start.

The first step in finding stores that buy men's clothes is to do some research. Look for stores in your area that specialize in men's clothing, such as consignment shops or second-hand stores. You can also check online marketplaces like eBay or Poshmark, which allow you to sell your items directly to buyers.

To get the most for your men's clothes, it's important to choose the right stores to sell to. Look for stores that specialize in the type of clothing you're selling, such as vintage or designer clothing. These stores are more likely to offer a fair price for your items, as they know the value of the clothing.

Example Store 1: Buffalo Exchange

One store that buys men's clothes is Buffalo Exchange. They specialize in vintage and designer clothing, and offer cash or trade for your items. I've personally sold clothes to Buffalo Exchange before and have always been happy with the price they offered.

When selling to Buffalo Exchange, it's important to bring in clean, gently-used clothing that is in good condition. They also have a list of brands that they are currently buying, so be sure to check their website before heading in.

Example Store 2: Plato's Closet

Another store that buys men's clothes is Plato's Closet. They specialize in trendy and stylish clothing for teens and young adults, but also buy men's clothing. When selling to Plato's Closet, it's important to bring in clothing that is in good condition and up to date with current trends.

Plato's Closet offers cash on the spot for your items, and their prices are generally fair. I've sold clothing to Plato's Closet before and have always been happy with the price they offered.

Online Marketplaces

If you're not interested in selling to a physical store, there are also online marketplaces that allow you to sell your men's clothing directly to buyers. eBay and Poshmark are two popular options that offer a wide range of clothing styles and sizes.

When selling on these platforms, it's important to take good photos of your items and write clear descriptions. This will help your items stand out and attract potential buyers.

Tips for Selling Your Men's Clothes

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selling your men's clothes:

  1. Make sure your clothing is clean and in good condition
  2. Research the stores or online marketplaces you're selling to
  3. Take good photos and write clear descriptions
  4. Don't be afraid to negotiate for a fair price

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a store is interested in buying my clothing?

A: Most stores will have a list of brands or styles that they are currently buying. Check their website or call ahead to ask.

Q: How do I determine the value of my clothing?

A: You can use online resources like eBay or Poshmark to see what similar items are selling for. You can also check with a consignment store to see what they would offer for your items.

Q: Can I sell clothing that is no longer in style?

A: It depends on the store or online marketplace. Some may only buy current styles, while others may be interested in vintage or unique items.

Q: What should I do with clothing that is not in good condition?

A: Consider donating it to a local charity or clothing drive.

Conclusion of Stores that Buy Mens Clothes

Selling your men's clothes can be a great way to make some extra cash, but it's important to choose the right stores or online marketplaces to sell to. Do your research, take good photos, and don't be afraid to negotiate for a fair price. With these tips, you'll be able to sell your men's clothes and get paid.
