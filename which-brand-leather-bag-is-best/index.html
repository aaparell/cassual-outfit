How to Care for Your Leather Bag

Caring for your leather bag is essential if you want it to last for years. Firstly, avoid exposing your bag to direct sunlight or heat, as this can cause the leather to dry out and crack. Secondly, avoid getting your bag wet, as this can also damage the leather. If your bag does get wet, allow it to dry naturally and avoid using a hairdryer. Lastly, store your bag in a cool, dry place, and avoid storing it in a plastic bag or container, as this can cause the leather to become discolored.
