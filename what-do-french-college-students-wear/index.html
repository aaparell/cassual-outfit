What Do French College Students Wear

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you planning to study abroad in France and wondering what the college students wear? Or maybe you're just curious about the fashion culture in France. Whatever your reason may be, understanding the fashion choices of French college students can be challenging. In this blog post, we'll explore what French college students wear, including popular trends and styles.

When it comes to fashion, France is known for its chic and sophisticated style. However, French college students tend to dress more casually and comfortably. They prefer a relaxed and effortless look, with a mix of designer and high-street brands. French students often opt for classic pieces like jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers, but with a French twist.

In summary, French college students typically wear casual and comfortable outfits. They mix high-end designer brands with high-street fashion for a relaxed yet stylish look.

Classic French Style

I remember my first day of classes in France. I wore a dress with heels and was surprised to see that most of the students were dressed casually. I learned that the key to French style is simplicity. French students keep their outfits simple and effortless, with classic pieces that never go out of style. They often wear jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers, but with a touch of French elegance. The key is to keep it simple and chic.

Accessorize with Scarves

Another trend that I noticed among French college students is their love for scarves. Scarves are a versatile accessory that can add a touch of elegance to any outfit. French students often wear scarves with their outfits, whether it's a casual t-shirt and jeans or a more formal dress. They also wear scarves in various ways, such as draped around their neck or tied in a knot.

French Brands

French college students often wear designer brands such as Chanel, Dior, and Louis Vuitton. However, they also love high-street brands like Zara, Mango, and H&M. Mixing high-end and high-street fashion is a common trend among French students. They also prefer sustainable and ethical fashion brands, which aligns with their values and love for the environment.

How to Achieve French Style

If you want to achieve French style, keep it simple and classic. Opt for quality pieces that never go out of style, such as a leather jacket, white t-shirt, or a pair of jeans. Accessorize with a scarf or a statement piece of jewelry. And don't be afraid to mix high-end and high-street fashion.

FAQs

Q: Do French college students wear a lot of makeup?

A: No, French college students prefer a natural and effortless look. They often wear minimal makeup and focus on skincare instead.

Q: What kind of shoes do French college students wear?

A: French college students often wear sneakers or comfortable flats. They prioritize comfort over style, but still manage to look chic.

Q: Do French college students wear bright colors?

A: French college students prefer neutral and muted colors, such as black, white, beige, and navy. However, they sometimes add a pop of color to their outfits with a scarf or a statement accessory.

Q: Is it appropriate to wear revealing clothing in France?

A: Revealing clothing is not common among French college students. They prefer a more modest and elegant look.

Conclusion

In conclusion, French college students prefer a casual and comfortable style, with a touch of French elegance. They mix high-end and high-street fashion for a relaxed yet stylish look. The key to French style is simplicity and quality pieces that never go out of style.
