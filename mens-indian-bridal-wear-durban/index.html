Breaking Down the Elements of Mens Indian Bridal Wear Durban

Mens Indian bridal wear Durban typically consists of a sherwani or a kurta, churidar, and a dupatta. Let's break down each element:

Sherwani: A sherwani is a long coat-like jacket that is worn over a kurta and churidar. It's typically made from silk, velvet, or brocade and is adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments.

Kurta: A kurta is a loose-fitting shirt that is typically made from cotton or silk. It's comfortable to wear and comes in a variety of colors and designs.

Churidar: A churidar is a fitted trouser that is typically made from cotton or silk. It's perfect for grooms who want to show off their footwear.

Dupatta: A dupatta is a long scarf that is draped over the sherwani. It's typically made from silk or chiffon and is adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments.
